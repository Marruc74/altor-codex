# Kandra — Entity Catalogue

*Source: the Kandra city sourcebook (Ereb Altor Vol. I, Äventyrsspel 1986/88), drawn from `Kandra_OCR.md`.*
*Each entry is tagged with the module's own section where it appears. Everyday goods, generic trades, and ordinary weapons are omitted. The sourcebook's misprint "Artema" is the canonical "Atrema"; both are noted where they occur.*

## Deities (de Unga Gudarna)

*The "Young Gods" who overthrew the evil "Old Gods" (de Gamla Gudarna). Listed because their temples and priests are named throughout.*

| Name | Domain | Section(s) |
|---|---|---|

| Sankma | Wind, sky, atmosphere; god of all birds; has no temple (worshipped on heights) | Religiöst / De Unga Gudarna |
| Visha | Agriculture and rain | Religiöst / De Unga Gudarna |
| Ninnave | Fertility and love; Visha's consort; protects the home against fire | Religiöst / De Unga Gudarna |
| Kushta | Death; guardian of the realm of the dead; opposes the "undead" state | Religiöst / De Unga Gudarna |
