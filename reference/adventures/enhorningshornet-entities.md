## The Two Orders & Their Grades

*Both orders teach magic (entry requires passing a magician-academy admission test) and admit men and women, but cap advancement by sex. Grades are listed lowest to highest.*

### Delithaorden (the light order)
*Founded 227 e.O.; animists/mentalists; only women advance past Invigd. Robe-bands mark the grade; AVSLÖJA SVARTPRÄSTER is learned at the Prästessa grade. Four temples in Berendien, one in Felicien, none in Kandra.*

| Grade | Band colour | Notes | Section(s) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Novis | Gul (yellow) | Entry grade; basic Animism and healing | SLP; Tempelordnarna |
| Invigd | Blå (blue) | Initiate; the highest grade a man may reach | SLP; Tempelordnarna |
| Livgiverska | Grön (green) | "Life-giver"; can channel HELA to others with the unicorn horn | SLP; Tempelordnarna |
| Prästessa | Röd (red) | Full priestess; learns AVSLÖJA SVARTPRÄSTER | SLP; Tempelordnarna |
| Helgad | Purpur (purple) | "Sanctified"; the order's highest grade (e.g. Den helgade) | SLP; Tempelordnarna |

### Den vänstra vägens prästerskap (the Zeeri / black order)
*Necromancers/elementalists; only men advance past Skuggpräst. Dress marks the grade (black cloak; silver nose-ring from Invigd; black-steel brow-band from Skuggpräst; a shaved part from Mörkerpräst, painted blood-red for Överstepräst; a tattooed blood-drop and gold neck-ring for the Fullkomnad). Eight temples in Berendien, none in Kandra. Their staves and the black knights' broadswords do double damage to elf-folk.*

| Rank | Notes | Section(s) |
|---|---|---|
| Novis | 1st rank; black cloak, no other marks | Hus 247; SLP; Tempelordnarna |
| Invigd | 2nd rank; adds the left-nostril silver ring | Hus 247; SLP; Tempelordnarna |
| Skuggpräst(inna) | 3rd rank; adds the black-steel brow-band; highest grade a woman may reach (e.g. Gallahan, Moira is one rank below at Invigd) | Hus 247; SLP; Tempelordnarna |
| Mörkerpräst | 4th rank; adds the shaved part through the hair | Resan; Hus 247; SLP; Tempelordnarna |
| Överstepräst | 5th rank; the part is painted blood-red; keeps a familiaris | Fortsatta handlingsmöjligheter; Hus 247; SLP; Tempelordnarna |
| Fullkomnad | 6th rank; tattooed blood-drop on the left palm and a thick gold neck-ring | Tempelordnarna |
| Svartriddare ("Den vänstra handen") | The order's black knights; white shields (landless), shaved heads; enchanted broadswords | Resan; Hus 247; SLP |
| Väpnare | Squires to the black knights; same gear but may not shave the head until knighted | Hus 247; SLP |
| Orcher slaves | Held in the temples to serve the priesthood | Inledning; Tempelordnarna |
