# Dåligt vatten (Bad Water) - Sinkadus 20

Source: *Sinkadus* nr 20 (August 1989), pp. 37-40. An adventure for *Drakar och
Demoner Expert* by Martin Rundkvist. Illustrations by Håkan Ackegård (the
crater sketch and the climbing figure of the Stormfågel; the full-page
"Augustibilden" of the druid-girl Felindia and the bird Quirk on p. 36 is the
month's centrefold and not part of the adventure).

Working-notes summary for the codex. The compendium page (`bad-water.md`) keeps
the lore only; the game numbers below are recorded here for reference.

## Premise

The remote coastal fishing village of **Springvatten** lies between the sea and
a desolate range, somewhere on **Zorakin's** coast. Its spring rises from the
basalt at the village edge; the water begins in the crater lake atop
**Grissleberget**, an old volcano (~300 m) on whose shoulder the village sits,
and runs down through the mountain's lava tunnels. A month ago the water turned
foul and villagers and livestock began to sicken.

Cause: a passing **roc** ("fågel rock"; the villagers call it *pälsörn*,
"fur-eagle") dropped a dead whale into the crater lake. Ten tons of rotting
whale have poisoned the water at its source. The village shaman, the
**Stormfågel**, can purify the spring but not while the carcass lies in it, so
he intends to summon another roc to lift it out, a rite ("ett bjudande", an
offering/inviting) that leaves him in trance most of a day and defenceless. He
hires outsiders to guard him. Fee: ~60 copper (paid out as 63).

## Structure

- **Inledning / Geografisk bakgrund** - author's note (no mapped dungeon, story-
  driven, for 3-5 mid-strength characters); the village and volcano.
- **Äventyret börjar** - hook at the tavern/hiring-hall **Slitna Stövlar** (Worn
  Boots), suggested city Pendon. Two villagers, **Bedvir** and **Kral**, hire the
  party after being mocked off the podium.
- **Resan** - a long day's walk over the mountains with a bad-tempered pack-
  donkey; arrival at Springvatten at sunset; the stone well-house reeking of rot.
- **Mottagandet / Berättelsen i bystugan** - the villagers (lean, sealskin-clad,
  long-haired, sparse beards); the Stormfågel introduces himself and explains the
  task over fish soup in the village hall.
- **SL:s information** - the whale/roc backstory; the villagers' dead are buried
  on the crater ledges; the Stormfågel's nature.
- **Springvatten** - village of ~20 houses, ~150 people; fishing, sealing,
  mussel-ropes, egg-robbing the seabird cliffs; guano bank feeds thin farming.
  The old say there has always been a Stormfågel: when one dies, another is born.
- **Till toppen** - breakfast (cold fish soup, goat milk, carrots, speckled
  "grissle" eggs); boat round the base with Kral to a sandy cleft; climb to a
  crack; through the lava-tunnel labyrinth (dwarves risk getting lost).
- **Kratern** - out onto a ledge in the ~150 m sheer crater; grey lake with the
  torn whale; burial urns wedged in the crevices; the griffin's nest on a ledge.
- **Dagen i kratern** - the Stormfågel drums himself into trance (~11:00);
  crater water sickens anyone who drinks it; waking him triggers nightmares. The
  griffin's nest holds the treasure (below). The griffin lands ~12:00, hungry and
  angry, and attacks.
- The **harpies**: 6 arrive ~16:30 to taunt and snatch, then leave to fetch the
  rest; ~40 more return after dark and rain thrown spears. Fight until the party
  is nearly overwhelmed, then the rite completes: drum stops, storm-wind rises,
  the roc darkens the crater, lifts the whale out and is gone, leaving a metre-
  long matte-black feather (shaft as thick as an arm). The Stormfågel scatters a
  powder on the lake, heals the party (Läkeförmåga), and heaves a dead harpy over
  the edge.
- **Nervägen och avslutningen** - climb back down; Kral's boat; the water runs
  clean; feast with lobster; Bedvir pays 63 copper; the Stormfågel's extra reward
  is a wordless gull-song that means "a cry for food" (sing it and a gull brings
  you a fish), to be taught sparingly. Party is welcome back in future.
- **Kommentar** - keep the roc a secret from players; let the Stormfågel keep his
  mystery (even the GM is told little about him).

## Stat blocks (DoD Expert)

**Stormfågel** - STY 11, FYS 15, STO 13, SMI 15, INT 18, PSY 20, KAR 19, SB -,
KP 14. Hjälteförmågor: Kattfot, Läkeförmåga. Färdigheter: Fiska 18, Klättra 17,
Hoppa 15, Havsöverlevnad 19, Lyssna 16, Sjökunnighet 16, Upptäcka fara 18, Finna
dolda ting 16, Sjunga B4, Tala jori B3, Tala satenu B2. Vapen: Dirk 10.

**Grip (griffin)** - STY 19, STO 14, FYS 13, SMI 16, INT 5, PSY 13, SB +1T4,
KP 14. Attacks: 2 claws 80% 1T6+SB, bite 60% 1T6+SB/2. Skydd: 4. Förflyttning:
F26/L12. Retreats over the crater edge below 5 KP.

**Harpies (42)** - STY 12, STO 12, FYS 12, SMI 17, INT 11, PSY 11, SB -, KP 12.
Attacks: throwing spear 50% 1T6-1 (stone-tipped), 2 claws 30% 1T6. Skydd: 1.
Förflyttning: F14/L4. Each carries 3 spears; will not melee; flees once
decimated by half.

## Treasure (in the griffin's nest)

- Gilded steel cuirass (STO 14, ~1200 sm).
- Enchanted dagger in sheath: FÖRTROLLA VAPEN E1 s15, SIGILL with PERMANENS
  triggered on drawing the blade, drains 1 PSY per use; ~300 sm if the buyer does
  not know its magic.
- Ground crystal lenses in steel-wire frames (spectacles; correct near-sight).
- The roc's shed black feather (a keepsake, not treasure).
