# Efaro

Efaro lies in the northwestern reaches of Soluna, where heat shapes both land and people. To the north stretches the Copper Sea, while the Serpent Lake marks the western edge and the Gizur Mountains rise to the south. It is a land of stone, wind, and hidden wealth, where the earth yields metals and gemstones in great abundance.

The people of Efaro are divided into eight tribes, each proud and watchful of the others. Alliances are brief and often broken, and power shifts as quickly as the desert winds. When one tribe rises above the rest, it does not rule in unity but in advantage. Rivals are weakened, and their most capable members are sold into slavery beyond Efaro's borders.

Yet this constant struggle has not brought ruin. Instead, Efaro endures in a strange balance. Wealth flows from its mines, and its people remain both fierce and creative. They are warriors shaped by conflict, but also makers of art, carrying beauty alongside hardship in a land that demands both strength and expression.

## Overview

- **Government:** Eight competing tribes, no unified ruler
- **Exports:** precious metals, gemstones, slaves

## Geography

Efaro lies in northwestern Soluna, bordering the Copper Sea to the north, the Serpent Lake to the west, and the Gizur Mountains to the south.

## People

The people of Efaro are tall and dark-skinned, with light-colored hair adapted to reflect the heat. They are shaped by constant conflict, yet maintain a strong interest in art and creative expression: warriors and makers alike.

## Society

Efaro is divided into eight competing tribes. Alliances are brief and often broken, and power shifts frequently. When one tribe rises above the rest, rivals are weakened and their most capable members sold into slavery beyond Efaro's borders.

## Economy

Despite constant internal conflict, Efaro maintains relative wealth through rich deposits of precious metals and gemstones. Slaves are traded to Morëlvidyn and Felicien.
