# The Sunstone

![A sunstone from the hills of Beyural](/codex/Geography/Ereb/Countries/Erebos/Sunstone.jpg "A sunstone specimen from the hills of Beyural")

#### Of Its Nature

The sunstone is a yellow gemstone found only in the hills of Beyural. Rough and irregular, it is seldom polished. Its color ranges from pale gold to deep amber, and when touched by light, it seems to hold it rather than reflect it.



#### Of Its Form

Unlike common gems, the sunstone does not sparkle. Light enters it and settles within, giving it a quiet depth. Small veins and inclusions run through its body, like threads caught in still warmth.



#### Of Its Use

Sunstones are worn as simple amulets, set in leather or bronze. Some carry them at the neck, others fix them to tools or weapons. They are traded widely, and their value has long enriched Beyural.



#### On Its Meaning

Though no true properties are proven, many believe the stone offers protection. It is said to guard against illness, misfortune, and unseen forces. Whether this is truth or comfort is not known.



#### Of Quiet Beliefs

There are those who claim the stone feels warm even in shade, or that it dulls when danger is near. Such things are not recorded, only spoken of in passing.



#### In the End

The sunstone does not shine.

It holds light:and perhaps, something more.
