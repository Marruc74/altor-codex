# Montures

Montures lies scattered across the warm waters of the Golwynda Sea, a chain of islands where life follows the rhythm of tides and trade. The high forests of Gogero stand quiet and poor, while Elmor carries the weight of power through the city of Timar, where merchants, nobles, and sailors shape the fate of the duchy. Across the smaller islands, fishing villages cling to the coast, their people bound to the sea that feeds them.

The land is warm and forgiving, with olive trees and low forests covering the hills, yet beyond the fertile ground lie mountains where fewer dare to settle. The history of Montures is marked by ducal rule, shifting alliances, and the constant pressure of pirates and rival powers. Trade flows through its harbors, bringing wealth, ambition, and conflict in equal measure.

In Montures, power is negotiated in quiet halls, fortunes are carried on ships, and every village holds its own struggles beneath the same sun.

## Geography

Montures lies in an island group in the Golwynda Sea.

Three main islands:

### Gogero

Mountainous island  
Dense forests  
Poorer region  
Some farming and hunting

### Elmor

The most important island  
Home to the capital Timar  
Stronger agriculture and trade

Smaller islands  
Small fishing communities  
Focused on seafaring and trade

The sea around the islands is warm and full of currents, making navigation and trade essential.
