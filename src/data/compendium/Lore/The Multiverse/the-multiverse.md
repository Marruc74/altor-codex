# The Multiverse

Altor is but one world among an infinity of worlds. Beyond the sphere of sky and stars that the astronomers chart lie countless other realms, each walled off from the rest, and the whole of them together the learned name the Multiverse. Few mortals ever look upon another world, for the only road between them is magic; but the mages who study such things hold that the shape of all creation was set in a single ancient act, and that Altor's place within it is neither its centre nor its edge.

## Overview

- **What it is:** The infinity of separated worlds that make up the whole of creation
- **Born of:** Chaos, the first and oldest power, dark and formless
- **Ordered by:** The higher powers, who raised the dimension walls
- **The light:** Radiates from an unknown centre; the inner worlds are pure and spiritual, the outer ones gross and material
- **The outer ring:** Demonicum, the frontier worlds that border raw Chaos
- **The only road between worlds:** Magic - a direct portal, or the grey path of the astral plane

## Chaos and the Light

In the beginning was Chaos - evil, dark, and full of terrible winds, the formless first power out of which all else came. From Chaos, in time, was born its opposite: the Light. About the Light the higher powers shaped new worlds and new beings, orderly and good, and to keep the corrupting tide of Chaos from ever reaching the Light they raised the dimension walls, dividing creation into an endless multitude of separated worlds. So was the Multiverse made, a thing of order carved out of, and forever besieged by, Chaos.

## The Inner and Outer Worlds

The Light radiates from a centre no mage has found. Nearest it lie the innermost worlds, pure realms of spirit-beings under the hand of the higher powers; and the farther a world lies from the Light, the more solid and material it grows, and the more Chaos presses upon it. Altor lies somewhere along that descent, a world of flesh and stone where good and ill contend. Farthest of all, at the very brink of Chaos, lies the ring of frontier worlds called Demonicum, a shield against the dark.

## Crossing Between Worlds

No road of land or sea joins one world to another; only magic can cross the dimension walls. A mage of surpassing power may tear open a direct portal from one world to the next, a perilous working that often opens upon the wrong and more dangerous place. Far more common, and only a little safer, is to walk the grey path: to step through a portal into the timeless astral country of the Grey Halls, and from there seek the gate to one's chosen world.
