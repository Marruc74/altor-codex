# Nehcrom

Nehcrom, called the Horned One, is the outermost of the three great Guardians of Demonicum, and the first line of all creation against Chaos. Of the Guardians he is the most openly rebellious: he draws his strength from violence, plunder, death and bloodshed, has thrown off the service of the higher powers that made him, and rules his war-realm as a god in his own right.

## Overview

- **Title:** The Horned One; outermost Guardian of Demonicum
- **Domain:** Violence, plunder, death and war
- **Realm:** Caboon — the black cliff Khla, built into an endless Black Fortress
- **Sworn enemy:** Bemoth, the Prince of Beasts, with whom he has warred for ages
- **Worshipped by:** The dark folk above all; his servants are warriors, assassins and executioners
- **Symbol:** A horned skull, black upon red

## The Horned One

Nehcrom appears as a vast humanoid thrice the height of a man, with sunken eyes and the jaws of a predator, two horns rising from his temples, his red-brown hide scarred and knotted with growths, and over it all black and blood-red armour. From the black rock of Khla, adrift in chaotic space, he has raised a fortress without end, its throne heaped of the crushed skulls of his enemies. At Khla's harbours lies his war-fleet, and in its smithies are forged ensouled weapons and engines of war whose like no mortal armourer could make.

## Wars and Worship

Nehcrom is locked in a war of ages with Bemoth, whose wild realm of Leithicor he once invaded and could not hold. Caliban he counts an enemy too, though the lord of madness has need of the chaos that Nehcrom sows. His hordes march under field-lords and under black priests who wear his horned skull and strike far-reaching pacts in his name. In Altor it is the dark folk who revere him most, taking the mightiest of demons for gods; and those who serve the Horned One are ever the takers of life — the warrior, the assassin, the headsman.
