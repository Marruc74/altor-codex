# Bemoth

Bemoth, the Prince of Beasts, is the second of the three great Guardians of Demonicum, and the only one still faithful to the task for which the Guardians were made: the hunting of Chaos through all the worlds of the Multiverse. His power is over the wild forces of nature and the beasts, and his nature is twofold — endlessly cruel, yet at times strangely mild, and ever true to his word to those he counts allies.

## Overview

- **Title:** Prince of Beasts; the second Guardian of Demonicum
- **Domain:** The wild forces of nature, and the beasts of every world
- **Realm:** Leithicor — untamed wildlands and ancient forests under an eternal moon
- **His seat:** Toroth, a stone altar within a ring of monoliths that roams the worlds with the phases of the moon
- **Sworn enemy:** Nehcrom, whose invasion of Leithicor he shattered
- **Honoured by:** Beast-peoples, shamans and druids; the elves do not count him a direct threat
- **Symbol:** A grinning, stag-horned predator skull, in black and blue

## The Prince of Beasts

Bemoth shows as a vast humanoid that drops to all fours to run, crowned not with the warped growths of other demons but with the branching antlers of a stag, his shaggy-maned body tailed and fanged like a great hunting-beast. He keeps no palace; his throne is the bare stone altar of Toroth, set within a circle of standing stones that wanders from world to world as the moon waxes and wanes. Alone among the Guardians he has stayed loyal to the old charge, and still ranges the Multiverse hunting the spawn of Chaos wherever they break through.

## The War and the Wild

When Nehcrom invaded Leithicor he won many of its worlds, but Bemoth's wild hordes — sweeping out of the sky and up from the earth at once — broke his armies, and Leithicor stands unconquered to this day, the war between them unended. Bemoth keeps no standing host; his hordes gather and scatter like weather, and his cults are few, his servants shunned even by their own kind. Among mortals he is revered by the peoples of the wild, by shamans and by druids, and there are those who whisper that the horned huntsman of the Wild Hunt, who rides down his quarry across the night, is Bemoth himself or one who serves him.
