# Tiamat

Tiamat is less a god than an idea: the chaos out of which the order of the world arose. In the creation-tales she is given shape as a giant woman, the formless deep made flesh, who was defeated by the creator-god so that the world could be made from what she had been. Where Marduk is the hand that struck and Ereshkigal the stern face fate wears for mortals, Tiamat is the thing behind them both - the unmade, unknowable beginning, too vast and too strange to be truly worshipped, only remembered.
