# The Blood-spattered Feather

The Blood-spattered Feather is the most feared assassins' guild of the duck-folk, and it takes only black ducks into its ranks. Where the brown ducks make warriors and the white ducks merchants and scholars, the black ducks turn pirate, and the very worst of them turn killer, and find their way at last to the Feather.

## Methods

The guild's members move in utter silence, and where another assassin would strike and be gone, the Feather prefers to carry its mark off alive and put him to the question at leisure. The guild-killer Arne Steelwing is infamous for one slow torment in particular, the tickling of a feather across the tenderest skin, that is said to have broken stronger creatures than it ever managed to kill outright.
