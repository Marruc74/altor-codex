# The Brotherhood of the Eternally Shining Star

The Brotherhood of the Eternally Shining Star is the knightly order of the god Eledain, and its members are widely known simply as the Knights of Eledain. They go in shining armour with the god's eight-pointed star upon the breast, and are famed less for feats of war than for the pacifist, high-minded ideals they keep.

## Creed

The Brotherhood stands for the bright things its god embodies: light, piety, magnanimity, life, creation and luck. Above all it holds that force is the last resort of all. Its knights never draw a weapon first and take up arms only at the very end of need, when no other way is left, which sets them apart from the prouder and more worldly orders of other faiths.

## The Knights of Eledain

For all their gentleness of creed, the knights are not helpless. They guard Eledain's mountain temples, strongholds set high and strong that double as refuges in time of war and house the god's magic-masters and healers as well as fighting men. When the light itself is threatened the last resort comes, and the order rides to war: it was knights of the Brotherhood who took the field against the dark lord Dakoth in the long struggle of the Eastlands.

## A Nameless Hero

The order's ideals are best remembered in a single knight whose name was never learned. He appeared as if from nowhere when raiders fell upon a frontier village, drove them off bearing his god's star, and died at the village wall in the doing of it. The grateful villagers buried him as a hero with all his shining gear in a hidden chamber beneath their church, and the tale of the Nameless Knight outlived the village itself.
