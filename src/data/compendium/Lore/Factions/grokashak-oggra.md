# Grokashak Oggra

![Grokashak Oggra](/compendium/Lore/Factions/grokashak-oggra.jpg "Grokashak Oggra")

Grokashak Oggra, the Sick Eye, is a small free-roving band that keeps to the Burned Earth Clan, some thirty-six strong and every one of them a warrior: thirty-three kraukh-orcs, two ylk-orcs and a lone rese. All of them are outcasts, driven from their own tribes for being too undisciplined or too dangerous to keep, and they are led by Grokashak himself. They range freely through the Aidne Mountains and the northern tip of the Kasenus Forests, living as nomads but holding faithful to the clan.

To hide the old ear-marks of the tribes that cast them out, every one of them has cut away the outer ears, and their shields are hung with a clutter of trophies: bone-bits, scraps of armour and captured banners. They are hard fighters, berserkers and six raiders among them, undisciplined but fearsomely aggressive, and they are often set to guard the clan's chieftains. They hold to Kraukh, and to a degree to Ylkaur, but owe no loyalty to any bone-man, and they have little love for dwarves, humans or satyrs.

Among them is the rese Trog, one of the largest of his kind, who bears an oversized double-axe and hates the svartnissar.
