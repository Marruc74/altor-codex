# Urgh Grobb

Urgh Grobb, the Swine-eaters, is a tribe of kraukh-orcs in the Burned Earth Clan, about two hundred and thirty strong with some hundred and fifty warriors, led by the chieftain Drogh.

The tribe is known for its initiation: a young warrior earns his place by killing and eating a live wild boar. Urgh Grobb holds an old grudge against the mountain- and kraukh-orcs gathered at the Black Tower, and that feud colours much of what the tribe does.
