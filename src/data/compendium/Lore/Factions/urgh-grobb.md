# Urgh Grobb

Urgh Grobb, the Swine-eaters, is a tribe of kraukh-orcs in the Burned Earth Clan, about two hundred and thirty strong with some hundred and fifty warriors, led by the chieftain Drogh. It dwells at the northern tip of the Kasenus Forests. Its mark is four cuts on the underside of the ear, and its shields are hung with the bones of swine and of fallen foes. In battle it cries "Ylock Grobb," eat them alive.

The tribe is known for its initiation: a young warrior earns his place by catching and eating a live wild boar. It is godless. The Swine-eaters ate their last bone-man, and the bone-men hold no sway over them since. Somewhat undisciplined and feared for its savagery, the tribe goes lightly armoured for speed and, with the Bloody Claw, forms the spearhead of an attack, where its mastery of orc hand-to-hand fighting tells. Among them is Brylk, small for an orc but with enormous jaws and tusks, who eats his way through a foe while the foe still lives.

Urgh Grobb holds an old grudge against the mountain- and kraukh-orcs gathered at the Black Tower, and it usually comes off the winner in single combat against them.
