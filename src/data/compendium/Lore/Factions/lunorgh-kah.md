# Lunorgh Kah

Lunorgh Kah, the Mad Moon, is the head tribe of the Burned Earth Clan, the orc power that holds the northern Aidne Mountains and is known for burning everything it cannot carry off. The tribe is made up of kraukh-orcs and worships the god Kraukh. Its mark is two round holes cut in the tips of the ears, and its shield bears a white full moon crossed with blood-red. In battle the warriors cry "Rulgh Lunorgh," moon-blood.

The tribe runs to about four hundred and sixty, of whom near three hundred are warriors, led by the chieftain Urgnagg. He is an unusually able leader for an orc, shrewd enough to keep a captured human as an adviser, and the bone-man Dryggl serves as his priest. Lunorgh Kah supplies thirty of the breakers who work the clan's great battering-ram, the Death-bringer.
