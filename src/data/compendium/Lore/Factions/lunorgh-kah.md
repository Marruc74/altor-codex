# Lunorgh Kah

Lunorgh Kah, the Mad Moon, is the head tribe of the Burned Earth Clan, the orc power that holds the northern Aidne Mountains and is known for burning everything it cannot carry off. The tribe is made up of kraukh-orcs and worships the god Kraukh with a fanatic's zeal. Its mark is two round holes cut in the tips of the ears, and its shield bears a white full moon crossed with blood-red. In battle the warriors cry "Rulgh Lunorgh," moon-blood, or blood for the moon.

The tribe runs to about four hundred and sixty, of whom near three hundred are warriors, and it keeps three fixed settlements on the highland around the northern Aidne Mountains. It is led by the chieftain Urgnagg, an unusually able leader for an orc: he can talk a little politics and bargain with civilised peoples in the middle of a siege, and when his own position is threatened he sets other orcs against one another rather than fight. He keeps a captured human as an adviser. His bone-man Dryggl is dangerously skilled and hungry for power, a master of bone-magic and the rites; under the high bone-man stand six lesser bone-men.

Lunorgh Kah is well-organised and unusually heavily armoured. It commands the clan's two stone-slings and its mobile siege-tower, fields warriors of every weapon-kind, and supplies the thirty breakers who work the clan's great battering-ram, the Death-bringer.
