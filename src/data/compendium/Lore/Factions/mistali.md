# Mistali

The mistali are the most dangerous warriors of the grey elves, the sea-folk's elite, sent in only when nothing else will win the day. They strike in fast, bloody raids and then vanish, coming by ship or swimming through open water to fall on the enemy where they are least expected.

## Ways

Every mistali shaves the crown of the head, wears a black-and-white cloth, and carries amulets of fish-bone. None can match them for endurance in the water: they swim far faster than a man and hold their breath for minutes at a time, and about one in twenty is gifted somewhere in life with the power to breathe water outright. Pitiless and tireless, they are what any enemy of the grey elves fears most at sea.
