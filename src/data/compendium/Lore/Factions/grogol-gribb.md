# Grogol Gribb

Grogol Gribb, the Dwarf-stranglers, is the Burned Earth Clan's tribe of mountain-orcs, the girnackh. It is the smallest of the clan's main tribes, about a hundred and seventy strong with some hundred and ten warriors, led by the chieftain Dooghrack and served by two bone-men, Nacklock and Ylknogh.

As mountain-orcs they are heavier and stronger than the clan's kraukh-orcs, and as their name promises they are first and worst the enemies of dwarves, whom they hunt and kill wherever the high passes bring the two peoples together.
