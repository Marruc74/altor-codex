# Ordo Magica

Ordo Magica is the order of magicians that rules the city of Krilloan from the shadows. Founded in 374 eO, it numbers more than a thousand in the city alone, better than half of all its magicians, and it governs not by open decree but by holding the strings of the city's council.

## The Hidden Hand

The order commands five of the council's twenty-six votes outright. Through the commandant, the watch-captain and the other officers it has bought or cowed, it holds fourteen in all, enough to carry whatever measure it likes while the Grand Vizier signs his name to it. Its will is enforced by the Sabertooth Guard, seventeen elite soldiers in sabertooth helms, each of them sworn to a single master of the order.

## The Order

Seventeen archmagi, the Masters, lead Ordo Magica, with the Order-master and the Masters of the Tower, the Books and the Gate set above them. Its seat is a tower of seventy-six floors in Meh-Sylfhin, the district built on tall pillars of stone. For a yearly fee the order opens to its members a great library of forty thousand books, schooling in the arts, the trade and analysis of magic items, spells for hire, and the city's fire-watch.

## History

Vlad Kamantur founded the order together with Shaza Sahivann and Toalpios T'ahhah. After the death of the pirate-emperor Kain in 377 eO it rose to rule Krilloan as a magocracy, a council of nineteen magicians, and expanded its reach for more than a century. The Great Fire of 486 cast the magicians down and ended their open rule, but the order endured, and in our own day it has taken the city back into its grip.
