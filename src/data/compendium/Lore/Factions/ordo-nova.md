# Ordo Nova

Ordo Nova is the secret merchant society of Nohstril, the capital of Erebos, founded around 250 eO by the city's leading traders to guard their own interests and keep the trade free. It numbers some two hundred and fifty members and is ruled by a Council of Seven seasoned merchants. It works in secret because a good part of what it does runs against the law, yet it operates with the quiet blessing of the Council of Five Houses that governs the city, and it has never sought power or recognition beyond its own ground.

## Overview

- **What it is:** A secret society of merchants in Nohstril, capital of Erebos
- **Founded:** around 250 eO, by the city's foremost traders
- **Strength:** some 250 members, ruled by a Council of Seven
- **Aim:** to protect its members' interests and keep the trade free
- **Watched by:** Ocula Negra, which keeps a spy inside the order
- **Its halls:** hidden chambers in the catacombs beneath Nohstril

## How It Works

Members pay a yearly fee and share out the profit on the cargoes the order protects. In return Ordo Nova arms ships for members who have none of their own, makes good their losses, and serves as the forum where new markets and trade-routes are weighed. It is not above staging pirate raids on rival trading houses, and membership tends to pass from father to son, with newcomers taken in only on recommendation and after careful testing. It keeps clear of affairs of state outside its own concerns, and runs its unofficial trade-policy with the tacit approval of the city's rulers.

## The Lower Halls

The order began digging its own catacombs under Nohstril soon after 260 eO, and they grew with it. During the invasion of Sagrin the Conqueror they served as a refuge and as a way to bring in vital goods through a guarded entrance outside the western wall. When the passages at last grew together with the rest of the city's catacombs, the order cut its lower level — its meeting-halls and storerooms — behind a secret door, kept by guards and reached only with the password. Members come and go through entrances hidden in their own houses.
