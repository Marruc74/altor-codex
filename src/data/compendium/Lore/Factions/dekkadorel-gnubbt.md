# Dekkadorel Gnubbt

Dekkadorel Gnubbt, Big and Strong, is a small band of resar that keeps company with the Burned Earth Clan without truly belonging to it. There are only about seventeen of them, but each is a three-metre giant of a creature, so the band counts every member a warrior. Their leader is Grymm Bergskross, with Kahllk Hjärnskvätta beside him as adviser. They are hugely built, the hair shaved into stripes across the head, and their shield bears two crossed silver axes on blood-red. Their war-cry is "Bwaargh-arak-arak," forward, kill, kill.

Fifteen of the seventeen are breakers, and not even houses or keeps are left standing after them, so the band is a spearhead in any pitched battle. They are enemies of all, and of dwarves and elves above all others. Foremost among them is Grettrum Aurrgh, a legendary berserker who must be bound with thick iron chains whenever there is no battle to spend him on; even the leaders are breakers themselves.

They worship an obscure war-god named Harrakk, and they hire themselves out as mercenaries, asking at least five hundred silver for their service — and only dark folk may hire them. Bound to no clan, they keep on friendly terms with the Burned Earth Clan among others.
