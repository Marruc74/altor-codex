# Dekkadorel Gnubbt

Dekkadorel Gnubbt, Big and Strong, is a small band of resar that keeps company with the Burned Earth Clan without truly belonging to it. There are only about seventeen of them, but each is a three-metre giant of a creature, so the band counts every member a warrior.

They worship the war-god Harrakk, and they hire themselves out as mercenaries, asking at least five hundred silver for their service. Their leader is Grymm Bergskross, with Kahllk Hjärnskvätta beside him.
