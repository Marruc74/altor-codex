# Treeshapers

The treeshapers are the elven gardeners of the living wood, masters of the craft that lets the wood elves live in the forest instead of cutting it down. It is they who coax the great trees into halls, dwellings and whole villages up in the crowns, so that a satari settlement is grown rather than built.

## Ways

A treeshaper can make seeds sprout in minutes. An acorn set in the ground at dusk may be a towering oak by morning. Given time and quiet the work costs them nothing, and even the trees and plants near a treeshaper grow faster and stronger for the company. They give themselves wholly to the green world, and are most often found among the amari and the deep forest tribes.
