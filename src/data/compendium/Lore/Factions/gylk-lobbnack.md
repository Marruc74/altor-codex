# Gylk Lobbnack

Gylk Lobbnack, the Howling Skulls, is a tribe of kraukh-orcs in the Burned Earth Clan, about two hundred and ten strong with some hundred and sixty warriors. Its chieftain is Funglobb, and its bone-man Gagnaf, who also serves the tribe as an oracle, reading the gods' will and passing on their words. The tribe worships Kraukh. Its mark is the hair-tails of slain foes knotted through holes in the ear, and its shields bear the face-bone of fallen foes' skulls, or a painted skull with its mouth open in a howl. In battle it cries "Ignarth," burn.

The tribe dwells in the hollows of an old stone keep on the highland north of the Aidne Mountains' tip, a hold very hard to take. It is undisciplined, an ordinary tribe of no special strength, and so it is given the poorer places in the horde's array. Gagnaf is reckoned a great religious leader across the whole clan, yet he is little understood by his own people, and his readings of the bone-signs often fling the tribe headlong into bitter campaigns.
