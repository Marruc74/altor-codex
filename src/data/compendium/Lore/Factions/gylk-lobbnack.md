# Gylk Lobbnack

Gylk Lobbnack, the Howling Skulls, is a tribe of kraukh-orcs in the Burned Earth Clan, about two hundred and ten strong with some hundred and sixty warriors. Its chieftain is Funglobb, and its bone-man Gagnaf, who also serves the tribe as an oracle, reading the gods' will and passing on their words.
