# Deathbringers

The deathbringers are small bands of elves, usually three to seven strong, who have given their lives to fighting the powers of darkness. They wander the world hunting evil, and they seem to know where and when it will surface, so they are often found waiting where trouble is about to break.

## Appearance

Deathbringers dress in black and red and wear heavily painted armour. Each carries blood-red feathers, one for every great battle it has fought in, and paints a red streak across the eyes before a fight. A deathbringer rides to war in black steel under a red mantle, with a sword, an elven longbow and two full quivers, a shield, and often a battle-trained horse.

## Ways

Deathbringers take no prisoners. Years of fighting the dark have left them hard to frighten, even before the worst of foes. A few have earned grim names that outlast them: the Orc-slayer, Blood-eye, the Ripper, Steel-bird.
