# RhobdoRana

RhobdoRana, the brotherhood of the striped frog (also written RhabdoRana), is an order of assassins that ranges across Ereb in the service of Ghumgakk, a demon of vengeance from the tale of Inashtar's descent into the underworld. They number perhaps a hundred and fifty in all, working in small cells of four to eight, and every member blackens the inside of the lower lip in homage to the demon.

What sets the brotherhood apart is a crude shared mind that binds its members together, a dim collective awareness that makes any one of them very hard to coerce or break by magic.

## Methods

RhobdoRana are patient, inventive killers. Among their skills are a silent, motionless knockout blow they call the hammer-blow, contortionist escapes, hypnosis, and a trick of moving each eye on its own. Their killings are made to look like anything but murder: a scythe that comes apart to be hidden, garrotte wire, fire-ants, trained weasels, red tree-scorpions, strangling spores, the strober-spider that brings on fits and burrows into the nose, and splinter-emerald, a crushed crystal that grows in the blood until the victim seems to have died a natural death.

## The Hunt for the Oracles

By the year 613 eO the brotherhood had been taken over from within by the deathless sorcerer Shagul, who rules it in secret through its collective thought. He set the assassins to murder every astrologer in Trakorien, to hide the timing of the fifth conflux, and to hunt down the foreigners who had once seen his true face on Marjura. That hunt, and the race to reach four oracle-seers before the assassins do, is the heart of the campaign known as the Oracle's Four Eyes.
