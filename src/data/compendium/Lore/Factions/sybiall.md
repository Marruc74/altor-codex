# Sybiall

A demonology academy in Krun, known among those who study such things as the demons' hell on Altor. Where most would never dare hold a demon for long, Sybiall keeps a dozen of them at once, shackled and caged, as subjects to be studied and questioned.

## The Caged

The academy's demons are held in demon-shackles and in great steel cages that sap their strength of body, mind and limb, and there they are kept as test-subjects and pressed for what they know of Inferno. The demonologers of Sybiall are reckoned to have unusually strong minds - which some lay to the bargains they strike with the knowledge-demons, and to the strain the work puts on the spirit.

## Caucron

The academy's most notorious prisoner is the demon Caucron, who in 320 eO stole the portal of an inexperienced demonologer and used it to ravage the villages of northern Krun. He has been shackled at Sybiall ever since, more than three centuries a captive, one more voice to be interrogated about the world he came from.
