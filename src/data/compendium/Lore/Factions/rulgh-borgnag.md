# Rulgh Borgnag

Rulgh Borgnag, the Bloody Claw, is the largest single tribe of the Burned Earth Clan, a tribe of kraukh-orcs about four hundred and eighty strong with some three hundred and twenty warriors, holding three fixed settlements in the northern Aidne Mountains. Its chieftain is Groth, and its bone-man Bloghvas. Its mark is two cuts on the upper edge of the ear, and its shield bears a bloody double-claw in white and red. Its war-cry is simply "Rulhg," blood.

The tribe is built for the charge. Nearly a third of its warriors are berserkers, and some hundred and twenty are claw-bearers, fighting with the hooked claw strapped to the forearm, a weapon that carries high status among them. Foremost of them is Urughk-Grigg, a claw-bearer, berserker and raider standing well over two metres, a pure terror to face. Being the clan's greatest tribe, Rulgh Borgnag takes its choice of position when the clan musters for war, and it chooses to stand nearest the enemy.

It is Rulgh Borgnag that keeps and guards the clan's great battering-ram, the Death-bringer, holding it well hidden in a cave in time of peace.
