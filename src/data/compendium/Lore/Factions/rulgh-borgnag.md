# Rulgh Borgnag

Rulgh Borgnag, the Bloody Claw, is one of the larger tribes of the Burned Earth Clan, a tribe of kraukh-orcs about four hundred and eighty strong with some three hundred and twenty warriors. Its chieftain is Groth, and its bone-man Bloghvas. Its shield bears a bloody double-claw in white and red.

The tribe is built for the charge. Nearly a third of its warriors are berserkers, and some hundred and twenty are claw-bearers, fighting with the hooked claw strapped to the forearm, a weapon that carries high status among them. It is Rulgh Borgnag that keeps and guards the clan's great battering-ram, the Death-bringer.
