# Ylkor kha oggra

Ylkor kha oggra, the Mad Dogs, is the Burned Earth Clan's svartalf tribe, about a hundred and eighty strong with some ninety warriors, led by the chieftain Wombutt. As svartalfs they bring the clan what orcs cannot: wolf-riders, the swift mounted scouts and spearhead that scatter an enemy line ahead of the heavier orc tribes.
