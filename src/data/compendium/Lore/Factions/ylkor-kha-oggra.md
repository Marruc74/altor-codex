# Ylkor kha oggra

Ylkor kha oggra, the Mad Dogs, is the Burned Earth Clan's svartalf tribe, about a hundred and eighty strong with some ninety warriors, led by the chieftain Wombutt. It dwells on the highland between Klomellien, the Kasenus Forests and the northern tip of the Aidne Mountains. As svartalfs they bring the clan what orcs cannot: wolf-riders, the swift mounted scouts and spearhead that scatter an enemy line ahead of the heavier orc tribes, deadliest of all in a charge. Their spears are dressed with wolf- and ulf-tails below the head, and their small parrying shields are set with wolf- and ulf-teeth. Their war-cry is "Eff grobb nichh yngrykk," the last to arrive gets no ears to eat.

The Mad Dogs are loyal to their chieftain and ride with a great wolf-pack, some sixty to eighty ulfs in their service. They worship Baawk, the wolf-god, and the moon, which they take for a god of its own. Foremost among them is Utugg the Mad, the finest wolf-rider of them all, a raider who cannot be thrown from the ulf's back. Ranging as widely as they do, they have made many enemies, and of them all they hate the wood-elves and the centaurs most.
