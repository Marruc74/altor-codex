# Kharynos

Kharynos (the name is from the old Nidish word *Qarhinosh*, "the clan") is the great crime-family of the south, a web of households bound together under a single head. It deals as readily in banking and honest trade as in murder and smuggling, and there is no telling, from the outside, where the lawful business of Kharynos ends and the unlawful begins. One is born into Kharynos, not recruited; to be invited in from outside is a rare and high honor, close to an adoption.

## Origin

The clan was founded in 469 eO in Felicien, by refugees who had fled the bleak country of Nidland. From nothing they built a power that no honest house in the south now dares to cross.

## The Family

Kharynos is strictly ordered. At its head sits a council of the household-heads; below them stand the specialists, the assassins, gentleman-thieves, mages and the like, and below these the sons and daughters, the ordinary members, who pay a tithe of all they earn into the family's coffers.

## Trade, Fair and Foul

About half the family's business is lawful: trade houses, banks and honest crafts. The rest is assassination (it keeps its own poisoner and its own killers, though for a clean job it would rather hire the Assassins' Guild), together with a highly developed protection racket, smuggling, spying, bribery and extortion.

## Strongholds and Enemies

The clan keeps many seats, one to each family and a place where the council gathers, usually the fine house or deep cellar of some member; none dare attack them, for fear of the family's vengeance. Its enemies are the merchant houses with their hired swords, and the other crime-organizations, the thieves' guild above all. But the worst enemy of Kharynos has always been Kharynos itself, and its bloodiest wars are the feuds it fights within its own walls.
