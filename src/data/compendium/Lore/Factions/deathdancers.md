The deathdancers are elves who have given their lives to one thing: perfection in battle, what they call dancing with death. They live apart and wander alone, given to meditation and a hard, ascetic life, and they keep so much to the wild that many take them for forest-spirits rather than elves.

## Ways

A deathdancer hones the blade in solitude, year after year. Once a year they come together, and through the night they sing and dance with their swords until dawn. They paint their bodies and hair white with clay and wear little else, and they are among the deadliest warriors of all the elf-folk.
