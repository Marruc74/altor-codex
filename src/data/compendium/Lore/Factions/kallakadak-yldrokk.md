# Kallakadak Yldrokk

Kallakadak Yldrokk, the small black-dipped beasts, is a band of svartnissar that trails after the Burned Earth Clan, attached to the tribe of the Bloody Claw. They number about a hundred, nearly all of them counted as fighters in the swarming way of their kind.

The svartnissar believe the Bloody Claw protects them, and they bring the orcs gifts to keep that favour: rotten fruit, handfuls of grey stones, whatever they take to be precious. The orcs tolerate them as a useful nuisance.
