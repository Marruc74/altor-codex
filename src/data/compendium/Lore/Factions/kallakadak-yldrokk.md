# Kallakadak Yldrokk

Kallakadak Yldrokk, the small black-dipped beasts, is a band of svartnissar that trails after the Burned Earth Clan, attached to the tribe of the Bloody Claw. They number about a hundred, nearly all of them counted as fighters in the swarming way of their kind, and they are led by Herryttras Frylkem Dreddras, who is at once their chieftain, their priest and their best warrior. Before each fight they bathe the whole body but the head in sticky black paint, and their war-cry is "Bwack-gibbler-erck-erck-erck," words that mean nothing but sound grand to them.

They carry no shield-marks; their arms are stonemason's tools taken from a sculptor, and a special squad of seven works clamps and tongs on a foe the others have pinned. Herryttras Frylkem Dreddras dresses himself as a bone-man, painted white, and is as dangerous as a good svartalf warrior, his chief weapon a long heavy pair of tongs. They worship the moon, which they hold to be a giant wheel of good cheese, and a young one proves his manhood by eating an old, reeking, mould-ridden cheese for the gas and the visions it brings.

The svartnissar believe the Bloody Claw protects them, and they envy and all but worship the orcs, counting everyone else an enemy. They bring the orcs gifts to keep that favour, rotten fruit and fine tree-branches and whatever else they take to be precious, and the orcs tolerate them as a useful nuisance, following along in hope that something amusing will happen.
