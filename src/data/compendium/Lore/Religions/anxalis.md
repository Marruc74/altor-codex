# Anxalis

Anxalis is the capricious queen of the sea-deeps, worshipped wherever folk live by the water and fear it. She gives and she takes as her mood turns, and the sailors of the islands court her favour before any voyage.

Her order is the Ambaquaorden, led from the temple at Soblak and keeping temples besides at Tigôld, Frimbole Olvi, Fam Kvalvi, Okruzande and Karnald. Those of her priests she favours are said to be able to still a storm at sea, to swell a boat's catch of fish, and even to walk upon the water as if it were dry land.
