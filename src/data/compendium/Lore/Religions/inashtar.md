# Inashtar

![Inashtar](/compendium/Lore/Religions/inashtar.jpg "Inashtar")

Inashtar came down from the ice-barbarians of the northeast, and she is the patron goddess of the Runglers and of all Trinsmyra. She is the goddess of fire, of war, and of unbridled desire, shown as a beautiful and devastating woman bearing a spear and pieces of armour, wreathed in wild flame. Her signs are the dove with a hawk's head and the strings of a harp.

## Ecstatic Worship

Across Trinsmyra she is worshipped without restraint, in towering bonfires, wild music and dancing, in orgies and contests of arms. There is no church to bind the faith together, and every woman is held to be more or less an Inashavi, a daughter of the goddess - which is why women stand so strong on Trinsmyra, stronger than anywhere else in the islands.

## The Sects

Out of Inashtar's worship grow darker branches. Some give themselves to the demon Ghumgakk, "the Avenger with the Black Lips." From her cult rose the RhabdoRana, the Brotherhood of the Striped Frog, a guild of hired killers founded around 150 eO that has terrorised the north of Ereb ever since. And there is the Little Leather-Fairy, queen of the astrilder - tiny, mostly unseen flying sprites who meddle in the affairs of lovers, and who are prayed to for desire or for a child.
