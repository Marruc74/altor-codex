# Valliman and Drigel

Valliman and Drigel are two god-brothers who together bear the sun across the vault of heaven. The tale tells that Drigel, for some folly of his brother's, shut Valliman away in the underworld, and the turning of the seasons is bound up with that imprisonment.

Valliman's fate is kept each year in the ritual called Valliman's Lament, which mourns the death of nature and celebrates its resurrection, and his story is told again in the old poem Valliman's Forest. Where his worship is kept, the dying and the greening of the year are read as the brother's fall and his return.
