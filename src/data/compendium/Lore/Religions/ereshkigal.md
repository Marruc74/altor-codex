# Ereshkigal

Ereshkigal is the goddess of the death-realm, and though the living fear her she is not reckoned an evil power. She is the stern keeper of fate, who turns her hourglass at every birth and gathers each soul again when its sand has run out. To her worshippers she is a simpler, more comprehensible face of the vast and formless chaos that older myths call Tiamat - the unknowable made into something a mortal can pray to.
