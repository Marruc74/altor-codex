# Slergolis

Slergolis is the goddess of darkness and gluttony, and the maker of the trolls, whom she shaped out of stone to rule the forests and mountains by night. The trolls are not a devout people and call on her only when they are in trouble, but she is theirs alone, and her hand is felt at every midwinter feast they hold in her honour.

## Overview

- **Name:** Slergolis, mistress of darkness and gluttony
- **Made:** the trolls, out of stone
- **Worshippers:** the trolls, and few others
- **Her gifts:** luck in battle, good fortune, and food that tastes the better for her favour
- **Her feast:** midwinter, the one great gathering of the troll-kind

## The Troll-Maker

Slergolis is pictured as a vastly fat, naked troll-woman with growths like a giraffe's horns rising from her head, her skin black or dark brown, and something always in her mouth, for she is forever eating. She gives her people luck in their fights and their ventures, and, in keeping with all she stands for, she makes their food taste better. The trolls thank her chiefly at midwinter, when the whole kind gathers to gorge and sing, and otherwise leave her be until misfortune drives them to ask her help.
