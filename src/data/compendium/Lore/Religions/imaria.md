# Imária

Imária is the goddess of the undead and of eternal torment, and the mightiest of the eight demon-princes of the Oktagon, who are bound to Raukhra, the evil red moon. Hers is a worship of the grave and of suffering made sacrament, kept in secret wherever the dead are made to serve and pain is offered up as a gift.

## The Chained Goddess

It is told that Imária does not range free, but lies chained upon the Montures, far to the north in Ereb, bound there by powers that feared her. Her cults labour toward the day of her release, and reckon the slow torment of the living and the raising of the dead as works pleasing to her. Among the demon-princes of the Oktagon she is held the greatest, and the necromancers and torturers who serve the red moon name her first.
