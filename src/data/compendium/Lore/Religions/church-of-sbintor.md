# Church of Sbintor

The Church of Sbintor is the established church of the island duchy of Montures, the institution built around the invented sun-god Sbintor to bind the islanders together and to fill the duke's coffers. Founded in 434 by Duke Benoval and his sister Abina, it grew into a power in its own right, and its head sits among the rulers of the realm.

## The Clergy

Its priests are drawn from the upper and middle classes and schooled in administration, astrology, heraldry, history and theology, as much officers of state as men of religion, and they keep a martial art of their own. They are known by the sun tattooed on breast and back, and the wandering monks of the order by their pale-yellow robes, dyed with the root of the munkrygg grass. Only a handful at the very top know that the god they serve was a fabrication of the dukes.

## Law and Offering

Every grown islander is bound by law to make a yearly offering to the church, and those who do not pay meet "Sbintor's wrath." So the church gathers in a great and steady wealth, much of which flows on to the duke's treasury, and through its hold on faith, record and ceremony it reaches into every corner of life in Montures. For the doctrine and the great temple at its heart, see Sbintor.
