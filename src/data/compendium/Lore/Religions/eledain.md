# Eledain

Eledain is a god of light and of the stars, and to his followers the giver of life, creation, magnanimity and good fortune. He is honoured by people of every kind, along the wilder frontiers and across the war-torn Eastlands alike, and though his faith is carried as much by sword and shining mail as by prayer, his knights count violence the last resort of all. His sign is an eight-pointed star, set like a compass-rose, worn on the breast of his knights and raised above the temples and frontier churches built in his name.

## Overview

- **Name:** Eledain, the Lightbringer, god of stars
- **Domain:** light, stars, life, creation, piety, magnanimity and luck
- **Symbol:** an eight-pointed star, in the manner of a compass-rose
- **Rival:** Sathmog, god of fire, hatred and malice
- **Knightly order:** the Brotherhood of the Eternally Shining Star, whose knights are known as the Knights of Eledain
- **Worshippers:** a broad and mixed following, from lone frontier villages to the temple-realms of the Eastlands

## The God of Light and Stars

Eledain stands for the bright and life-giving things: the light of the stars, the turning of the seasons, the making and keeping of living things, and the plain virtues of piety and open-handedness. His worshippers are not one people or one class but a great mixture, drawn together only by what the god holds dear. In the unsettled north a single village far from any town might raise a church to him in thanks for its good years, while in the Eastlands his name anchors whole realms; the same god is honoured in both, under the same eight-pointed star.

## Worship and Symbol

The mark of the faith is the eight-pointed star, shown upon altars, vestments, shields and banners wherever Eledain is served. His churches keep his light against the dark in every sense, and his priests are reckoned healers and counsellors as much as clergy. Because his following is so scattered and so varied, custom differs from place to place, but the star and the devotion to light and life are everywhere the same.

## Knightly Orders

Eledain is served by a single knightly order, the **Brotherhood of the Eternally Shining Star**, whose members go in shining armour with his eight-pointed star upon the breast and are widely known simply as the Knights of Eledain. The order is famed for its pacifist and high-minded ideals, standing for light, piety, magnanimity, life, creation and luck; its knights never draw a weapon first and take up arms only as an absolute last resort. Yet when the light itself is threatened that resort comes: it was the Brotherhood's knights who guarded Eledain's mountain temples and rode to war against the dark lord in the long struggle of the Eastlands, and a lone knight of the order who once died at a frontier village's wall driving off a raid. Those temples, set high and strong, are home to magic-masters and healers as well as fighting men, and serve as refuges in time of war.

## Eledain and Sathmog

In the Eastlands the servants of Eledain were for ages the bitter enemies of the servants of Sathmog, the god of fire, hatred and malice, two faiths of all but opposite temper whose followers counted one another mortal foes. It took the rise of the dark lord Dakoth, a danger greater than either, to force them into one army, and the slow fading of an older evil's influence to let them set aside the worst of their hatred. Even reconciled they remain uneasy allies, their truce held together more by common danger than by any love, and apt to fall back into the old quarrel once the shared enemy is gone.
