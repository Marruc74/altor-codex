# Tigwalvan

Tigwalvan, sometimes Tigwalwan, is a god of light, held in special honour on the island of Mereld. Where the harsher faiths of the islands preach strength and self-love, his teaching runs the other way, toward mercy, humility and open-handedness.

His worship is carried into the world by the Kafriler order, who keep relief-houses for the poor and the sick in all the larger cities of Palamux and Paratorna. The Kafriler brothers go among the wretched of the great trading towns doing the charity their own god's neighbours - Kastyke above all - hold to be a waste, and they are valued for it even by those who do not share their faith.
