# Marduk

Marduk is the lord of storms and weather, one of the old gods inherited from Jorpagna, and his hand is felt by farmer and sailor alike. He holds sway over the winds and the rain at sea, and it is in his gift to send the downpour that saves a harvest or the one that ruins it.

The old creation-tales set him at the very beginning of the world: it was Marduk who struck down the primal chaos, and the deepest myths name his weapon in that war - the axe Geardon, whose first blow against the monster is remembered in the great gap of the Dragonback called Geardon's Gap.
