# Enki

Enki is the lord of fresh water and of wisdom, one of the old archetypal gods that Ereb's southern cultures inherited from Jorpagna. He dwells, the faithful say, in Abzû, the boundless underground lake from which all sweet water rises, and he is the guardian of age and of tradition - the keeper of what is old and tried against the rashness of the new.

His chief temple stands at Frimbole Olvi, and greater temples to him are kept at Tricilve, Soblak, Privavi, Zaroflas and Melse, and a somewhat faded one at Favoki. Where men prize learning and the wisdom of their elders, it is to Enki they look.
