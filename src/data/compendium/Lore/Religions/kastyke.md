# Kastyke

Kastyke is the patron goddess of Paratorna and of the Trakorien realm, though she began as a local hunting-goddess among the skecker, the dog-headed first folk of the woods. Her teaching is that life is a struggle in which one must never give up, and that the goddess lends her aid to the strong and the active hunter. Its heart is a single hard saying: "Kastyke loves the one who loves himself."

## A Church of Business

Kastyke's priesthood is, before all else, a brotherhood of businessmen; priestly rights are often simply bought with gold. The head of the church is the Pillar-Father, who resides in Fam Kvalvi and appoints all the higher clergy. One Pillar-Father, Maxulaf the Pious, had folk put to death for plain commercial gain. The faith owns but a single saint, Famrahjold, whose body was cut into pieces and sold off as holy relics. Her priests wear the strutzer-helm, a guard against outer uncleanliness.

## The Doctrine of the Strong

By Kastyke's teaching a soul sins only through what it does, never through what it leaves undone - so there is no call to pretend at charity. The poor and the sick deserve their misery, for the goddess does not love them, and a worshipper need feel no obligation to play the beggar's friend. The skecker, whose goddess she once was, want nothing to do with this modern, mercenary cult. Kastyke grants her faithful no special gifts; the advantage of her worship is purely the worldly one of money and power.
