# The Oktagon

The Oktagon is a company of eight demon-princes bound to Raukhra, the evil red moon, and the demon-cults that serve them. Theirs is no open faith. They are worshipped in secret, chiefly by the Soluni, in cities such as Krilloan where any god may be served and few questions asked.

## The Eight Princes

Foremost of the eight is **Imária**, goddess of the undead and of eternal torment, said to lie chained upon the Montures far to the north. With her stand **Onaabys** the Soul-eater, prince of sicknesses; **Khergoll** of anarchy and chaos; **Maal**, the prince of pain, whose worship is self-torment; **Zinn** of the sand and the desert, served in southern Soluna; **Tupagon** the Fly-queen, mother of the insectoids; **Hasitaal** of carnal devotion, who has not yet come to Krilloan; and **Gendilj**, the renegade of the Oktagon, whose cult is the smallest of all.

## The Brotherhoods

Beside the cults of the eight run other dark fellowships. The Black Rose Brotherhood worships the goat-footed demon Hemaquiel, the Tempter, and prizes the hallucinogenic black roses that bear its name. The Star Doctrine passes itself off as a society of star-readers, but in truth it serves Onaabys. A pact with any of these powers is bought with five years of service, and at the last with the servant's own soul.
