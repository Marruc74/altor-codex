# Luvena

Luvena is the moon-goddess, and also the goddess of sickness and of the creeping death that comes slow and unseen. For all that, she carries an undeserved ill name in Trakorien, where she is in truth worshipped openly and commonly, a familiar figure of the night sky rather than a terror.

The darkness gathers at her edges, though. On Palamux there are secret moon-cults that keep her worship in a fouler form, giving themselves to necromancy, to human sacrifice, and to the eating of human flesh - and it is these hidden sects, more than the goddess herself, that have blackened her name among honest folk.
