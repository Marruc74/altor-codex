# Ley Lines

Across the face of Altor run the ley lines, unseen rivers of magical force. A magician who works his art close to one finds his power grown of itself, and where several lines run near together the swelling is greater still. Where those currents cross or rise to the surface they gather into the magic-nodes, the strongest places of all, and where they fail to reach at all lie the magic-dead lands.
