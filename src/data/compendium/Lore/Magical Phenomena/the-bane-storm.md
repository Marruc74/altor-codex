# The Bane Storm

![The Bane Storm](/compendium/Lore/Magical Phenomena/the-bane-storm.jpg "The Bane Storm")

The Bane Storm is the most dreaded of the magical scourges of Ereb, a vast black thunderstorm that comes on like a dark flood-wave rolling over the land, and leaves behind it nothing but waste and silence. Where it has passed, the fields are dead, the people gone, and the country stands empty for years after.

## Overview

- **What it is:** A vast black storm, like a dark flood-wave, that lays a land desolate
- **Worst recorded toll:** More than thirteen thousand dead in the land of Thai
- **Associated with:** The walking dead, and a moving gateway to the realm of death
- **Disposition:** Utterly ruinous; nothing living is left behind it

## The Storm and the Door of Death

The Bane Storm is no common weather. Those who have seen it and lived tell that the dead walk in its shadow, and the wise hold that somewhere within the black wall of it there opens a gateway to the realm of death, a door that moves with the storm and does not stay in one place to be found or shut. The dead that follow in its wake are reckoned to come through that door. Its most famous passage swept the land of Thai and killed upward of thirteen thousand souls, a toll that has fixed its name in dread ever since.
