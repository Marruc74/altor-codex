# The Three Wolves

The Three Wolves are three brothers out of Zoraryd in the county of Greveryd, in Zorakin, whose name makes the nobles of Ereb tremble. A local lord had their father hanged, and the brothers have given their lives ever since to breaking the power of the nobility wherever they find it.

## The Brotherhood

They are never short of allies. Their banner is a great red shield bearing three black wolf-heads, and beneath it they gather forces drawn mostly from battle-trained peasants who have taken up arms against their oppressors; even dark folk have been seen marching in their company. The eldest of the three is Abdon, a man of thirty-five, and where the Three Wolves ride, the lords of the land bar their gates.
