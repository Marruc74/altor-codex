# Sebastian Marol

Sebastian Marol was born in 578 in Zorakin, the third son of a nobleman, raised on the family estate and trained in arms as every young Zorakin noble is. With no inheritance to come to him his father meant him for a knightly order, but at nineteen he was unmasked as a drug-smuggler, cast off by his family and driven into exile.

## The Soldier

He went north to Trakorien and took service as a soldier, fighting in the wars on Marjura through the Sulphur Winter, where he made a name as a hard and capable man. There he earned the lasting hatred of the shagulite sect by killing one of its leaders over its meddling in the peace between the Trakorians and the Marjurans. Returning south in 601 he turned adventurer, and on an expedition into the deserts beyond the Copper Sea he lost his right eye to a manticore's dart, his open helm no guard against it.

## The Red Owl

Since then he has lived as a mercenary captain, having founded a heavy cavalry company, the Red Owl, named for its device of a silver field charged with a red owl clutching a black mouse, and he has hired his sword across Zorakin, Erebos, Kardien and beyond, with a name good enough that he is seldom short of a contract. He is cold and hard, always purposeful and rarely at a loss, solving his problems with little regard for the people caught up in them, the very model of an officer. He shows little feeling, almost never speaks of himself, and pours what he has into books and training, holding to the right of the strong and able to rule the weak and the ignorant. His eyes are an icy grey, and his cropped brown hair has gone partly to grey.
