# Tara

Tara is a witch of some two hundred and seventy-five years, feared throughout Ereb. Most who catch sight of her flee, and those who have crossed her and lived speak of her as no woman at all but a demon clothed in flesh.

## The Black Sheep

She was born Taramhylia, in the country where the heart of old Jorpagna once lay, the black sheep of her family, and ran away from home at twelve. Found half-starved and senseless by the elemental magician Kovenier D'Reih, she was taken in and taught the rudiments of his art; in the years since she has killed more than one teacher for refusing to open the inner mysteries of magic to her. In 369 she vanished without trace from the academy she had risen to head, and returned three years later wanting nothing to do with anyone, withdrawing into the eastern reaches of the Aidne mountains, where she lives still and from which she wanders Ereb on dark errands of her own.

## A Demon in Woman's Shape

The high priest Loshekrick Bignose, who tried to hunt her down and was one of the very few to escape with his life, swore afterward that she was filled with the blackest evil of the abyss. She can take many shapes, but her own is that of a true witch: a floor-length black robe of heavy wool, woven through with noble-silver that turns aside blows and frets at magic, a tall pointed hat, and a tangle of unkempt black hair. Everything about her radiates cruelty. Surly, ruthless and contemptuous, she nurses a hatred of the human race that cast her out, and delights in others' suffering, often toying with a good-hearted fighter at length before she kills him. It was Tara who made the flying broom Qhriz, binding a spirit into it with a ritual that all but drained her.
