# The Hunter

The Hunter is the strange guardian-spirit of the Vergili Forest on the Montures, the agent through whom the forest-god Mokylider makes his will felt among the trees. The hunters and woodsmen who keep the old faith speak of him in their songs, and those who wrong the wood learn to fear him.

## The Forest's Warden

The Hunter appears as a pale humanoid figure crowned with a stag's horns, but he wears no single shape for long: he can take the form of any animal he pleases, and move at will to any corner of the Vergili Forest. He commands its trees and its beasts, and the worst of his powers is kept for those who break the forest's law without need, slayers of beasts and fellers of trees, whom he can turn into trees themselves. He carries a panflute of his own enchantment, and so long as the wood is respected he lets travellers pass unharmed.
