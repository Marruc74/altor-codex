# Baron Piet Steeljaw

The most feared pirate of the western Copper Sea, and once a lord of Zorakin. Baron Piet Steeljaw ruled his lands with a cruelty that brought the church's ban down on him and his own king's condemnation, and rather than answer for it he took to the sea. Every attempt to capture him since has failed, and there is three thousand silver on his head.

## The Steel Jaw

He is a big man, near six feet and built mostly of muscle, his hair cut short and black, his face burned brown by years at sea. The name comes from the jaw he lost four years ago, when an Erebos squadron ran him to ground near Hakkai and a boarding-pike took him through the chin in the fighting. The dwarf alchemist and inventor Guazzo Arathaso, in Krilloan, made him a jaw of steel to replace it, and he hides the join behind a great false beard - it takes a sharp eye to see that the beard is loose.

## The Man Behind It

Until his middle thirties Steeljaw was simply a hard and pitiless ruler. The banishment turned all of that to hatred, and it is hatred he carries to sea. Yet with his own crew he is another thing entirely: stern but fair, neither favouring a man beyond his deserts nor cheating him of them. The eighty who sail under him obey without a murmur, and to them he is as a god - a fine leader and a deadly fighter, and every man who has challenged him to a duel is dead. He sails the galleon Wave-Crusher out of the hidden island base of the Harpies' Nest, a red-clad figure with a parrot on his shoulder and a chalk pipe in the corner of his mouth.
