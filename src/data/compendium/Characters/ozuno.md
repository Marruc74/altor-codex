# Ozuno

Ozuno is a prince among the oni, and where most of his kind are content to serve, he means to rule. From a fortress raised in secret by his slaves inside the crater of the Fire-Peak, above its lake of lava, he spins a patient web of intrigue across the northern Jih-pun province of Nara, and he counts the emperors and shoguns of mortals as no more than passing disturbances to be outlasted.

## The Long Game

His aim is nothing less than a realm of his own among the mountains of Bei-kodo, built by creeping into the councils and trades of people and hollowing them out from within. In Nara that means unseating the daimyo Sorin Arima and setting in his place a double of Ozuno's own making, with the help of a ninja he has slipped into the lord's bed as a concubine. To that end he has bought the loyalty of the Shima ninja clan with food and medicine, sworn a secret pact with the proud rival daimyo of Gihata, and set his agents to smuggle the shogun's gemstones and to peddle an addictive drug through the province.

## The Mask

Ozuno's true shape is a serpent with arms and the head of a wolf, but he is never seen in it; he goes always in human form and gives himself out as a mighty magician who has built himself a dwelling among the peaks. He is a cold and effective politician, skirting open force, for his one real weakness is a shortage of able servants: with only a handful of agents to work through, he acts with great care, never showing his hand before he must.
