# Kaelene Fenholt

![Kaelene Fenholt](/compendium/Characters/kaelene-fenholt.jpg "Kaelene Fenholt")

Kaelene grew up in Poane, a grey harbour town in the salt-country of Klomellien, and clawed her way out of it, which is more than most there manage. She could lift a purse and read a crowd before she was grown, and that skill is what saved her: a quiet man called Tarek saw it, took her in, and taught her the rest.

## The Thief Tarek Made

Tarek trained her in Poane until she could walk into a room and know everyone in it inside a minute. He taught her the marks the holy orders leave behind, the Shining Way's sunburst-and-sword among them, and that reading has opened more doors since than any lockpick. Three years before the trio's first road job, the two of them went down into a tomb beneath ruined Jorpagna. Only Kaelene walked back out.

## The Cold Thread

What they woke down there was old and crowned, and it took Tarek. It left something in her too, a thread of cold that runs from that tomb into her sleep, where she walks the same dark corridor night after night. For years she carried the Hollow Mark without seeing it, a sign no mirror ever showed her. She can read a stranger in seconds and misses the thing written on her own skin, and the whole long hunt turns on that.
