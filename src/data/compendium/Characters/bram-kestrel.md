# Bram Kestrel

![Bram Kestrel](/compendium/Characters/bram-kestrel.jpg "Bram Kestrel")

Bram was born in Brinewatch, a whitewashed fishing village on the Zorakin coast, the son of a dock master who loved his sons through work and expectation and not much else. One morning Bram walked out rather than inherit the harbour, carrying a worn ship-coin his brother had pressed on him, and he has been meaning to go back ever since.

## The Road

He learned his trade as caravan muscle on the long circuit between Vesket, Morral and the debt-broken village of Mern, under a weathered guard named Orvith who taught by showing rather than telling. It ended at Adrath, where he warned a village about a soft-voiced moneylender named Corvel and lost his post for it. The caravan master let him go without hard feeling. He does not regret the warning.

## The Black Sword

On the Misty Island, Bram took a pitch-black broadsword called the Deathbringer out of a drowned hall, and a dead champion's warding medallion with it. The sword's runes call living fire when they are spoken aloud, and for a long time the calling frightened him more than any enemy did. His road since has been the slow work of learning that the thing he feared was never the sword but his own reaching for it. By the time he carried it into a dragon's cave, he could leave the fire sheathed.
