# Aelthira Moonveil

![Aelthira Moonveil](/compendium/Characters/aelthira-moonveil.jpg "Aelthira Moonveil")

Aelthira Moonveil is an elf of the Academy at Atrema, the university city below the Carenthi peaks, where she studied the schools of magic for six years and then left rather than keep teaching something she knew was false. She travels with the trio as its scholar, though what she is really doing is chasing that lie all the way to its root.

## The Academy

She had a steady study-partner in Fen Marwick, who counselled caution and then carried her bag to the gate when caution lost, and a mentor in Professor Carenthal, who always took her seriously and was, in the end, part of the institution's refusal. The thread she pulled was a single hedging word in the Mireth Principles, a foundational text that wrote "generally" where it should have written nothing at all. She pulled, and the whole buried lie came with it.

## The One Who Names It

Where Kaelene carries the cold and Bram carries the sword, Aelthira carries the knowing. She is the one who recognises the Hollow Mark when it turns up cut into stone, and the one who can tell a true working from the cold's own shadow. Each near-rhyme she rules a thing with a maker and a method, not the buried horror itself. It is careful, unglamorous work, and it is the reason the trio have stayed a step ahead of what follows them.
