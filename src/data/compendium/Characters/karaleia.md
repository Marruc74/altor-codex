# Karaleia

![Karaleia](/compendium/Characters/karaleia.jpg "Karaleia")
![Karaleia](/compendium/Characters/karaleia-2.jpg "Karaleia")

Karaleia VII was once the queen of the Montures, and is now an undying vampire and the faithful servant of the demigoddess she loosed upon her own people. She lies sealed in an obsidian sarcophagus in the Agino Mountains, and on her freedom hangs the fate of the isles.

## The Queen Who Damned Her People

Long ago, granted a vision of the demigoddess Imaria, Karaleia freed her from her mountain prison, and so unknowingly sold her own soul. Imaria turned the whole people of the islands into walking dead, and Karaleia herself was made a vampire. When the land broke in the Third Conflux and the imariot city fell into the Gideon Chasm, the queen fled to a crypt and was sealed within it, where she remains, knowing nothing of all that has passed in the world since.

## An Unquiet Vampire

Karaleia is no creature of the old tales. She casts a reflection, walks unharmed in sunlight though she dislikes it, needs no coffin, and fears neither cross nor holy sign; only blood, not the sun, keeps her strength. She is a mighty necromancer and a consummate actress and infiltrator, and her one purpose is the service of Imaria. Should she ever be freed from her sarcophagus, it is she, above all others, who could set the chained goddess loose again, and with her the doom of every living soul on the Montures.
