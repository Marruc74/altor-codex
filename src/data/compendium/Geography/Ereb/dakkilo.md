# Dakkilo

Dakkilo is the smallest of the islands of Erebos, and very nearly the whole of it is given over to the capital, Nohstril. Beyond the city walls the land is poor, only thin pasture for steppe-goats and for the long-wooled sheep that are the island's quiet fortune.

## Nohstril

Nohstril is the heart of the realm, seat of the Council of Five Houses that has ruled Erebos for a century, and the island's life is essentially the city's life. From here the great merchant houses weigh their ventures and govern by unanimous vote, each holding a veto over the rest.

## The wool of Dakkilo

What land lies outside the capital is grazed by long-wooled sheep, and their fleece is woven into a warm, fine fabric prized far beyond the islands. Of late it has become fashionable among the wealthy of Kardien and Felicien, and it is one of the goods that keep the realm's fleets busy.
