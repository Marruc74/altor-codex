# Beyural

Beyural is an island of Erebos, set in the Copper Sea, its land raised and shaped by old volcanic fire. The dark soil of its lower slopes is deep and fertile, while the higher, drier ground bakes under long seasons of heat. Between them the island carries hills, terraced fields and gnarled groves, and its people have learned to draw a steady living from ground that gives nothing easily.

What Beyural is known for is its bounty. The grain hersch grows tall across the fertile slopes and feeds the island and much of the coast beyond it. The lullad tree yields an oil prized for its long keeping, and the hills give up the sunstone, a quiet amber gem whose trade has long enriched the place. Through it all moves the hornox, the broad horned beast that pulls the plough and hauls the stone, without which little of the rest would be possible.

*Expanded from the brief official lore of Beyural in Ereb Altor.*
