# Tolokfe

Tolokfe is the smaller island of Erebos that the sailors call the Cyclops' Isle, and it is shunned by all. By legend the cyclopes were cursed onto it in ancient times, and no trees grow upon its bare ground, so that the shipwrecked have no means to build a craft and escape.

## A shunned shore

Treacherous currents, the Megalossundet and the Gensnuporgraven, together with a perpetual fog, keep Tolokfe almost wholly unexplored. The very few who return come back mad, and one invasion of some five hundred men once vanished upon it without a trace.

## The cyclopes

The cyclopes dwell in vast cave-cities beneath the island and raise steppe-goats in the dark. They are not evil, only hungry, and what they wish above all is to be left alone, a wish the rest of Erebos is glad enough to grant them.
