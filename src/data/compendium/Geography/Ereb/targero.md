# Targero

Targero is the largest of the islands of Erebos and also the poorest, a rugged land whose people grow bigger and tougher than their southern kin. Most of the realm's fleet-soldiers are recruited here, and lively fishing sustains its coasts where the soil will not.

## A rugged frontier

Targero is the only island of Erebos troubled by dark folk, both orcs and trolls, and its high places carry an ill name. Sailors trade rumours of perytons, hydras, basilisks and even dragon-serpents lurking in the mountains, and few travel far from the coast without good reason.

## The Jorpagna ruins

In Targero's mountains lie the ruins of old Jorpagna, for when the empire spread to these waters in the 700s fO the island was more fertile than now and a favoured haunt of mages. The ruins still draw treasure-hunters, though the dark folk and the wild beasts of the heights make the search a perilous one.
