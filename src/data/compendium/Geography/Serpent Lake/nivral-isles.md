# Nivral Isles

The Nivral Isles are a long chain of islands set in the Serpent Lake, the sea that lies between Samkarna and Soluna. Sailors of the Copper Sea speak of them as "the world's edge," for to many who reach them these scattered crowns of green and stone mark the limit of what is known and mapped. Beyond them the charts grow uncertain and the waters less friendly to the hand that would tame them.

The islands hold the kingdom of Niferland, a realm built on trade and welcoming to merchants from far coasts. Yet the way to the Nivral Isles is never easy, for the Serpent Lake's winds are weak and ships must be driven across it by the twin banks of oars in the slave-galleys. To the west turns the great whirlpool Faryngia, the World's Throat, a reminder set always at the edge of sight that the sea here is never wholly tamed.

## Overview

- **Type:** Island chain in the Serpent Lake
- **Byname:** The world's edge
- **Holds:** The trade-friendly kingdom of Niferland

## The World's Edge

For the sailors who cross from the Copper Sea, the Nivral Isles are where the certain world ends. The harbors of the chain are the last that fly familiar flags, the last where charts can be trusted and where a captain knows what lies under his keel. South of the isles the water warms and the rule of law thins, and so the islands stand as a final anchor before the open unknown.

## Niferland in the Isles

The chief power of the Nivral Isles is the kingdom of Niferland, whose harbors are never silent and whose galleys range the northern waters of the Serpent Lake. Through Niferland the isles trade with distant lands, taking in goods, news, and stories from coasts far beyond the horizon, and it is largely on this kingdom's account that the Nivral Isles are known at all to the wider world.
