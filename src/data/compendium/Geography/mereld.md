# Mereld

![Mereld](/compendium/Geography/mereld.jpg "Mereld")

Mereld is an island republic out beyond Trakorien, ruled in all but name by its magicians. Most of them are animists and elemental mages, and the island is best known for its academy, the Centre of the Spheres, which draws students from across the Copper Sea. The merelder keep no crown over themselves, and they look on their neighbours with a mild, faintly superior goodwill that those neighbours do not always thank them for.

## Overview

- **Government:** free republic, led in practice by its magicians
- **Religion:** animism; the light-god Tigwalvan, kept by the Kafriler order
- **Known for:** elemental and animist magic, and the academy called the Centre of the Spheres
- **Neighbours:** lies near the Trakorian colony of Bzegusta, across the seas from Trakorien

## Geography

Mereld is an island, set among the many small powers scattered across the seas from Trakorien and close to the Trakorian colony of Bzegusta. It sits within reach of the Copper Sea trade, far enough from the great kingdoms to go its own way.

## People

The merelder are a people of mages rather than of soldiers. Animists and elemental magicians set the tone of the island, and its learning matters more to it than its arms. They meet other folk with a patient, overseeing kindness, sure of their own good intentions, and that surety can grate on those they mean to help.

## Government

Mereld is a free republic with no king and no noble house above the rest. In practice the magicians hold the say, and the academy stands at the centre of the island's life, so that learning and rule are hard to tell apart.

## Religion

The faith of the island is animism, and its keepers are the Kafriler order, who worship Tigwalvan, the god of light. They preach mercy, humility and open-handed giving, and they put it to work in hospices for the poor and the sick. Their mission reaches well past Mereld: in Trakorien the Kafriler have run charity houses for the poor and the sick on Palamux and Paratorna ever since the war of 523, helped along by gifts from Bodåken.

## The Centre of the Spheres

The island's renowned academy was founded by Gonesastra the Good, and her spirit is said to watch over Mereld still. It teaches the elemental and animist arts, and it is reckoned one of the best schools of magic on the Copper Sea. That reputation is much of why so small a place gets taken seriously at all.

## History

- **523 eO**: Trakorien makes war on Mereld and loses, thrown back by the island's magicians. Since that defeat the Kafriler order has tended the poor and the sick across Trakorien.

## Foreign Relations

Mereld keeps a free hand and an even temper toward its neighbours. Trakorien once tried to take it by force and failed, and the two have settled since into an odd peace, with Mereld's own Kafriler order caring for the poor of the realm that attacked it.
