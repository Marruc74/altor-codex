# Maminira me Delema

![Maminira me Delema](/compendium/Geography/Samkarna/Maminira me Delema/Maminira1.jpg "Maminira me Delema")
![Maminira me Delema](/compendium/Geography/Samkarna/Maminira me Delema/Maminira2.jpg "Maminira me Delema")

Maminira me Delema, the archipelago of the rustling races, is a scatter of islands off the great southern continent of Samkarna. Its name comes from the dry, ceaseless rustling that fills its forests and shores, for the islands are home to insectoids of enormous size - and these are no mindless vermin, but creatures possessed of true intelligence, with purposes and patience of their own.

The archipelago is little known to the wider world, and that ignorance has been bought at a terrible price. For it was from here, long ages past, that the doom of an empire crawled forth.

## Overview

- **Type:** Archipelago
- **Inhabitants:** Giant, intelligent insectoids
- **Distinction:** Birthplace of the koettbitare swarm

## Geography

The islands lie off the coast of Samkarna, that wet and swampy continent teeming with deadly life. Warm and rain-fed like the mainland, they support the vast insectoid races from which the archipelago takes its name.

## The Rustling Races

The insectoids of Maminira me Delema grow to great size and, unlike common insects, are intelligent. The constant rustling of their movement gives the archipelago its name, and marks every part of it as their domain.

## The Coming of the Koettbitare

In 599 B.O. a swarm of flesh-eating grasshoppers, the koettbitare, rose out of these islands. Crossing by way of the Samkarna marshes, the swarm spread north and fell upon central Ereb, devouring all in its path. In its wake the great Empire of Jorpagna was brought down - a catastrophe whose origin lay here, among the rustling islands of the south.
