# Golwynda Sea

The Golwynda Sea is the great freshwater sea that lies cradled between the lands of Ereb, Soluna and Akrogal. It is the largest body of sweet water known to the peoples of the three continents, and a thing of wonder to any who first look upon it, for its waters do not lie flat. The press of so vast a sea against the lands that hem it in forces the whole expanse upward, so that it bulges toward the sky like a great glass mountain. At its swollen heart the Golwynda stands roughly 250 metres higher than the Copper Sea below, and from that height it strains always to escape its banks.

Because the sea sits so much higher than the waters around it, no keel can ever pass between the Golwynda and the Copper Sea. The currents that race toward the edges are too strong for any oar or sail to master, and where the sea finally breaks free it does so in thundering falls that would shatter any ship foolish enough to ride them. The Golwynda is, for all its size, a sea sealed against shipping - a sea that may be drunk and fished and worshipped, but never crossed from one sea to the next.

## Overview

- **Type:** Great freshwater sea between Ereb, Soluna and Akrogal
- **Surface:** Bulges upward like a glass mountain, roughly 250 m above the Copper Sea
- **Shipping:** Effectively sealed - currents too strong, falls impassable
- **Outflows:** Raas Narram, the Demon Tongue, Grelgifallen

## A Sea That Cannot Be Sailed Through

The same currents that make the Golwynda so mighty also make it useless as a highway. A vessel that drifts too near the rim is seized and dragged toward the falls, and there is no rowing back against the pull. For this reason the trade that other seas carry on their backs is here pushed ashore. The wealth of the Golwynda changes hands not on the water but in the port-towns that crowd beside its outflows, where goods are landed, hauled overland, and loaded again onto fresh vessels in the lower seas. Chief among these are Stabul, which sits beside the falls of Raas Narram, and Ircront in Nidland.

## The Three Outflows

The Golwynda drains its excess through three great mouths, each a marvel in its own right.

- **Raas Narram**: a vast waterfall in the west, pouring down to the Copper Sea beside the ruined lock-stair of Emperor Garhues of Krun
- **The Demon Tongue**: where the sea bores through the stone bridge linking Ereb and Akrogal and jets out over the Maseva Bay, worshipped as the god Okasu
- **Grelgifallen**: the eastern falls, lower but far broader than the others, holding within them the magicians' island of Habete Grelge
