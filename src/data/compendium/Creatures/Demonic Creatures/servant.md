# Servant

Servants are ordinary humanoids, usually necromancers, who have struck a pact with a demon noble, a demon prince or some other power great enough, gaining special gifts in return. Bound by the bargain and its rules, they are often set a task: succeed and near-limitless power and wealth await; fail, and so does a fitting and agonising death.

## Appearance

Nothing outward betrays a servant or their pact.

## Behaviour

Nothing unusual marks them out, but a servant's presence sours the land within half a kilometre: milk and cream turn, butter goes rancid, every flame burns a round clear blue, crops stop growing, cocks fall silent at dawn, and paint will not dry.

## Abilities

A servant has one or several dark gifts, such as a withering energy-beam, ageing or rejuvenating a victim, burning out eyes with a glance, flight, or various demonic powers. They are undone by holy ground and water, salt or garlic in a wound, an iron nail driven into their footprint, a horseshoe hung points-up, and the bite of a copper coin.
