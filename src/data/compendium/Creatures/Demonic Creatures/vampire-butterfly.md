# Vampire Butterfly

Vampire butterflies are blood-sucking demon-creatures out of Chaos, at home in any environment and often blundering into our world through the many dimension-gates of the Chaos realm; they belong to no single plane.

## Appearance

The body is a finger-long, extremely slender red humanoid demon with a thin tail, sharp fangs and razor claws on hands and feet, and great wings about half a metre across that shimmer endlessly through every colour of the rainbow.

## Behaviour

A blood-drinking predator that flies up silently behind its prey, lulls it, then sinks its teeth into a vein and drains blood, though it takes only so much that the victim usually survives, weakened for a time.

## Abilities

Anyone who sees its wings must resist or be lulled by the strange shifting colours; destroy its body and it simply returns to its home plane to grow a new one.
