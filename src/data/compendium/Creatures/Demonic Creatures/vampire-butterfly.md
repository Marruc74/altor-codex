# Vampire Butterfly


![Vampire Butterfly](/compendium/Creatures/Demonic Creatures/vampire-butterfly.png "Vampire Butterfly")
Vampire butterflies are blood-sucking demon-creatures with their origin in Chaos. They fare well in any surroundings and often end up in our world by mistake, flying among the strange dimension-gates of the Chaos dimension. They have no fixed home, but are scattered across all the planes of the Multiverse.

## Appearance

The vampire butterfly's body is a tiny, extremely slender red humanoid demon, no longer than a hand, with a thin tail of the same length. The demon has sharp fangs and razor-sharp claws on both hands and feet. Its large wings, spanning about half a metre, shift constantly through all the colours of the rainbow.

## Behaviour

The vampire butterfly is a blood-sucking predator that attacks by flying up silently behind its victim and fluttering close to lull him into sleep, then sinking its teeth into a throat-vein or wrist to drain the body of blood. It always drinks a fixed measure of blood, so the victim may well survive the attack, though weakened until his strength returns over the following days.

## Abilities

Anyone who sees a vampire butterfly's wings must steel his mind, or be lulled to sleep by their strange, shifting pattern of colour. A vampire butterfly whose body is destroyed returns to its plane of origin and there creates a new one.
