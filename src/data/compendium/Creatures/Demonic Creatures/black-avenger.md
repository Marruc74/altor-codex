# Black Avenger

A black avenger is about the most dangerous thing one can meet in the mortal world, the Death Angel perhaps aside. It is what results when a very powerful undead, such as a death wraith, is possessed by a demon noble, and it always has one fixed task, to guard some object or to kill some person.

## Appearance

A two-and-a-half-metre knight in gleaming coal-black full plate with a black cloak, only a red light showing behind the visor, always armed with a demonic double-axe that cuts through any material.

## Behaviour

It has one purpose and does nothing else: set to guard a thing, it guards it, and if the thing is stolen it hunts the thief, kills him and his fellows, and returns the loot to its place; sometimes this frees it, sometimes not.

## Abilities

It never eats, drinks, tires or grows bored, travels underwater or through the air as fast as on land, and hacks straight through any physical barrier in its way.
