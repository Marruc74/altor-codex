# Quetzal

Quetzals are feathered, foul-smelling flying-lizard demons, bred and ridden as mounts by the legions of the Abyss. They are loyal and witless in equal measure, and that is the point of them: a quetzal will carry its rider straight into a fight it cannot survive and never balk, flying to its own certain death at a word of command.

Where a demon host goes to war the quetzals go in their thousands, bearing whole legions through the air. They have no will to spare for self-preservation and no cunning to question an order, which makes them as expendable as they are useful to the things that ride them.
