# Chaos Corporal

A chaos corporal is weaker than a chaos knight, the chief difference being that it always travels with ravagers and slayers, usually appearing in our world in a band of five to fifteen chaos warriors under a corporal's command. The band tries to hire on as sellswords, and failing that falls to raiding small villages and farms.

## Appearance

As a chaos knight: a powerful warrior in real or illusory full plate.

## Behaviour

As a chaos knight, leading its war-band to feed where it can.

## Abilities

Like all chaos warriors it shapeshifts, drains life by touch, and reverts to its true form when its body falls.
