# Ravager

Ravagers are the absolute opposite of slayers. Going into battle they grow utterly savage and attack until the foe has fallen, heedless of their own wounds.

## Appearance

Ravagers look like perfectly ordinary humans, usually in leather armour or a coat of mail. They always fight with two-handed weapons, chiefly the double-axe or the two-handed sword. Their true form, like all chaos warriors, is a green-shimmering lump of jelly hung with eyed and clawed tentacles.

## Behaviour

One must search far to find more aggressive and savage fighters than the ravagers. They love to fight above all else and are very easily provoked, and once they have begun they do not stop until they are cut down.

## Abilities

A ravager strikes twice in the press of a fight, at the cost of a little of its own vigour with each blow, and feels no wound at all until it falls, whereupon it reverts to its true jelly form and is wholly restored. It can heal itself slowly, sinking for a moment into a demonic trance, wholly shut off from the world. Like all chaos warriors it can shapeshift into any humanoid, drinks life-force by touch, and can remain in the human world about a month before vanishing without trace.
