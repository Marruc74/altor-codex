# Ravager

Ravagers are the slayers' opposite: once in a fight they turn utterly wild, attacking until the foe is dead and caring nothing for their own wounds.

## Appearance

They look like perfectly ordinary people, usually in leather or ring-mail, and always fight with two-handed weapons, by preference a double-axe or great sword.

## Behaviour

You will search far for fighters more aggressive or wild; they love battle above all and are quick to anger, and once started they do not stop until they drop.

## Abilities

A ravager strikes twice a round, losing a little of its own vigour with every blow, feels no injury until it falls, can heal itself in a brief demonic trance, and reverts to its true form, fully restored, when its body is destroyed.
