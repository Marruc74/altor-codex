# Light Dragon

Light dragons are close kin to fire-dragons, though much smaller and nowhere near as powerful. Their chief weapon, the bolt of light, is mightier than either the gout of flame or the cone of cold, which makes them very dangerous foes all the same. Like fire-dragons they live in high cliff-caves, but they will also den in the throats of dead volcanoes, where a dozen dragons may sometimes be found in one place, a thing unique among dragonkind, though each always keeps its own cave.

## Appearance

A full-grown light dragon runs to only about four metres from nose to tail-tip and stands barely a metre and a half at the highest point of the back. It has large, slender wings that grant it superb flight, for it is the swiftest and most agile flier of all dragons. In other respects it is almost an exact copy of its larger cousins, save that in pitch darkness its red scales can be seen to give off a faint, warm, yellowish glow, enough to light the whole cave. Treasure-crust is commoner on light dragons than on other kinds, and theirs is always made up entirely of jewels.

## Behaviour

Like other dragons, light dragons spend the greater part of their lives asleep in their high mountain caves. They are extremely fond of gemstones rather than gold, and often keep enormous heaps of rough, cut and set jewels in their hoards. They are predators whose bearing matches well what one expects of a dragon, though they tend to be aggressive rather than watchful, and they always take care to keep their distance from any potential foe, since a light dragon's natural weapons, the bolt of light aside, are not especially formidable.

## Abilities

The light dragon's answer to the gout of flame, the bolt of light, is a great spray of lightning that can reach up to two hundred metres with great precision, each bolt striking like a lightning-stroke. All who see the burst are also badly blinded for a short while unless they steel their minds, left as though in pitch darkness. In all else the light dragon shares the common dragon traits: keen senses, burning black blood, and dragon-magic akin to mentalism.
