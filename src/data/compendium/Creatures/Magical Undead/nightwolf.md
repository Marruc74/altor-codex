# Nightwolf

The nightwolf is a dreadful undead, a constructed beast possessed by a ghost, most often the servant or bodyguard of the necromancer who made it, or the warden of something precious; it counts among the higher undead.

## Appearance

A giant coal-black wolf with two or three heads, about a metre and a half tall and two and a half long, with long, dense night-black fur and red eyes that spread malice wherever they look.

## Behaviour

Unlike other magical undead it needs no constant commands but has a will of its own, abroad only by night or where no sunlight reaches, for it cannot bear the sun.

## Abilities

Sunlight slowly burns it; its saliva is a venom that ages a victim a year a day until they die of old age, and its terrible howl strikes terror. The several heads act independently, one striking fear while the others tear.
