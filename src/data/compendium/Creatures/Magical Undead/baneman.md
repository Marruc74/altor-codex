# Baneman

Banemen are a kind of zombie that only a death knight can make, raised from fallen foes to serve as followers and servants; they count among the lesser undead.

## Appearance

It looks just as it did in life, but the skin is pale, lifeless and cracked with no blood in the cracks, the eyes and nails wholly black, and an air of death and darkness hangs about it.

## Behaviour

Having no will of its own, it obeys its death knight blindly, and since new banemen are easily made, the death knight spends them freely on any hard or dangerous task rather than risk itself.

## Abilities

It can fight with ordinary or natural weapons, but prefers to loose a beam of concentrated darkness from its eyes that drains a victim's spirit at ten metres' range.
