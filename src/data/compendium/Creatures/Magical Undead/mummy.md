# Mummy

By treating a corpse with chemicals and magic, a body can be raised as a mummy. They often serve as guardians of tombs and temples, for they obey blindly and need neither sleep, drink nor food; they count among the lesser undead.

## Appearance

A corpse preserved with embalming salves and wound in thick linen or cotton bandages. Being slow, they rarely use ranged weapons.

## Behaviour

Will-less servants of the mage who made them, they keep the skills of their former life save those of the mind, fight as cunningly as their maker, but can falter when a battle takes an unexpected turn.

## Abilities

Their wrappings give some protection but are flammable; a burning mummy feels no pain and fights on for a short while before collapsing into a reeking heap whose stench sickens all nearby.
