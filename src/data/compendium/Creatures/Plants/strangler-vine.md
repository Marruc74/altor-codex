# Strangler Vine


![Strangler Vine](/compendium/Creatures/Plants/strangler-vine.png "Strangler Vine")
The strangler-vine is a carnivorous, very greedy plant of unknown origin. It is sometimes used as a guardian, since it attacks anything that comes into contact with it.

## Appearance

The vine consists of a very short, central stalk on which sits a mouth that looks like a large pit in the ground, around a metre across and a metre deep. The insides of the mouth are coated in a very strong secretion that at once begins to dissolve any organic material it touches, and which can easily seep in through the joints of metal armour. Above the stalk the vine's branches spread into a bush that bears large green leaves all year round, partly masking what sits on the stalk, and from the upper part of the stalk stretch many tentacles that can grow up to four metres long.

## Behaviour

The vine's tentacles grasp automatically at everything that comes into contact with them, trying to catch and strangle it. When the victim is unconscious the vine carries it to the mouth, where it is swiftly dissolved so the vine can drink in the nourishment the victim is made of.

## Abilities

The tentacles do no damage themselves, but the first to strike successfully is taken to have coiled around the victim's throat, while the rest wind chiefly around the wrists and then the ankles. The one around the throat slowly tightens its grip, and each moment it holds on the victim weakens until at last he passes out from want of air, unless the tentacle is hacked off, whereupon a new one tries to take the throat in its place. An arm or leg snared by a tentacle cannot be used unless the vine's strength is overpowered.
