# Mushroom-Man

The mushroom-man is a creature shaped like an enormous fungus with two tentacle-like arms, dwelling in the damp, mossy forests. It is in truth a peaceable thing that asks only to be left alone, but it is dangerous to rouse, and turns on any who harry it.

## Appearance

A mushroom-man looks much like a giant mushroom, a thick pale stalk crowned with a broad cap, from which reach two long, groping tentacle-arms.

## Behaviour

It keeps to the wettest, mossiest woods, slow-moving and slow to anger, and would far rather be passed by than fought. Provoked or threatened, though, it lashes out at once with surprising heat.

## Abilities

The mushroom-man's very skin is poisonous, so that even to brush against it brings a burning hurt, and it can spray a cloud of venomous spores from its body. Those the spore-poison overcomes are thrown into wild fits, or frozen rigid, or sent fleeing in blind terror, or worst of all driven into a frenzy that turns them upon their own companions.
