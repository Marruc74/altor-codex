# Wraiths & Wights

Wraiths and wights are the mightiest of the undead spirits, risen from the rare few whose strength of soul in life was so overwhelming that death could not carry them off. A magician obsessed with a single spell, a thief consumed by one stolen treasure, a warrior bent on vengeance: when such a will dies, the spirit sometimes stays, and what remains is far more terrible than any common ghost.

## The Great Shades

The death wraith stands at the head of them, most often the shade of an extraordinarily mighty magician, and but for the death angel and the black avengers it is the most powerful undead thing in the world. The barrow wight is of the same kind but lesser, keeping to grave-vaults and catacombs where the sun cannot reach, for sunlight burns it; the dark wraith and the phantom are kindred horrors. All of them drift bodiless through the air, slip through the narrowest crack, and shrug off any weapon not armed against them.
