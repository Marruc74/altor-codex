Banemen are a kind of zombie that only a death knight can make, fashioning them from fallen foes and using them as companions and servants.

## Appearance

A baneman looks just as it did in life, but its skin is pale, lifeless and cracked, with no blood showing in the cracks. Its eyes and nails are wholly black, and the whole baneman is wrapped in an air of death and darkness.

## Behaviour

A baneman can use ordinary weapons or its natural ones, but usually relies first on its dark attack. Banemen have no will of their own and obey blindly the death knight that made them, and since a death knight can easily make new ones, it does not hesitate to spend them on every grim or dangerous task rather than risk its own unlife.

## Abilities

A baneman can shoot a beam of concentrated darkness from its eyes, reaching some ten metres and aimed at a single victim, striving to overcome his will; if it succeeds the victim loses strength of mind, recovered in time, and should that fall to nothing he dies. Like all magical undead it feels no pain, is bloodless, and has perfect vision in the dark.
