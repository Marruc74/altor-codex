# Magical Undead

The magical undead are corpses woken to unlife by a magician's necromancy. Skeletons, zombies and mummies are the plainest of them: dead bodies raised, stripped of any will of their own, able only to obey the one who called them up. A necromancer keeps them as guards, labourers and soldiers, tireless and fearless because there is no longer anyone at home to tire or to fear.

## When the Rite Goes Wrong

Near the lairs of necromancers the dead lie thick, and the air is thick with stray spirits, and there the raising does not always go to plan. A wandering spirit may slip into a zombie's empty body and make a living dead, far stronger in mind and body than either would be alone. Others take stranger shapes still, the baneman, the nightwolf, the imariot. All of them begin the same way, as dead flesh called back by magic rather than by any will of the dead themselves.
