# Tako

The tako is a giant eight-armed octopus of the seas around Jih-pun, and unlike the dull-witted kraken of Ereb's waters it is rapacious and clever, clever enough that some of them take up weapons. It can haul itself out of the water and move on land for as much as an hour, and its eyes give it sight on every side at once.

## In Battle

Each of its arms can strike on its own, and an arm that takes hold drags its prey down to be eaten. A few of the cunningest tako wield a blade or a club in one tentacle while the others grapple, which makes them a far worse danger to a small boat than any beast of their size has a right to be. To break free of a tako's grip is a matter of sheer strength against the strength of the arm that holds you.
