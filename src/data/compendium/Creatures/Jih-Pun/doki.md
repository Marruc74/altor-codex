# Doki

The doki is the nymph of the deep forests, who makes her home in the hollow of an old tree and seldom strays far from it. She is the woodland one of the three kinds, keeping her stretch of forest as the hannya keeps her crags.

She sings and heals and charms as her sisters do, and the traveller who turns off the path to follow her voice in among the trees may not find his own way out again with his mind his own.
