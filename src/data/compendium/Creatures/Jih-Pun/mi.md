# Mi

The mi is a distant and lesser kin of the tatsu, a beast six to eight metres long with a dragon's head and four short legs. Like the true dragon it runs upon the air and the clouds, but it is a poorer thing in every way: it speaks the human and dragon tongues only badly, it cannot breathe fire, and neither its breath nor its blood will burn.

## Habits

What the mi shares with its great cousin is greed. Driven always by hunger, it gathers what treasure it can into its nest, and a hoard heaped up over years may be worth the seeking, if the seeker can reach it. For the mi is itself hunted: the largest webs of the tree-spiders, the kumo, are spun strong enough to hold one, and the great burrowing mukade will take a mi as readily as anything else that crosses its tunnels.
