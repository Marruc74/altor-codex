# Kirin

![Kirin](/compendium/Creatures/Jih-Pun/kirin.jpg "Kirin")

The kirin is the unicorn of Jih-pun, a gentle horned beast that runs upon the empty air as lightly as over the ground, so that it seems half to fly. It is a shy thing and wants no part in the quarrels of the world, keeping to the quiet places and slipping away before it can be caught up in any fight.

No spell will bewitch a kirin, and none can drive it where it does not choose to go. Its power lies in its horn, though, and whoever takes the horn holds the beast a slave, to be granted three wishes before it goes free. For that the hunters of the islands have chased the kirin down the ages, and for that it has grown warier than almost any living creature.
