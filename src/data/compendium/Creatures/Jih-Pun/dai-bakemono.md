# Dai-bakemono

![Dai-bakemono](/compendium/Creatures/Jih-Pun/dai-bakemono-4.jpg "Dai-bakemono")

The dai-bakemono are the greater black-folk, the orc-kin of Jih-pun: big, cunning creatures who speak the black-folk tongue and fight with a blade in either hand. They are cannibals with a great weakness for sake, and the sharpest of them can train as shugenja.

Where a wizard or a warlord keeps such creatures it is the dai-bakemono who hold the gate and loose the lesser bakemono-sho of the wilds, standing over them as captains over a rabble.
