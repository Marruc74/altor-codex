# O-bakemono

The o-bakemono are the mountain-trolls of Jih-pun, larger and duller than the rest of the black-folk and more man-like for all their bulk. They swing the heavy iron club called a tetsubo, feel nothing of the sunlight that would trouble a western troll, and carry off comely women when they can.

Slow of thought as they are, they are the most numerous of the evil things on the islands, and most of the mischief blamed on the black-folk is really theirs.
