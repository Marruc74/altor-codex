# Mukade

The mukade is a giant burrowing worm, brown and slime-covered, with eyes that glow a sickly yellow and light the dark for ten metres around it. It bores its tunnels through fertile soil and eats whatever it meets, sparing nothing, and the greatest of them run twenty-five or thirty metres long and more than three metres thick.

## The Cocoons

What it cannot eat at once the mukade wraps and stores in cocoons of its own slime, and that slime is corrosive, eating slowly into anything it touches. Its innermost chamber is sometimes shared, after a fashion, by a mi that has gathered treasure there.

## Enemy of the Dragon

The mukade can swim as well as burrow, and it is the sworn enemy of the tatsu, the two beasts set against each other by some old enmity, so that where the dragon hoards in the heights the great worm tunnels beneath, and neither suffers the other to thrive.
