# Shura

A shura is the ghost of a warrior who met a sudden, needless or unnaturally violent death, and could not pass on for the wrong of it. It keeps the form it had in life, most often a stately samurai in full armour, and lingers at the place where it fell, hungry for vengeance on any who come there.

## Hard to Lay

The shura is all but proof against ordinary harm, and only magic, or weapons of silver or mulberry-wood, will bite on it. Driven down, it merely fades and seeks a new place to haunt, and within a month it is back. The rites of exorcism will move it on, but to end it for good its bones must be dug up, burned on a pyre of mulberry-wood, and the ashes scattered over the ground where it died.
