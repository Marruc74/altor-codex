# Nymph

The nymphs of Jih-pun are beautiful spirit-women of the wild places, and they come in three kinds: the **hannya** of the mountains and caves, the **doki** of the deep forests, and the **nushi** of springs and pools.

## Song and Spell

A nymph sings and dances and weaves with rare skill, and her song can heal a wound a little with every passing day. But a man who once looks upon her is in danger, for unless his wits hold against her beauty he is charmed and made will-less, following her without thought until she chooses to release him.

## Habits

They are gentle by inclination and quick to hide, wanting little to do with the affairs of mortals. Yet a spring or a wood that has a nymph for its keeper is a perilous place for a careless traveller, who may wander in after the sound of singing and not come out again with his own mind.
