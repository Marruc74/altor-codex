# Nushi

The nushi is the nymph of springs and pools, who dwells in huts built beneath the water and breathes as easily below the surface as above it. Fishermen tell of her singing rising from still water, and of men who waded in after it and were never seen again.

She means as little harm by it as the hannya or the doki, but her song charms all the same, and a charmed man in deep water is in danger whether the nymph wills it or not.
