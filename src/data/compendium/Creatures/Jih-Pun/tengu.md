# Tengu

![Tengu](/compendium/Creatures/Jih-Pun/tengu.jpg "Tengu")

Tengu are the proud bird-folk of the eastern forests of Jih-pun, feathered and beaked humanoids who hold their woods as their own and meet strangers with a drawn blade rather than a greeting. They are quick, vain, and easily slighted, and a traveller who blunders through their trees without the right deference is as likely to be challenged as warned off.

For all their fierceness they are warriors with a code of sorts, and a tengu fights as a point of pride as much as for any practical end. Those who treat the bird-folk and their forest with respect fare far better than those who take either lightly.
