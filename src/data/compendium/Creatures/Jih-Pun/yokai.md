# Yokai

![Yokai](/compendium/Creatures/Jih-Pun/yokai.jpg "Yokai")

Yokai is the common name in Jih-pun for a ghost, the restless spirit of one who died and did not pass cleanly on. They haunt the places that held them in life, half-seen and cold, and the sight of one is enough to shake the nerve of all but the steadiest.

Some do more than linger and frighten. As many as half of them reach into the sleep of the living to send nightmares night after night, in the way of the wicked uba, wearing a chosen victim down until rest becomes impossible. Like the other unquiet dead of the islands, a yokai is answered not with the sword but with the rites that lay a troubled spirit to rest.
