# Uba

An uba is a female spirit, gaunt and knotted of limb, with dark skin and stringy hair, and she may be a terror or a blessing. Some are wicked, nursemaids who devour the children left in their care; others are kindly, and more than one of Jih-pun's great heroes was raised and guarded by a good uba.

## Powers

The uba can take the shape of a black cat or melt away into mist, and her claws may drip a poison that is deadly to women alone. The wicked sort send nightmares to torment a chosen sleeper night after night until the victim can find no rest, and loose a maddening laughter on all who stand near. A few go further still and make pacts with evil spirits, taking a serpent's tail in place of legs.

## Telling Them Apart

The good and the evil look much alike, and the safest sign is in their bearing and their deeds rather than their faces, for the cruel ones tend to little beauty and great strength, while a kindly uba asks only to be allowed to tend and protect.
