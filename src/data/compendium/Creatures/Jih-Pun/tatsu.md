# Tatsu

The tatsu is the dragon of Jih-pun, a serpent fifteen to twenty-five metres long that bears no wings and yet runs upon the air and the clouds as easily as over the ground. Its hide shimmers like metal and is harder than any metal, green or gold or blue, and the darker the scale the older and the deadlier the beast.

## Habits

A tatsu spends two parts in three of its long life asleep, though never deeply, and rouses perhaps once a century to go raiding. It hoards on a vast scale, demanding tribute of the lands around its lair to leave them in peace and heaping the spoils in guarded caves, and the oldest and greatest of them, beasts of more than two thousand years, keep their young in the innermost chambers under the mother's eye. It is hugely intelligent and fond of riddles and guessing-games, and a clever tongue may win from a tatsu what no blade could.

## In Battle

The grown tatsu breathes a long cone of fire, and its very blood is corrosive, eating the edge from any weapon that wounds it. Its senses outreach a hawk's, and a reek of sulphur tends to warn of its coming. Worst of all is its gaze: any creature that meets a tatsu eye to eye is gripped by terror, and a weaker will is frozen outright, held fast until the dragon chooses to let go.
