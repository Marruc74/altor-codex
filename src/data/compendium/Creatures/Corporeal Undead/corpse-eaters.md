# Corpse Eaters

Corpse eaters are a strange kind of corporeal undead, not truly undead at all since they keep their living vigour, a thing between dead and living that must still eat, drink and breathe. They are intelligent folk infected by a corpse eater's bite.

## Appearance

Their bodies change slowly: the teeth and jaw become a predator's, the black nails sharp claws, the skin grey-white, cracked and foul-smelling, the hair grey and stringy, the eyes lifeless and yellow.

## Behaviour

Dull-witted scavengers who can live only on the corpses of intelligent folk, they haunt graveyards or the edges of settlements, killing some poor soul under cover of dark to devour, and otherwise shun all contact, for everyone fears and despises them.

## Abilities

Anyone wounded by a corpse eater's natural weapons may become one. They cannot be touched by poison, disease or sleep, nor by spells that work only on the undead.
