# Vampire

Vampires are ordinary people infected with vampirism, a mix of disease, curse and divine gift that drastically changes the body. They are higher undead and pass their condition on through their bite.

## Appearance

They look like beautiful, perfectly ordinary people, a little paler, with overgrown sharp fangs. They age one year in ten and cannot die naturally, and they cast no reflection.

## Behaviour

Discreet to a fault to keep their nature hidden, they shun mirrors and almost always wear gloves against silver. They gather in great cities, where the destitute are easy prey, hiding-places are many, and night-wandering is easily excused.

## Abilities

They must drink the blood of the living to survive, and can lull a victim into a dreamlike daze with a look. A vampire cannot be killed by ordinary means, only by sunlight, a stake through the heart in its coffin, a silver weapon to the heart, or silver dust in the blood; it takes half damage from common arms and double from silver.
