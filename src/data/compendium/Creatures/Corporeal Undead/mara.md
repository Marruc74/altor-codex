# Mara

A mara is an evil female undead of human origin, reckoned among the higher undead.

## Appearance

A gaunt, aged woman with knotted limbs, coal-black skin, long stringy hair and shining blue eyes, her black nails grown into short sharp claws and a hoarse, cackling laugh issuing from her toothless mouth.

## Behaviour

She works her mischief in two ways: most often she takes a sleeping owner's horse on a wild night-ride, leaving it lathered and spent by morning and sometimes ridden to death; or she sends nightmares to a chosen victim, who wakes unrested and shaken.

## Abilities

Her claws carry a venom that can turn another woman into a mara, who dies and rises again four days later unless her body is burned. Her dreadful laugh strikes terror, and she can wield neither magic nor magic items.
