# Mara


![Mara](/compendium/Creatures/Corporeal Undead/mara.png "Mara")
A mara is an evil female undead of human origin.

## Appearance

The mara takes the form of a gaunt, aged woman with knotted limbs, coal-black skin, long lank hair and glowing blue eyes. Her black nails have grown out into short, sharp claws, and from her toothless mouth comes a hoarse, cackling, croaking laugh.

## Behaviour

Maras work their mischief in two ways. The commoner is to take a horse out on a wild, nightly mare-ride while its owner sleeps, so that by morning the horse is sweated, lathered and utterly spent; driven on by the mara's will, it may be pushed past what it can endure, and sometimes never wakes again, dying of heart-failure. A mara can also send nightmares to sleeping men, choosing a victim and overcoming his will, so that he suffers violent nightmares all night, wakes wholly unrested, and is left fearful and shaken until he can sleep undisturbed.

## Abilities

The mara's claws are coated in a venom that can turn another woman into a mara if it pierces her body and overcomes her; the woman then dies and rises again as a mara some days later, unless her body is burned. Where the venom does not work this change it merely wounds, against women and men alike. Her power to strike terror is her dreadful laugh, which fills all who hear it with fear. A mara can never use magic or magical items.
