# Frost Wolf

Frost wolves dwell on the elemental plane of cold. Like all elemental creatures they have an intelligence of their own but lack the elementals' special powers, and being without bodily vigour they cannot be harmed by poison, sleep, fear or disease; when slain in our world they return to their plane.

## Appearance

The frost wolf looks like a gigantic, wholly white wolf.

## Behaviour

A predator that hunts with bite and breath. Like all elemental creatures it has a will of its own.

## Abilities

The frost wolf can blow a stream of dreadfully cold air from its nostrils a short way, against which armour gives no protection. Like all elemental creatures it is immune to poison, sleep, fear and disease, and returns to the plane of cold when its body is destroyed.
