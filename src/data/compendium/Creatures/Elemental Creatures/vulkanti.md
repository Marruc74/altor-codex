# Vulkanti

Vulkanti dwell on the elemental plane of heat. Like all elemental creatures they have an intelligence of their own but lack the elementals' special powers, and being without bodily vigour they cannot be harmed by poison, sleep, fear or disease; when slain in our world they return to their plane.

## Appearance

A vulkanti looks like a human-like creature made of glowing coals.

## Behaviour

It fights at close quarters with fist and kick, every blow searing. Like all elemental creatures it has a will of its own.

## Abilities

Each time a vulkanti strikes with a natural weapon it sears the victim with an added burst of heat, against which metal armour gives no protection. It takes no harm from fire or fire-spells, and like all elemental creatures it is immune to poison, sleep, fear and disease, returning to the plane of heat when its body is destroyed.
