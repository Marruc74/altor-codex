# Fire Horse

Fire horses dwell on the elemental plane of fire. Like all elemental creatures they have an intelligence of their own but lack the elementals' special powers, and being without bodily vigour they cannot be harmed by poison, sleep, fear or disease; when slain in our world they return to their plane.

## Appearance

The fire horse resembles a fire-red, noble steed whose very coat is made of flickering flames.

## Behaviour

A fire horse can be used as a mount by one who protects himself with the right spells against its burning coat. Like all elemental creatures it has a will of its own.

## Abilities

Swift beyond an ordinary horse, the fire horse strikes with hoof and bite. Like all elemental creatures it cannot be harmed by poison, sleep, fear or disease, and when its body is destroyed it returns at once to the plane of fire.
