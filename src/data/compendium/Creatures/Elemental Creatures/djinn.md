# Djinn

Djinn dwell on the elemental plane of air. Like all elemental creatures they have an intelligence of their own but lack the elementals' special powers, and being without bodily vigour they cannot be harmed by poison, sleep, fear or disease; when slain in our world they return to their plane.

## Appearance

The djinn appears as a small, smoke-filled tornado.

## Behaviour

Swift and elusive, a djinn fights by hurling its foes off their feet with sheer wind. Like all elemental creatures it has a will of its own.

## Abilities

A djinn can blow a creature over by overpowering it with the force of its winds. It cannot be harmed by ordinary weapons, and like all elemental creatures it is immune to poison, sleep, fear and disease, returning to the plane of air when its body is destroyed.
