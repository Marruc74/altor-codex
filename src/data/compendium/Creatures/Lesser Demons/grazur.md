# Grazur

The grazur are another of Inferno's lesser demon-folk, kin in their lot to the pazuzu of the shadow circle, set to torment the damned as overseers, torturers and gaolers.

## Appearance

The grazur are long, ash-coloured creatures that look like something between a reptile and a fly. They have the body of a crag-lizard but walk upright on two legs, and otherwise resemble the pazuzu.

## Behaviour

Like the pazuzu beside whom they dwell, the grazur of the dismal shadow circle are melancholy and feel the transience of all things. They seldom come to the human world unless sent or summoned.

## Abilities

A grazur commands a few demonic powers, and cannot reach the human world by its own strength, but must be sent by a mightier demon or called up by a demonologist. As with all demons, only its physical body is slain in our world, dissolving to dust as it returns to its own plane.
