# Grazur

The grazur are another of Inferno's lesser demon folk, the rank-and-file that serve as overseers and tormentors of the damned.

## Appearance

Tall, ash-grey creatures like a cross between reptile and fly, with the body of a rock-lizard but walking upright on two legs, otherwise much like the pazuzu.

## Behaviour

Sharing the gloom of the shadow circle, they are melancholy and weighed down by the transience of things.

## Abilities

A few demonic gifts each, and like all lesser demons they reach the mortal world only when sent by a greater demon or summoned by a demonologist.
