# Belmon

Belmons are a lesser demon folk of Inferno's chaos circle, the rank-and-file of that ever-shifting place.

## Appearance

Human-like, with half-transparent skin and white, dead eyes, their hair shimmering speckled in bright pastels. Living in the chaos circle, their bodies tend to lose all structure and run into amoeba-like blobs of jelly.

## Behaviour

Impulsive and easily confused, creative but quick to turn destructive and ruinous.

## Abilities

A few demonic gifts each, and like all lesser demons they reach the mortal world only when sent or summoned.
