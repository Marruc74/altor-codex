# Uzorak

Uzorak dwell in the Abyss, the great chasm that runs through all nine circles of Inferno and binds them together; they are among its lesser demon folk.

## Appearance

Vaguely human but taller and stronger, with rainbow-sheened black skin, nails grown into heavy claws and teeth into predator's fangs; their arms and legs have two joints instead of one, the legs folding backward.

## Behaviour

They live for power, won gladly through battle, assassination and treachery.

## Abilities

A few demonic gifts each, and like all lesser demons they reach the mortal world only when sent or summoned.
