# Uzorak

In the Abyss, the great rift that runs through all nine circles of Inferno and joins them together, live the uzorak, among the lowest of its demon-folk, set to torment the damned.

## Appearance

Vaguely human-like but taller and heavier, with rainbow-shimmering black skin. Their nails have grown into powerful claws and their teeth into sharp predator's fangs. Their arms and legs have two joints instead of one, and both joints of the legs fold backward.

## Behaviour

Uzorak live to gain ever more power, gladly through battle, assassination and treachery.

## Abilities

An uzorak commands a few demonic powers, and cannot reach the human world by its own strength, but must be sent by a mightier demon or called up by a demonologist. As with all demons, only its physical body is slain in our world, dissolving to dust as it returns to its own plane.
