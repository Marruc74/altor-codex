# Desert Lord

The desert lord is the elemental lord of heat.

## Appearance

A very powerful giant with vivid red skin and short black hair, often wielding an enormous two-handed sword.

## Abilities

Immune to all fire but taking double damage from cold, and unharmed by ordinary weapons, it can set all water within a hundred metres boiling, burn a creature by touch, heat the air around it past bearing, and heat a metal weapon until it cuts through any metal but mithril. It destroys cold-elementals and can summon heat elementals.
