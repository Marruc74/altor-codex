# Desert Lord

A desert lord is the elemental lord of heat, one of the very powerful elemental beings that come and go between the planes and the human world as they please, bent on spreading heat through every world. Some desert lords have settled permanently on Altor and contend with the ice lords for mastery of the world. Like all its kind it appears almost always alone, attended by lesser heat-elementals and elemental creatures.

## Appearance

A desert lord looks like a very powerful giant with bright red skin and short black hair, and often wields an enormous two-handed sword.

## Behaviour

Like all elemental lords it is not by nature aggressive, but its enormous powers and indifference to other living things make it dangerous; it may be set to guard something or sent on reconnaissance, and the endless clash of the elements shows itself in catastrophes such as heatwaves and droughts.

## Abilities

Like all elemental lords, a desert lord wields great magic, is immune to all fire, cannot be harmed by ordinary weapons, which it dulls, and can summon or dismiss heat-elementals and elemental creatures. It can destroy cold-creatures such as glacials, frost wolves and ice lords by overpowering their will. It can bring all water within a wide radius to the boil, sear warm-blooded creatures at a touch, against which metal armour gives no help, and heat the air around it so fiercely that none can draw near. It can wield metal weapons heated so fiercely they cut through any metal but mithril, and takes double harm from all cold.
