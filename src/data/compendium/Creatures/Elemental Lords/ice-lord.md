# Ice Lord

An ice lord is the elemental lord of cold, one of the very powerful elemental beings that come and go between the planes and the human world as they please, bent on spreading cold through every world. Some ice lords have settled permanently on Altor and contend with the desert lords for mastery of the world. Like all its kind it appears almost always alone, attended by lesser cold-elementals and elemental creatures.

## Appearance

An ice lord always takes the form of a giant with a great white beard full of rime-frost, frosty eyebrows, misting breath and warm furs, often bearing an enormous wooden club over the shoulder.

## Behaviour

Like all elemental lords it is not by nature aggressive, but its enormous powers and indifference to other living things make it dangerous; it may be set to guard something or sent on reconnaissance, and the endless clash of the elements shows itself in catastrophes such as blizzards and unnatural frosts.

## Abilities

Like all elemental lords, an ice lord wields great magic, is immune to its own element, cannot be harmed by ordinary weapons, which it dulls, and can summon or dismiss cold-elementals and elemental creatures. It can destroy fire- and heat-creatures such as salamanders, therms, fire horses, vulkanti, magmani and desert lords by overpowering their will. It can freeze all water within a wide radius, sear warm-blooded creatures with cold at a touch, against which metal armour gives no help, and chill the air around it so bitterly that none can draw near. It takes no harm from cold, but double harm from all fire.
