# Ice Lord

The ice lord is the elemental lord of cold.

## Appearance

Always a giant with a great rime-filled white beard, frosted brows and misting breath, wrapped in warm furs, often with a huge wooden club over one shoulder.

## Abilities

Immune to cold but taking double damage from fire, and unharmed by ordinary weapons, it can freeze all water within a hundred metres, inflict cold injury by touch, and chill the air around it so fiercely that none can approach. It destroys fire- and heat-elementals and can summon cold elementals.
