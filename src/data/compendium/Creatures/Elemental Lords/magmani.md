# Magmani

The magmani is the elemental lord of fire.

## Appearance

A huge, powerful human with glowing red skin and short black hair wreathed in little flames, or a vast ball of fire three metres across with a core of lava and two reaching arms.

## Abilities

Immune to its own element and unharmed by ordinary weapons, it can set alight anything flammable within twenty-five metres, even incoming arrows, heat a metal weapon until it shears through any metal but mithril, and in its ball-form engulf a victim in flame. It can destroy water- and cold-elementals and summon fire elementals.
