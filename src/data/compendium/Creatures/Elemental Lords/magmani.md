# Magmani

The magmani is the elemental lord of fire, one of the very powerful elemental beings that come and go between the planes and the human world as they please, bent on spreading fire through every world. Like all its kind it appears almost always alone, attended by lesser fire-elementals and elemental creatures.

## Appearance

A magmani takes the form of a gigantic, very powerful human with glowing red skin and short black hair, the whole body wreathed in small flames. It can also take the form of an enormous ball of fire with a core of lava, two arms reaching out from the body.

## Behaviour

Like all elemental lords it is not by nature aggressive, but its enormous powers and indifference to other living things make it dangerous to be near; it may be set to guard a portal or a perilous object, or sent on reconnaissance, and the clash of the elements shows itself in great catastrophes such as volcanic eruptions.

## Abilities

Like all elemental lords, the magmani wields great magic, is immune to its own element of fire, cannot be harmed by ordinary weapons, which it dulls, and can summon or dismiss fire-elementals and elemental creatures. It can set alight anything burnable nearby, even arrows shot at it, and can destroy water- and cold-creatures by overpowering their will. It can wield metal weapons heated so fiercely they cut through any metal but mithril, and in its ball-form can engulf a victim in flames that no armour wards off.
