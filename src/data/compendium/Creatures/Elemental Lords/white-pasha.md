# White Pasha

A white pasha is the elemental lord of light, one of the very powerful elemental beings that come and go between the planes and the human world as they please, bent on spreading light through every world. Like all its kind it appears almost always alone, attended by lesser light-elementals and elemental creatures.

## Appearance

The white pasha can take the form of a great, mighty magician in light, airy silken robes and a great light-shimmering turban, radiating a strong glow. In its true form the white pasha appears as a great globe of very clear, shining light.

## Behaviour

Like all elemental lords it is not by nature aggressive, but its enormous powers and indifference to other living things make it dangerous; it may be set to guard something or sent on reconnaissance, and the endless clash of the elements shows itself only seldom.

## Abilities

Like all elemental lords, a white pasha wields great magic, is immune to its own element, cannot be harmed by ordinary weapons, which it dulls, and can summon or dismiss light-elementals and elemental creatures. It can destroy an umbra, a shadow beast or a black sultan by overpowering its will. It can light a wide area as bright as day, and blind every creature that looks upon it, the more surely those who see in the dark, leaving them as though blind for a while; it is wholly immune to all fire- and light-attacks, and can hurl bolts of lightning at foes that no armour wards off.
