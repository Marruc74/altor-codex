# White Pasha

The white pasha is the elemental lord of light.

## Appearance

A great, mighty mage in airy white silks and a shining turban, radiating brilliant light, or in its true form a globe of intensely shining light.

## Abilities

Immune to its own element and unharmed by ordinary weapons, it can light a wide area as bright as day, blind those who look upon it, and hurl bolts of lightning that no armour turns; it destroys dark-elementals and is wholly immune to fire and light. It can summon light elementals.
