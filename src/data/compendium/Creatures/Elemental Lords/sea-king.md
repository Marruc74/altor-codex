# Sea King

The sea king is the elemental lord of water.

## Appearance

An enormous human clad in seaweed with bluish skin, fighting with great tridents, or a five-metre wave with two reaching arms.

## Abilities

Immune to his own element and unharmed by ordinary weapons, he commands all water within three hundred metres, raising fierce currents, and can loose a concentrated jet of water that knocks a target down; in wave-form he sweeps away earth-elementals. He can summon water elementals.
