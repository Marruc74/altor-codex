# Cyclone

The cyclone is the elemental lord of air, one of the eight mighty elemental lords who come and go between the planes as they please, each bent on spreading its own element through the world.

## Appearance

Either a giant funnel of whirling wind or an enormous human with a body of cloud, its outline diffuse and its eyes two bright pale-blue points; it wears no armour and bears no weapon.

## Abilities

Like all elemental lords it is immune to its own element, cannot be harmed by ordinary weapons, which it blunts, and can summon and dismiss air elementals. It commands every wind within three hundred metres, raising gales that flatten the unwary and render missiles all but useless, hurls concentrated darts of wind, and can destroy fire-elementals by force of will.
