# Matter Queen

The matter queen is the elemental queen of earth.

## Appearance

A very beautiful woman who seems hewn from chalk-white marble or coal-black basalt; she has no true form.

## Abilities

Immune to her own element and unharmed by ordinary weapons, she can raise earthquakes around her and tear open or seal chasms in the most solid bedrock, and can wear down or destroy water-elementals. She can summon earth elementals.
