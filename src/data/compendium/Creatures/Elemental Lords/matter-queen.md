# Matter Queen

The matter queens are the elemental rulers of earth, very powerful elemental beings that come and go between the planes and the human world as they please, bent on spreading their element through every world. Like all their kind they appear almost always alone, attended by lesser earth-elementals and elemental creatures.

## Appearance

Matter queens look like very beautiful women hewn from chalk-white marble or coal-black basalt. They have no true form.

## Behaviour

Like all elemental lords they are not by nature aggressive, but their enormous powers and indifference to other living things make them dangerous; they may be set to guard something or sent on reconnaissance, and the clash of the elements shows itself in catastrophes such as earthquakes.

## Abilities

Like all elemental lords, a matter queen wields great magic, is immune to her own element of earth, cannot be harmed by ordinary weapons, which she dulls, and can summon or dismiss earth-elementals and elemental creatures. She can raise earthquakes around her and open and close chasms even in the most solid bedrock, and can destroy or grind down water-creatures by overpowering their will.
