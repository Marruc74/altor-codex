# Black Sultan

A black sultan is the elemental lord of darkness, one of the very powerful elemental beings that come and go between the planes and the human world as they please, bent on spreading darkness through every world. Like all its kind it appears almost always alone, attended by lesser dark-elementals and elemental creatures.

## Appearance

A black sultan can take the form of a vast, coal-black shadow in the shape of a human, then clad in a long black robe and a great coal-black turban, fighting with a coal-black scimitar. In its true form the black sultan is a shapeless figure of compact darkness.

## Behaviour

Like all elemental lords it is not by nature aggressive, but its enormous powers and indifference to other living things make it dangerous; it may be set to guard something or sent on reconnaissance, and the endless clash of the elements shows itself only seldom.

## Abilities

Like all elemental lords, a black sultan wields great magic, is immune to its own element, cannot be harmed by ordinary weapons, which it dulls, and can summon or dismiss dark-elementals and elemental creatures. It cannot be sensed in the dark by ordinary wariness, only made out by a careful, searching eye, and it can destroy a luminal, a light bird or a white pasha by overpowering its will. It can smother all light within a wide globe, where light-sources still burn but give off no light and dark-sight fails, and it can wrap itself around a living creature to strike it with terror. It takes double harm from all fire- and light-attacks.
