# Black Sultan

The black sultan is the elemental lord of darkness.

## Appearance

A vast, coal-black man-shaped shadow in a long black robe and great black turban, wielding a black curved sabre, or in its true form a shapeless mass of compact darkness.

## Abilities

Immune to its own element and unharmed by ordinary weapons, it cannot be sensed in the dark by instinct alone, can smother all light within a globe around it, and can strike a creature it enfolds with terror; it destroys light-elementals but takes double damage from fire and light. It can summon dark elementals.
