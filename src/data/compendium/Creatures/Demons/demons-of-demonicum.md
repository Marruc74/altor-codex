# Demons of Demonicum

Beyond Inferno stretch the wider worlds of Demonicum, the outer wall of the Multiverse, where a war without end is fought against the Chaos that laps at creation's edge. The demons here were shaped by the higher powers to hold that wall, and were walled in along with the Chaos they fight, lest they grow mighty enough to turn inward on their makers. They reach Altor far more rarely than the demons of Inferno, and then only at the call of a mighty demonologist.

## The Demon Courts

Where Inferno counts its ranks in circles and princes, the wider worlds keep a different ladder of five degrees. Highest are the Guardians, the first-created generals of the war against Chaos, whose power rivals the higher powers themselves and cannot be measured or compelled. Below them stand the paschas, the demon-warlords who are the mightiest demons ever to visit Altor and the source of most of its demon-legends. Then come the war demons, the officers who command the hordes and are the demons most often met with; beneath them the thralls, the foot-soldiers; and lowest of all the demonettes, weak and often formless, hand-high, used as beasts and drudges.

## The Guardians and Their Worlds

Three Guardians tower over the rest. Nehcrom, the Horned One, rules Caboon from the black cliff Khla, built into an endless fortress, and drives his war-hosts outward against Chaos and inward against his rivals; the azoth are his elite warriors. Bemoth, Prince of Beasts, keeps the wild forests of Leithicor and hunts Chaos through the worlds with wild hordes that sweep from sky and earth, among them the winged karnack and the hunting nerocq. Caliban, the Eternal Wind, is king of madness in the shifting fortress Methucalem, served by the trickster Darubah, the humble feot, and the towering pascha Khurún.

## The Farther Worlds

Past the Guardians' realms lie stranger places. Gehenna is an airless fire-world whose demons are living flame, shaped like dragons and titans, and its burning demon-fire flares in pain the moment it touches our air. Niflhem is five frozen worlds wheeling about a dead star, home to the beautiful, treacherous ice demons. And in Libricum the hooded knowledge demons keep vast libraries of all that the Multiverse knows, selling their learning for a price and never freely.
