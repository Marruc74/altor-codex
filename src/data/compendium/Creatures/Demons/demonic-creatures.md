# Demonic Creatures

Not every horror with a demon's mark on it is a demon. Below the true demons of Inferno and the wider worlds come the demonic creatures: the beasts, mounts, servants and monsters that demons breed, possess, bargain with, or leave in their wake. Some are creatures of Chaos that drift into our world by mischance, some are shaped of demon-stuff or born in Inferno's circles, and a few belong to Altor itself, twisted into something worse.

## What Sets Them Apart

A true demon holds a station in the Demon Courts or on Inferno's ladder, and its slain body only crumbles and reforms on its home plane. The demonic creatures keep no such rank. A black unicorn or a vampire butterfly can be killed for good like any beast of our world; a servant is a living person who has struck a bargain; a black avenger is an undead worn like armour by the demon noble that possesses it. What binds them is a demonic nature or a demonic master, not a place in the infernal order.

## Beasts, Servants and Omens

They come in every guise. The cold beast is an ice-monster of Inferno's second circle, part of it built from demon-ice, while the quetzal is a witless flying mount bred in its thousands to carry the legions of the Abyss to their deaths. The death cherub is the soul of a damned child, set to herd the newly dead down to the ninth circle; the death angel is the deadliest tool the Chaos princes wield in the human world, a messenger and reaper both. And the servant is no monster at all to look upon, only a schemer who has sold himself to a demon for power, and whose nearness alone is enough to sour milk and curdle cream.
