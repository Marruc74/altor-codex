# Demons

A demon is a creature of Chaos, or a thing set to fight it, and the old tales cannot agree which. What every demon shares is a home beyond the dimension walls, in Demonicum, the ring of frontier worlds that borders raw Chaos at the very edge of creation. From there they are called or sent into Altor one at a time, and there alone can they truly be killed.

## What Binds Them

Demons differ from one another more than any mortal race does, in shape, in the number of their limbs, in size and in power, yet a few things hold true of nearly all of them. They are proud, selfish and faithless, keeping no bargain unless a higher power or a cosmic law compels it, and they hunger without end for power, growing stronger and taking on new gifts the longer they live and the higher they climb. A demon cannot cross to our world under its own strength until it has been sent here once, whether summoned by a demonologist or dispatched by a mightier demon; after that first crossing the road lies open to it. Killing one buys only a reprieve, for the body dies and crumbles to dust while the demon itself wakes again on its home plane, to be destroyed for good only there.

## Two Realms

Altor's demon-lore draws on two realms above all others. Nearest to our world lies Inferno, a cellar-dimension of nine circles ruled by warring demon-princes, and it is from Inferno that most summoned demons come. Beyond it lie the wider worlds of Demonicum, the war-realms of the three Guardians and the strange elemental worlds past them, whose demons reach Altor more rarely and only at the call of a mighty demonologist. The two keep different orders and rank their kind by different names, which is why the demons of one seldom resemble the demons of the other.

## Demons in Altor

Now and then a demon walks in Ereb Altor, and no two tales of it are alike. Echram Schroedel lies chained in a pauper's grave beneath Nohstril, counting a debt of blood he means to collect. Urbaug the Insane, a fallen prince set to humble labour, blunders across the land setting right his own ruin. A fire demon torn screaming out of Gehenna wants nothing but to burn its way home. Each is a story of its own, and none of them is a simple monster.
