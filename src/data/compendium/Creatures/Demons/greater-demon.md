# Greater Demon

Greater demons are those that have survived long enough to climb some way up Inferno's ranks, serving as chancellors, high officers, judges or mine-bosses. They stand a clear step above the lesser demons.

## Appearance

It varies, but commonly black skin and red or yellow eyes with almond-shaped black pupils; demons rarely have any body hair, and their shape, size and number of limbs vary endlessly, limited only by imagination.

## Behaviour

It depends on the demon's goal and office. Not always evil, but almost always selfish in some way; even a humble, obliging servant is so only because it sees greater advantage to come.

## Abilities

Several demonic gifts each. They cannot reach the mortal world unaided, but once sent there they can use the same path again at will.
