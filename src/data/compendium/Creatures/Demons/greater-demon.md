# Greater Demon

The greater demons are those that have survived long enough to climb a fair way up the ranks of Inferno. A demon of this standing may serve as a chancellor, a high officer, a judge, or the overseer of some mine. Demons are a kind of Chaos creature, and it is not certain whether they all share one origin; some seem to come from planes teeming with demon-kinds, others from planes where they are the only being of their sort.

## Appearance

It varies, but usually black skin and red or yellow eyes with almond-shaped black pupils. Demons rarely have any body-hair. Body-shape, the number of limbs, and size vary enormously, and only imagination sets the bounds.

## Behaviour

It varies with the demon's goal and office. Not necessarily evil, but almost always selfish in one way or another; even when a demon plays the humble, modest servant, it does so only because it knows it can win greater advantages later. Like all demons it grows in strength over its endless life, gaining more natural weapons and powers the more power it holds.

## Abilities

A greater demon commands several demonic powers, drawn from a vast and varied store, fire-breath, corrosive blood, life-draining, invisibility, regeneration and many more. It cannot travel to the human world by its own strength, but must be sent by a mightier demon or called up by a demonologist, though once sent it can use the same road again of its own accord. A demon's body may be slain in any world of the Multiverse, but only its physical form dies and dissolves to dust; it returns to its own plane and takes a new body there, and can be destroyed forever only on its home plane.
