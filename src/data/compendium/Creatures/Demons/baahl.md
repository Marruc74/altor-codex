# Baahl

The baahl are the war-race of the Abyss, taller and stronger than men, their black skin sheened with a faint rainbow like oil on water. Their limbs are double-jointed and their knees bend backward, which lends them a strange, lurching speed and a reach no human fighter expects. They live only for power, and reckon it won three ways: in open battle, in assassination, and in treachery against their own.

Baahl gather in clans and in armies, and those armies run from the thousands into the millions, for the race breeds for war and spends its numbers freely. A baahl rises by killing those above it, so even their own ranks are a battlefield, and an alliance among them lasts exactly as long as it serves the stronger party.
