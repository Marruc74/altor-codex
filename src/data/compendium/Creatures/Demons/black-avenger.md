# Black Avenger

![Black Avenger](/compendium/Creatures/Demons/black-avenger-2.jpg "Black Avenger")
![Black Avenger](/compendium/Creatures/Demons/black-avenger-3.jpg "Black Avenger")


![Black Avenger](/compendium/Creatures/Demons/black-avenger.jpg "Black Avenger")
A black avenger is the most dangerous thing one could ever meet in the human world, the death angel perhaps excepted. A black avenger is what results when a very powerful undead, such as a death wraith, is possessed by a demon noble. They always have a single special task, such as to guard an object or to kill a particular person.

## Appearance

A black avenger has the form of a knight two and a half metres tall, in a coal-black, gleaming full harness with a black cloak over the shoulders. Behind the eye-slits of the close helm shows only a red glow of light. It is always armed with a demonic double-axe that can cut through any material.

## Behaviour

The black avengers have one very definite task and do nothing besides. Set to guard an object, they guard it, and should it be stolen they pursue the thief, kill him and his companions, and return the goods to their rightful place. In some cases this is enough to free the black avenger from its charge; in others it remains.

## Abilities

A black avenger never needs to eat or drink. It never grows bored or weary. It can travel underwater or through the air at the same speed as on land; in short, it goes everywhere, hacking its way straight through physical barriers such as walls.
