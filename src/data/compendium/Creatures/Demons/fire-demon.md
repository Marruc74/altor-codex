# Fire Demon

The fire demons come from Gehenna, the airless fire-world of Demonicum, and they are creatures of living flame in the shape of dragons and titans.

## Appearance

Dragon- or titan-like, wreathed and bodied in fire, so bright they are painful to look upon.

## Behaviour

Called into the air of our world, they flare up in pain at the touch of it and turn at once upon the magician who summoned them. They want only to burn their way back home.

## Abilities

A fire demon's very touch sears, and its burning body kindles whatever it meets; it breathes and hurls the demon-fire of Gehenna. Water and cold harm it doubly, and the wise summoner keeps both close at hand before ever he calls one up.
