# Krazzt

![Krazzt](/compendium/Creatures/Demons/krazzt.jpg "Krazzt")

Krazzt are enormous, eyeless praying-mantises bound up in thick coils of spider-web, used as war-mounts in the circles of Inferno. The belmôns ride them, spurs driven into their flanks, and steer them not by rein or word but by hunger and pain, for a krazzt is far too dull and too blind to be led any other way.

Sightless and half-starved by design, a krazzt knows only the goad at its side and the prey ahead of it. Where the belmôns go to war it carries them in swarms, a mount as expendable as the demons that ride it and every bit as pitiless.
