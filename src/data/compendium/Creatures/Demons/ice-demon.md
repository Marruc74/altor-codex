# Ice Demon

The ice demons dwell in Niflhem, the five frozen worlds that wheel about a dead star, and they are the cold mirror of the fire demons of Gehenna.

## Appearance

A beautiful human or elf wrought all of ice and sheathed in frost, a fair seeming over a deadly thing.

## Behaviour

Treacherous and patient. An ice demon lures a victim out into the wilds and there steals the warmth from his body, little by little, until he is dead.

## Abilities

Its touch freezes flesh, and it charms and hypnotises with an unearthly beauty. Heat and fire harm it doubly, and burn away the lovely shape to show the thing beneath.
