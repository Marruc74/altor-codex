# Echram Schroedel

A war-demon out of the sixth circle of Inferno, the Circle of Wars, whose true name is Z'crohederc and who hates everything that Altor is. For a century his soul has lain chained in a pauper's grave beneath Nohstril, dismembered and betrayed, counting a debt of blood he means one day to collect.

## The Betrayal

Around a hundred years ago, when Nohstril was newly made the capital of Erebos, the merchant house Festglade hired Echram to be rid of a rival. In a single night he slaughtered the twelve cyclops mercenaries who guarded the house of the Bullhorn line at Gringul - a killing historians still argue was impossible. His price was a human sacrifice a day. Festglade would not pay it. Instead they had him killed, cut his body to pieces, sealed his soul inside a grave, and sold his great two-handed sword to a magician in Krilloan. Chained there, unable to return to his own dimension, he managed only to scratch a verse onto his grave-lid - the rite by which he might be woken again.

## What He Is Owed

Echram has reckoned the debt. Festglade owes him better than thirty-six thousand offerings, before interest, and he intends to take payment the moment he is loosed: to go berserk and kill until something kills him. He bears no grudge against whoever frees him - that one he leaves be - but everything else in his path he would lay waste. To return in full he needs three things: his sword for the fighting, a living body to wear, and a staff to channel his magic.

## Hearts and Eyes

Two horrors mark the work of raising him. Three hearts are fed to his soul, so that he can be slain three times before he truly dies - each death withers one heart away. And seven eyes are given him for the focus of his mind, through which he sees all things wherever he is, and can throw his magic through any one of them. He wears a borrowed body like a glove, swelling it with a measure of his own strength while the flesh greys and the eyes go red. For all his power he has one weakness: he cannot swim, and deep enough water will drown the body he wears.
