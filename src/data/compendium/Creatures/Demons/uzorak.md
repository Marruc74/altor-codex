# Uzorak


![Uzorak](/compendium/Creatures/Demons/uzorak.jpg "Uzorak")
In the Abyss, the great rift that runs through all nine circles of Inferno and joins them together, live the uzorak, among the lowest of its demon-folk, set to torment the damned. The Demon Prince adventure names this same race the **baahl**, the war-people of the Abyss who march in their millions under Prince Antraxhus.

## Appearance

Vaguely human-like but taller and heavier, with rainbow-shimmering black skin like oil on water. Their nails have grown into powerful claws and their teeth into sharp predator's fangs. Their arms and legs have two joints instead of one, and both joints of the legs fold backward, which lends them a strange lurching speed and a reach no human fighter expects.

## Behaviour

Uzorak live to gain ever more power, gladly through battle, assassination and treachery. They gather in clans and in armies, and those armies run from the thousands into the millions, for the race breeds for war and spends its numbers freely. One rises by killing those above it, so even their own ranks are a battlefield, and an alliance among them lasts only as long as it serves the stronger party. They keep no families and quarter themselves in fortress-holds called khraal.

## Abilities

An uzorak commands a few demonic powers, and cannot reach the human world by its own strength, but must be sent by a mightier demon or called up by a demonologist. As with all demons, only its physical body is slain in our world, dissolving to dust as it returns to its own plane. They speak their own tongue, baahl, though most also know some Erebish language.
