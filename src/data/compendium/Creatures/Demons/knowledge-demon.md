# Knowledge Demon

The knowledge demons keep the vast libraries of Libricum, the world of Demonicum where all that the Multiverse knows is gathered and set down.

## Appearance

A robed, hooded humanoid whose face is never seen, speaking ever in a soft and level voice.

## Behaviour

It sees other beings as insects, and sells its knowledge for a price and never freely, while it labours without end to gather still more lore to carry home to Libricum.

## Abilities

A master of many arts and a keeper of deep learning, the knowledge demon perceives much at a distance and speaks every tongue ever uttered. Like all its kind it can reach out to punish one who cheats a bargain made with it.
