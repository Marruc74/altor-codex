# Diabolin

Diabolins are the least of demons, small horned imps of the demon-princes' owned worlds, summoned for short, petty service and gone again within hours. A witch in league with a demon-prince can raise a handful of them from black poodles by a brief rite, setting the little demons loose to fetch, harry or kill for a while before the binding lapses and they vanish back where they came from.

On their own a diabolin is a minor thing, more nuisance than terror, but they come in numbers and at no great cost to the one who calls them, which is what makes them useful to a coven. They serve only as long as the rite holds, and have no loyalty beyond it.
