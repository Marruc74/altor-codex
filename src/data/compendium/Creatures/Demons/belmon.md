# Belmon


![Belmon](/compendium/Creatures/Demons/belmon.jpg "Belmon")
In the Chaos circle of Inferno live the belmôns, among the lowest of its demon-folk, set like all lesser demons to torment the damned souls as overseers, torturers and gaolers.

## Appearance

A human-like kind with half-transparent skin and white, dead eyes, their hair shimmering speckled in clear pastel shades. Because they live in the Chaos circle their bodies tend to lose their structure and run into amoeba-like lumps of jelly.

## Behaviour

Belmôns are impulsive and easily confused. They are creative, but just as readily turn destructive and ruinous.

## Abilities

A belmôn commands a few demonic powers, and cannot reach the human world by its own strength, but must be sent by a mightier demon or called up by a demonologist. As with all demons, only its physical body is slain in our world, dissolving to dust as it returns to its own plane.
