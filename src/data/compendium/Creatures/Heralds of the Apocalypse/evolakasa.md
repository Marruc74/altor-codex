# Evolakasa

Evolakasa goes before Pestilence, one of the four forerunners that herald the Riders of the world's end. The Riders are the end itself and past any summoning, but their forerunners are not, and a bold enough demonologist can drag one across into our world for a while.

## Appearance

The shape of a naked woman with lank green hair sharper than iron. Her skin never holds still: mostly pale, but at least a third of it always blistered, scabbed and weeping with open sores. A cloud of flies hangs about her, and she stinks of pus and rotting meat. She speaks almost never, and then only in hoarse, coughing sounds.

## Nature

Everything about her is contagion. She is called only by those willing to loose sickness on a whole country, and she answers no one for long.

## Abilities

She brings plague and epidemic with her, rots flesh, and withers limbs and tongues. She breathes out clouds of foul air, and at a distance she flings the iron spikes of her hair like thrown knives.
