# Kalembri

Kalembri is the forerunner of War, and of the four it is the loudest in its coming. The Riders themselves end the world and cannot be called; the demon that runs ahead of each can be, by a demonologist with the strength and the stomach for it.

## Appearance

A floating head, tusked, with yellow eyes and red skin. Its hair is ten spiralled tentacles, and each of them most often grips a curved saber. A strong reek of blood hangs about it, and its voice is like rolling thunder.

## Nature

It is slaughter looking for somewhere to happen. It stirs up bloodshed wherever it stands, and it gives itself to a summoner grudgingly and never for long.

## Abilities

It fills every fighter near it with battle-madness and bloodlust, and breaks weaker nerve with a single war-cry. Its hide goes hard as iron, its spilled blood burns like acid where it falls, and its ten sabers come at a foe from every side at once.
