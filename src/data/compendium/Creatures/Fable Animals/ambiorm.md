# Ambiorm

The ambiorm is a rightly feared creature: a great serpent that bears, where its tail ought to be, a second head. What mad turn of evolution or deranged wizard's experiment first produced this monster, no one knows.

## Appearance

Large and heavy, an ambiorm easily grows over three metres long, with a head at each end of the body and luminous, pale-yellow eyes. It is a garish green in colour, and down the length of its back runs a red zigzag pattern.

## Behaviour

By nature an aggressive and fairly intelligent predator, it lives by preference on small and middling mammals, favouring easier prey than humans, with ducks and ratmen its especial delight, though it is quick to attack a human too given the chance. An ambiorm can be made half-tame, but only with great effort, and it will turn on even the one who tamed it the moment it sees an opening. These unnatural creatures of legend keep to dark underground places where they can hide from the hated sunlight.

## Abilities

Cut an ambiorm in two and new heads grow from the severed ends within about a week; any wound not dealt by fire or acid it regenerates steadily. Each bite that pierces armour carries a venom, and though it has two brains it has only a single mind, so that severing one head halves its spirit.
