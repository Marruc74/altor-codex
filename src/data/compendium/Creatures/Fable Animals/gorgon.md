# Gorgon

It is said that only three gorgons exist. Once they were beautiful women, but now they are dreadful monsters. Two of the gorgons are immortal, named Stheno and Euryale, while the third, Medusa, can be slain.

## Appearance

The gorgons have boar's tusks, hands of bronze and golden wings. Their hair is made of small venomous serpents, and their faces are so frightful that whoever looks upon one is turned to stone.

## Behaviour

The gorgons dwell alone in exquisitely furnished palaces filled with the bloody remains of their victims. They live on intelligent creatures, and gather treasure into great heaps, though it is very rare for them to travel more than a few dozen kilometres from their dwelling.

## Abilities

Gorgons can be harmed only by magical weapons. When an immortal gorgon's strength is spent she collapses into a torpor, only to wake again after a week; how to kill them for good remains a mystery. In a fight with a gorgon one must each moment master one's will to avoid meeting her face, for she always tries to coax people into looking at her with sweet words, and anyone within earshot who succumbs is drawn to look upon her face and is petrified if he stands near enough to make out her features, some fifty metres for a human.
