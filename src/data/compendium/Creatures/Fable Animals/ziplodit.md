# Ziplodit

![Ziplodit](/compendium/Creatures/Fable Animals/ziplodit.jpg "Ziplodit")
![Ziplodit](/compendium/Creatures/Fable Animals/ziplodit-2.jpg "Ziplodit")
The ziploditer are the native beasts of Magilre, a family of creatures that come in several kinds, set apart by their size and their temper. Some are the curse of the farmer and some the friend of the carter, but all of them belong to that flat and watery land as nothing else does.

## The Swamp Ziplodit

The giant of the family, the swamp ziplodit grows so vast that at around two hundred years it can no longer bear its own weight and collapses. Its flesh is foul to the tongue, so not even its death is much use to anyone.

## The Draft Ziplodit

Slow but immensely strong, the draft ziplodit is a gentle leaf-eater and the ideal beast of burden. It is these that haul the great wagon-trains along the roads of Magilre, plodding the length of the country under their loads.

## The Steppe Ziplodit

Smallest of the true ziploditer and the swiftest, the steppe ziplodit is a sharp-sensed grass-eater that gathers into herds to raid the maize fields, and is loathed by every farmer whose crop it strips.
