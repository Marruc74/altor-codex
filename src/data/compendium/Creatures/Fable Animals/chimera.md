# Chimera


![Chimera](/compendium/Creatures/Fable Animals/chimera.jpg "Chimera")
In one of the most turbulent of the Chaos realms, a bored Chaos lord created the chimeras. Unhappily he grew bored of them very quickly and dispatched them to other dimensions without much further thought, which is why chimeras are found in most of the dimensions of the Multiverse.

## Appearance

The chimera looks like a great lion with a lion's head in front, a goat's head growing from its back, a goat's hindquarters, and a venomous serpent for a tail. It is in the goat's head that the chimera's brain sits, and there too its mind.

## Behaviour

As the perverse creature of Chaos it is, the chimera has no rational instincts, but is filled with hate. As a predator it is voracious and quarrelsome. It is usually found in mountainous country, where it makes its lair in a deep cave or cleft of rock.

## Abilities

Because the chimera has three pairs of eyes it is very watchful, able to listen and sense danger even while it fights, which makes it extremely hard to surprise. Furthermore it can always bring its serpent-bite to bear on a foe within reach in the same moment that it uses one of its other natural weapons, and the venom of that bite is potent indeed.
