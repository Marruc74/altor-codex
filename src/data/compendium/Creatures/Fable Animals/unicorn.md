# Unicorn


![Unicorn](/compendium/Creatures/Fable Animals/unicorn-2.jpg "Unicorn")
![Unicorn](/compendium/Creatures/Fable Animals/unicorn.jpg "Unicorn")
The unicorn is probably the most mythical of all the legendary beasts. White as snow and pure as angels, they are the very symbol of goodness, and they are feared as well for their power to fight against evil.

## Appearance

A unicorn looks like a tall white stallion, with a spiralled golden horn set in the middle of its brow. The hooves are faintly golden too, and the teeth white and perfect.

## Behaviour

Unicorns are tremendously proud and very shy. Solitary, they avoid coming near humans and other intelligent creatures. Violent beings, and those made by powers that are enemies of Mother Earth, are the unicorn's mortal foes, and it attacks them with a death-defying fury should they come near. Pursued by friendly creatures it flees as long as it can, and only when cornered does it attack. Only if a good-hearted, pure and innocent maiden approaches is there a chance it will let her near, and it may then even let itself be charmed by her, remaining her servant and steed for as long as she stays pure and good-hearted and lets no man come close to her.

## Abilities

Unicorns are immune to all poisons. If a unicorn touches a friendly creature with its horn the touch heals its wounds, and it senses telepathically every creature ill-disposed toward it or its mistress. Unicorns are intelligent and speak mind to mind with their friends across as much as a hundred metres.
