# Rock Lizard

![Rock Lizard](/compendium/Creatures/Fable Animals/rock-lizard.jpg "Rock Lizard")
The rock lizard is a large lizard of the mountains and high country, easily known by the heavy store of fat it carries in its tail. That tail is reckoned a delicacy, which has not been kind to the creature.

## Appearance

A stout, broad-bodied lizard, its most marked feature is the thick, fat-laden tail. On each hind leg it bears a spur tipped with a strong venom.

## Behaviour

The rock lizard hunts from ambush, lying in wait by a waterhole and seizing careless prey in its powerful jaws. When a larger animal threatens it, it either digs itself in beneath a stone or curls up tight into a ball.

## Abilities

Its real defence lies in the venomed spurs on its hind legs, whose poison numbs and deadens whatever flesh it pierces. Anything that lays a hand on the lizard is met with a lightning kick, aimed with startling precision straight at the one who touched it.
