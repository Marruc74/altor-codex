# Harpy


![Harpy](/compendium/Creatures/Fable Animals/harpy.jpg "Harpy")
Harpies so hate their own existence that they find no pleasure in life but to spread as much misery around them as they can. Filthy and surly, they are extremely vindictive.

## Appearance

A harpy has a woman's head and chest, but the rest of the body recalls an extremely shabby, scrawny vulture, with a pair of clawed wings.

## Behaviour

Harpies are notorious foulers. Cowardly, they attack only when they outnumber their foes, but they love to drop their filth on other creatures and into their food, and any food a harpy fouls turns disease-ridden and wholly inedible. Provoke a harpy in earnest and she may pursue you for many kilometres, though without ever coming too close, shrieking insults all the while. Should harpies spy a band of travellers fewer than themselves, they attack in diving stoops with beak and claw.

## Abilities

Harpies are carriers of disease, and every wound one inflicts risks giving the victim a grave contagion such as plague, smallpox or cholera.
