# Aries


![Aries](/compendium/Creatures/Fable Animals/aries.jpg "Aries")
The aries is an amphibious predator with the power to turn its hind legs into a long fish-tail when it is in the water. It lives on fish and small rodents and attacks people only when it feels threatened. They are always met alone.

## Appearance

On land the aries has the shape of a grey-white goat with a long, shaggy coat over its body. In water the hindquarters turn into a long fish-tail covered in scales, with a great fin at the very end. On its brow are two curved horns, which are said to grant the power to breathe underwater if pulverised and mixed into an elixir to drink.

## Behaviour

An aries is normally a peaceable animal that does not attack without cause.

## Abilities

The aries cannot breathe underwater, but must come up to the surface for air; it can hold its breath for some minutes, less if it is fighting, and it has perfect sight beneath the water. All who see an aries must steel their minds, or else behold an exact copy of themselves, and a victim of this glamour can scarcely bring himself to attack the mirror-image.
