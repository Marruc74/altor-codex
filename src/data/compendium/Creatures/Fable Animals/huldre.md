# Huldre

The huldre is an evil nature-spirit that lives by luring lone male woodsmen astray and then absorbing the life-energy of the dying man to sustain her own existence. Just once in her life she lets a man catch her, so that she may bear a child, and the man always dies the moment the deed is done.

## Appearance

From the front a huldre looks like an astonishingly beautiful, wholly naked woman with brown eyes and brown hair. Her back, her neck and the backs of her legs, however, are entirely covered in coarse bark.

## Behaviour

Huldres always live alone in great forests. If they come upon any wandering man, woodcutter, wood-gatherer or adventurer, they try to lure him away with them by means of a charm.

## Abilities

If the huldre's charm succeeds the victim at once begins to run blindly toward her, and he does not stop until she herself wishes it, usually only after several hours, once she is quite sure the man can never find his way out of the forest again. Should anyone try to stop the man, the huldre's hold must be overpowered, and the victim then collapses and lies as though lifeless for some days. A huldre never closes to melee, and no creature can catch her within a forest, so she can be harmed only by ranged weapons and magic, and her dead body dissolves into a cloud of smoke and vanishes without trace.
