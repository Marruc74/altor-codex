# Harebir

The harebir is a huge, frog-like beast of the deep sea, so rarely seen that most who live by the coast never lay eyes on one in a lifetime. When it does break the surface, it brings foul weather with it, and sailors have learned to dread the sudden storm that comes out of a clear sky.

## Appearance

A vast amphibious thing with powerful swimming-fins, the harebir has white skin armoured in hard, spiked plates of horn. It is built for the deep and almost never rises from it.

## Abilities

On the rare occasions a harebir surfaces, the magical discharges that come off it spawn sudden, inexplicable storms, which is why its appearances are marked by weather no one can account for. It can be driven where another will wants it to go only by powerful magic, as the conspirators of Moril Dilaz once learned when they lured one onto the volcano beneath their bay.
