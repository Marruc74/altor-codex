# Snake Sphere


![Snake Sphere](/compendium/Creatures/Fable Animals/snake-sphere.png "Snake Sphere")
A snake-sphere is a monster originally called up from some hidden hell, but which has managed to manifest itself fully and breed in our human world like any other creature. They keep chiefly to dark places, where their black, floating shapes are all but impossible to spot. They always appear alone, or perhaps in the company of a gorgon, and breed by division.

## Appearance

The snake-sphere takes the shape of a great black cloud of writhing tentacles drifting through the air. Each tentacle ends in a small, eyeless serpent-head with two sharp venom-fangs, and can shoot out with astonishing speed; the whole sphere is about a metre across.

## Behaviour

Snake-spheres are carnivores that attack anything that comes near. They strike either with their power to paralyse or by trying to bite the victim in the face, and once they have killed with their venom they drift slowly down to the victim's face, where the serpent-heads pick it clean of all flesh, leaving an untouched corpse with a wholly stripped skull upon its shoulders.

## Abilities

A snake-sphere is immune to all petrifaction, for it has no eyes; instead it finds its way by sending out waves of thought that reflect back the exact lie of its surroundings, so it sees invisible creatures and objects and is never fooled by illusion. From these same thought-waves comes its special power: it can loose an extremely powerful wave that wholly blocks the thinking of every intelligent creature within range, leaving any who cannot resist completely paralysed until the sphere ceases, which it does only on winning the fight or on dying. Its venom works the same way, blocking all thought and paralysing the victim utterly for a short while.
