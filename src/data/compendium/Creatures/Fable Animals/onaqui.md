# Onaqui


![Onaqui](/compendium/Creatures/Fable Animals/onaqui.png "Onaqui")
The onaqui is a most frightening creature that lives in tropical jungles. Where these beings come from, and what they are doing in our human world, is unknown.

## Appearance

An onaqui can grow up to two and a half metres tall and has a human-like body, clawed hands and feet, bat-wings, and a bat's head with large hairy ears, blood-red pupil-less eyes, a great flat snout and small but very sharp teeth. It is large, and so handles rather poorly in flight.

## Behaviour

Onaqui are intelligent and wicked. Pure carnivores, they do not scruple to attack prey as large as humans, and human hearts are their favourite food. They often command a few spells of elemental magic and necromancy. They are very rare and usually go alone, though sometimes they have raised small followings of zombies and skeletons about them. They move very quietly, but their hearts beat with a strong, pounding sound, and when an onaqui dies its body dissolves into a stinking cloud of smoke.

## Abilities

Ordinary weapons do an onaqui only half harm; magical weapons, weapons of jade and spells do it full harm. One who keeps his wits about him can hear an onaqui's heartbeat within fifteen metres even when it has crept up unseen. It finds its way in the dark as bats do, acting freely in pitch blackness, though it cannot cast spells while it does so.
