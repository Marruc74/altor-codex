# Giant Amoeba

A giant amoeba is a great, foul-smelling lump of slime whose chief occupation is to find organic matter it can absorb.

## Appearance

A giant amoeba looks like an enormous, shapeless, greyish, half-transparent lump of slime. It stinks abominably and is faintly sticky on the surface, with a bulk of some eight thousand litres, around two metres to a side.

## Behaviour

Although the amoeba is wholly without intelligence, it can instinctively seek out sources of nourishment, which is to say every form of life. When it meets some living thing it engulfs it with its body and begins to dissolve it with its fluids so as to absorb the nourishment. It keeps strictly to the underground and to water, since it quickly dries out in the open air.

## Abilities

An amoeba cannot be harmed by weapons, which simply slide right through it, nor by mind-affecting spells, for it has no mind at all; only fire, and spells of fire, lightning, heat and cold, do it normal harm. From its mass it can extend several tentacle-like arms to grip, say, a human's leg, and these can be cut or torn away, though the severed piece clings to the body and must be burned off. Anyone in contact with any part of the amoeba steadily loses flesh; armour and shields protect, but a clinging scrap of amoeba eats away at their substance.
