# Primeval Monster

Primeval monsters are dreadful predators that can live in every kind of environment and climate. They lack the normal drives; they are quite simply ruled by an insatiable, ceaseless hunger, and they roam about in small bands to seek food from beast and humanoid alike.

## Appearance

A primeval monster is a humanoid-like creature that can grow up to two and a half metres tall. Its arms and legs are very thin, while the upper body is heavy and muscular. In place of hands it has three very long, thin, pointed fingers like claws, and in place of feet three claws splayed in different directions, which give it fairly good balance. It has no body-hair at all save a grey mane at the back of the head and neck. The sinewy neck is short and ends in a flattened head with no human features whatever, but for a single great maw of sharp, jutting fangs well suited to tearing flesh apart.

## Behaviour

The primeval monsters' ability to live anywhere, combined with their tremendous hunger, makes them extremely dangerous creatures. They attack every living thing they come upon and eat their victims when hungry, which is nearly always. They drift from place to place in search of food; one may meet them in forests, in cave-systems, in mountain country, or even in settlements, which they attack with no regard for their own lives.

## Abilities

Since the primeval monsters have no eyes, they sense the world by a power resembling the mentalist spells of sight and true-seeing. They can thus see perfectly in any conditions, can see invisible creatures, and are never fooled by illusion. They take only half damage from elemental spells.
