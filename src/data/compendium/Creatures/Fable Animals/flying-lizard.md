# Flying Lizard

Flying lizards are large reptiles with short legs and an enormously broad wingspan. Now and then some intelligent creature tames one and uses it as a mount, though the rider must be both clever and hardy to dominate the beast.

## Appearance

The flying lizard has a powerful, reptilian body, short clawed legs and broad wings spanning some ten metres. At the ends of the wings sit clawed hands, much like a giant bat's. It is a dingy brown in colour and stinks so badly that one can detect it by smell alone from thirty metres off. The beak-like jaws are set with razor-sharp teeth.

## Behaviour

Flying lizards keep to dry, open country, preferring hilly ground. They live on any kind of mammal that looks a reasonably easy meal, from voles to halflings, humans and deer. They hunt only by day, and by night lie asleep in their nests, which are set in shallow caves, clefts of rock, or burrows in the ground.

## Abilities

Flying lizards have no special powers.
