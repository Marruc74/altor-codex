# Manticore


![Manticore](/compendium/Creatures/Fable Animals/manticore.jpg "Manticore")
The manticore is not the cleverest foe one might meet, but it is dangerous. Manticores are often found in the service of magicians who need reliable watchdogs.

## Appearance

A manticore looks like a russet lion with an angry human face. Its tail is a very dangerous weapon, much like a scorpion's, ending in a cluster of spines tipped with a very strong venom; one spine, larger than the rest, sits at the very tip, the others ringed around it.

## Behaviour

Manticores possess a certain intelligence, but are not especially clever. Rapacious and hungry, they will eat practically any creature. In the wild they live in open, preferably rocky country, but they are also found in tamed service.

## Abilities

Manticores have a language of their own, and some also speak certain human tongues. The spines of the manticore's tail are venomous, and it can fire all but one of them as projectiles, which regrow over time until they number seven again. The manticore can strike with its tail at the same time as it fights with claws or teeth.
