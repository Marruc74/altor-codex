# Kerberos


![Kerberos](/compendium/Creatures/Fable Animals/kerberos.jpg "Kerberos")
There are many roads to the Realm of Death. Each is guarded by a terrible creature set there by the death-gods to keep the souls of the dead from returning to the circle of the living. Kerberos is one of these wardens. Should Kerberos be slain, one of the death-gods can raise him again, so that he may return to his watch.

## Appearance

Kerberos is a gigantic, black, wolf-like hound with three heads. Each head has a drooling maw of long, sharp teeth. The eyes are blood-red and radiate intelligence and violence.

## Behaviour

Kerberos is set to guard the gate of the Realm of Death, and so never stirs from it. He needs no bodily nourishment, living instead on the psychic force that streams from the lost souls of the realm, and he has no need of sleep.

## Abilities

Because Kerberos is half magical in nature, magical weapons are needed to do him full harm; ordinary weapons do only half. Through his enormous size and ferocity he can bowl his victims over with relative ease, and a creature knocked down by his bite is left floundering, unable to act and easily struck until it can regain its feet. Note that for all his three heads, Kerberos has but a single mind.
