# Dwarf Horse

The dwarf-horse is a small, hardy horse of the Montures, running wild on the pasture-isle of Jalujak. Left to itself it lives a free enough life, but few of its kind are left to it.

## The Mine-Beast

The dwarf-horses are caught on Jalujak and broken to labour, then carried up into the silver-mines of the Agino Mountains to draw the ore-carts through the narrow tunnels. It is a short and brutal life: a mine dwarf-horse lasts only about five years before the work wears it out, and once it goes down into the shafts it never comes up into the light again.
