# Kalydon


![Kalydon](/compendium/Creatures/Fable Animals/kalydon.jpg "Kalydon")
A kalydon is a gigantic fire-breathing boar. Most likely it is, like several other vile creatures, the result of some magician's repugnant experiment, in this case probably the manipulation of ordinary wild boar; or the breed may have its origin in one of the realms of Chaos and have broken through to our dimension by mistake.

## Appearance

The kalydon looks like a gigantic ordinary wild boar. It is white in colour, as large as an ox, and its tusks recall an elephant's.

## Behaviour

The beast is a savage and cruel predator that will not scruple to attack creatures larger than itself. It does not live in herds, and there seem to be neither sows nor piglets. It is found chiefly in open country, where nothing checks its violent charge.

## Abilities

A kalydon, as its name suggests, spits fire. It can hold a limited number of fire-charges and takes a full day to replenish each one, the gout of flame forming a cone several metres long. For all its fury it is not subtle, and a foe who keeps clear of both its charge and its flame has the measure of it.
