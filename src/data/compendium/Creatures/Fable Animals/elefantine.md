# Elefantine

The elefantiner are tiny wild "elephants" of Magilre, scarcely a metre tall and famously ferocious for their size. For all that they look like miniatures of the great beasts, they are no one's idea of harmless.

## Appearance

An elefantine stands about a metre high, a small, fierce creature of the plains and bogs. There are two kinds: one walks on ordinary feet, while the other is flat-footed, its toes able to splay wide so that it can cross the soft ground of the swamp of Efronzink without sinking.
