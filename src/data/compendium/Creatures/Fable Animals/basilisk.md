# Basilisk


![Basilisk](/compendium/Creatures/Fable Animals/basilisk.jpg "Basilisk")
The reptilian basilisk and its petrifying gaze are no matter for a novice adventurer, though a seasoned fighter usually has a fair chance against the vile creature.

## Appearance

It looks like a middling reptile with a sharp-beaked bird's head, crowned by a crest like a comb so that the head faintly recalls a rooster's. It has black, leathery wings and bird's legs tipped with sharp claws.

## Behaviour

A solitary creature, the basilisk often turns up as a pet, though never a true familiar, kept by less scrupulous sorcerers. Aggressive and single-minded in its zeal to defend its territory, it fights on until it is beaten to death.

## Abilities

A basilisk can turn a foe to stone with its gaze. Each moment it can fix a creature with its eye and strive to overcome its will; if it succeeds the victim is turned instantly to stone, and if it fails nothing happens. The gaze cuts through magical defences as though it were a weak spell, and being faster than any weapon the basilisk always strikes first. It flies, but slowly and poorly.
