# Shranck

Shranck is a giant shark of prehistoric size, worshipped as a god by the shark-men of Bychow on the Montures. Far larger than the great sharks the bestiaries already know, it is a thing of the deep that the villagers both fear and revere.

## The Shark-God

The shark-men keep their worship of Shranck at a place they call Shranck's Reef, where they bring their offerings to the beast. Most of what they give it is the catch of the sea, but not all: travellers who learn too much of the village's secret are murdered and offered up to the shark-god as well. Whether Shranck is truly a single ancient creature or merely the greatest of its kind, none who have seen it up close have lived to say.
