# Alfin


![Alfin](/compendium/Creatures/Fable Animals/alfin.jpg "Alfin")
The alfin, or elf-cat, is a small and uncanny feline that keeps the company of the elf-folk. Bound to the fey and their deep woods, it shares something of their nature, and goes among them as companion and watcher rather than as any tamed pet. It is rarely seen by other peoples, and never truly tames to a human hand.
