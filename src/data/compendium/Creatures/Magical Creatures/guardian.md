# Guardian

Guardians are powerful magical creatures with a single purpose: to keep other intelligent creatures from passing them. A guardian closely resembles a golem, but the difference is that in a guardian a spirit has been bound by a mighty rune to the petrified creature. The only way to drive out the spirit is to destroy the rune.

## Appearance

A guardian is a petrified, enlarged version of an ordinary animal or monster, with one or more heads. Common forms are wolves, griffins, minotaurs or some other creature very skilled in a fight.

## Behaviour

If anyone comes within sight or hearing of a guardian, it at once begins to make its noise, barking, shrieking, roaring or whatever the animal or monster might, and the closer the intruder comes, the louder the noise grows.

## Abilities

Each head a guardian has bears its own rune, so to silence a three-headed guardian three runes must be destroyed. Guardians are immune to all ordinary weapons and to fire, poison and sleep, but take normal harm from spells and magical weapons. Anyone wishing to come within a few metres of a guardian must master the dreadful din or be forced to cover his ears for a moment before trying again. A guardian never needs to sleep, eat or drink, and the natural weapons it uses depend on what it was made from.
