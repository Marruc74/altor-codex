# Guardian

Guardians are powerful magical creatures with a single purpose: to stop others from passing them. A guardian is much like a golem, save that a spirit has been bound by a mighty rune into the petrified beast, and only destroying the rune will drive it out.

## Appearance

A petrified, enlarged version of some ordinary animal or monster, with one or more heads, often a wolf, griffin or minotaur, or whatever else is deadly in a fight.

## Behaviour

When anything comes within sight or earshot it begins to sound, barking, screaming or roaring ever louder the nearer the intruder comes.

## Abilities

Immune to all ordinary weapons and to fire, poison and sleep, each of its heads bears a rune that must be destroyed to silence it, and the dreadful din alone can drive off any who try to pass.
