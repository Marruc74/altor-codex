# Phantom

Phantoms are the half-material spirits of those who gave their lives in the search for some particular object, a thing long sought or one created at the moment of death, such as a mage slain by a backfire while making a great artefact. Unlike death wraiths they did not become wraiths willingly, and so their powers are limited.

## Appearance

It appears as its body was before death, without death-wounds, in a faintly blue-shimmering form, usually bearing the gear it carried when it died.

## Behaviour

Their temperament varies with the soul they had in life, for it is misfortune rather than malice that makes a phantom. The one way to free it is to destroy the object it is bound to, which it will do anything to prevent, and it can never willingly let anyone harm that object.

## Abilities

Solid enough to handle light objects and wield a one-handed weapon in both hands, it takes no harm from ordinary weapons and only half from magic; the touch of its body or weapon strikes like a bolt of lightning. It cannot stray more than a few metres from its object.
