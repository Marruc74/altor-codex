# Dark Wraith

Dark wraiths are something between barrow-wights and death wraiths, their essence never quite solid enough to handle objects, so in that they are more like the formless spirits.

## Appearance

Alone among the wraiths it keeps only its humanoid outline; every other feature is wiped away, leaving the shape of a black, faceless silhouette with two glowing coals for eyes.

## Behaviour

Like barrow-wights it favours dark places, though sunlight does it no harm, and it is evil by nature, the purpose of its undeath varying from one to the next.

## Abilities

Its body is so diffuse that an attacker in darkness can barely land a blow, while strong light makes it easier; it can be hurt only by magic weapons, spells and fire. A touch drains the body's vigour forever and leaves the victim paralysed.
