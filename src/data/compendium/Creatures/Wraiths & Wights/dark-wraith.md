# Dark Wraith

Dark wraiths are a kind of midpoint between barrow wights and death wraiths. Their being has not, as with the barrow wights, managed to take on a form solid enough to handle objects; in this respect they are more like the bodiless spirits.

## Appearance

Unlike other wraiths the dark wraith keeps only its humanoid outline from its former life. Every other individual feature is wiped wholly away, and it takes the shape of a black silhouette without face or features, with two glowing coals in place of eyes.

## Behaviour

Like the barrow wights, dark wraiths keep by preference to dark places, though they take no harm from sunlight. They are evil by nature, or they would not have become wraiths at all, but the purpose of their unlife may vary.

## Abilities

Because a dark wraith's body is so diffuse, an attacker in darkness can scarcely strike it at all, while near strong light, magical or natural, it is easier to hit. It can be harmed only by magical weapons, spells and fire. It cannot keep any skills based on bodily strength or agility, but keeps the rest of its abilities from life. By touching a victim and overcoming his body with its will, a dark wraith can drain his strength permanently and leave him completely paralysed for a short while; armour, clothing and ordinary wards are no help, though strong protective magic shields against it.
