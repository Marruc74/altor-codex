# Will-o'-the-Wisp

Will-o'-the-wisps are the ghosts of wicked folk who drowned or met a violent end in marshes, swamps or little forest pools.

## Appearance

Balls of light drifting freely through the air, the dead one's features dim within the glow. They can brighten, dim or snuff the light at will.

## Behaviour

Their whole aim is to lure careless travellers off the safe marsh paths and out into the bottomless mire, where a drowning victim's spirit can be drawn off and devoured.

## Abilities

A will-o'-the-wisp can seize a person's mind and draw them from the path to drown, feeding on the will of intelligent creatures that die nearby and draining its victims away point by point, for good.
