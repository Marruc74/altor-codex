# Poltergeist

A poltergeist is a spirit from another dimension, easily called up by spiritists and usually returning when the spell ends, though now and then one breaks loose or slips into the mortal world by other means and stays until it is slain or banished.

## Appearance

An immaterial, near-transparent thing that can change its shape however it likes.

## Behaviour

Violent, wild and aggressive, yet unable to harm a living creature directly; instead it works terrible chaos, hurling about every loose object within ten metres and swelling all nearby sound, until it departs and everything falls still again.

## Abilities

It can lift and fling any number of objects up to a fair weight, pelting people with knives, axes or stones by sheer force of will.
