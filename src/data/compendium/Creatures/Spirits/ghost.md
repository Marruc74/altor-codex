# Ghost

Ghosts are the spirits of those who died with some important task unfinished; the will and rage of that moment bound them to the mortal world, and only by completing the task can they escape it.

## Appearance

A blue-shimmering, immaterial figure looking just as it did at death, in the same clothes, bearing its old wounds, perhaps even a half-severed head.

## Behaviour

Most long only to be freed from their undeath. If the task is to fetch, free, destroy or slay something it cannot manage alone, a ghost may help those it meets; but if the task is to guard or protect something, it turns far crueller and strikes with terror.

## Abilities

By a mere touch a ghost drains the living body's vigour, and it cannot stray far from the place where it died.
