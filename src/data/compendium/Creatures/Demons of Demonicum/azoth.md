# Azoth

The azoth are the elite demon-warriors of Nehcrom, the Horned One, bred and armed in his black fortress on the cliff Khla to be the spearhead of his endless wars against Chaos and against his rival Bemoth.

## Appearance

A humanoid standing well over two metres, with a pair of long horns curving forward from the brow, some two to three hand-spans in length, and eyes that are white and without pupils. They go to war armoured and armed for nothing else.

## Behaviour

Loyal to Nehcrom above all things, relentlessly aggressive, and cold of feeling. They fight without anger and without mercy, as a tool fights in a hand.

## Abilities

The azoth are tireless and hard to put down. They strike before any foe can move, they shrug off pain that would fell a lesser thing, and their wounds knit closed almost as fast as they are dealt. They are very rarely seen in our world, and then only where a mighty demonologist has called up a war-band of them at once.
