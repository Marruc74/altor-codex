# Nerocq

The nerocq is a monstrous hunting-beast of Leithicor, prized by the servants of the Guardian Bemoth as a mount and a hound of war. The greatest honour Bemoth grants a favoured follower is a loyal nerocq to ride.

## Appearance

A four-footed beast, heavy of claw and jaw, its head armoured and set with spikes, with a short, thick tail.

## Behaviour

Very aggressive. It lies slow and still as a stone, and then strikes with lightning speed.

## Abilities

The nerocq moves unseen and untracked through the wild. It cloaks itself in mist, melts into the background, and leaves no trail behind. Then it falls upon its prey with rending claws and a crushing bite, and a blood-thirst that will not be denied once the blood begins to flow.
