# Darubah

Darubah is a unique demon, the trickster-servant of Caliban, the lord of madness, who keeps the shifting halls of the fortress Methucalem and welcomes those few who win their way to its master.

## Appearance

Fat and big-bellied, with long limbs and a narrow head, a spiny back and tail, and bluish skin.

## Behaviour

A deceiver to the bone, charming and courteous one moment and treacherous the next, who would always far rather win by cunning than by force, and who is loyal, in his own crooked fashion, to Caliban alone.

## Abilities

Darubah reads the thoughts of those he speaks with and changes his shape at will, and he can snare a victim in a waking trance. He breathes out madness upon all around him, and can reach across the worlds to punish any who break an oath sworn to him.
