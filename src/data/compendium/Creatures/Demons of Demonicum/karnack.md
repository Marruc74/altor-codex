# Karnack

Karnacks are the winged war-demons of Leithicor, the wild realm of the Guardian Bemoth, loosed from the sky upon any who threaten their lord or their flock.

## Appearance

A bird-monster with a wingspan of some two and a half metres, dark-blue leathery skin and a tearing beak.

## Behaviour

Aggressive and flock-minded, swift to fall upon a threat and slow to leave it.

## Abilities

Karnacks are fast and tireless flyers, driven by a constant gnawing hunger. They come in numbers and sweep down out of the air, and they were part of the wild hordes that broke Nehcrom's armies from above when the Horned One dared to invade Leithicor.
