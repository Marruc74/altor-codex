# Feot

The feot are the humble servant-demons of Methucalem, set to the menial work of Caliban's mad halls.

## Appearance

Dog-like, with long ears, very long arms and short legs, and clad in leather armour. Their long reach lets them fight to advantage with weapons of any length.

## Behaviour

Humble and servile, for all that they are demons, yet no less dangerous for it.

## Abilities

The feot cloak themselves in mist and camouflage and sense danger keenly, and they can turn a victim's mind with a trance or a sudden stab of pain. The sight or smell of blood can rouse a blood-thirst in them that is slow to cool. They are met in packs.
