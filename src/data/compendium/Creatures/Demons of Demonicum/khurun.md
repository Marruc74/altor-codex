# Khurún

Khurún is a unique pascha, one of the demon-warlords that count among the mightiest demons to trouble the worlds, and he serves Caliban from the shifting fortress of Methucalem.

## Appearance

A humanoid some four metres tall, grossly over-muscled, horned, dark-red of skin, with a heavy mace-like tail.

## Behaviour

Arrogant beyond all measure, he commands every being that comes near him and brooks no defiance.

## Abilities

Few demons that cross into Altor are so terrible. Khurún reads minds, flies, sees in the dark, and hurls lightning. In battle he goes berserk, looses demon-fire and a death-glare of green flame, crushes a foe beneath his own multiplied weight, and wraps himself in an aura of pure terror.
