# Sylf


![Sylf](/compendium/Creatures/Elementals/sylf.jpg "Sylf")
Elementals are incarnations of the elements, summoned from the elemental planes into our world by a magician's elemental magic. They have no will or wit of their own and must be directed by the full attention of the one who called them; they are immune to poison but can be harmed by magic as normal. The sylf is the elemental of air.

## Appearance

A sylf appears as a barely-seen swirl of moving air, a ripple in the world more felt than glimpsed.

## Behaviour

Like all elementals, the sylf has no will of its own and acts only at the command of the magician who summoned it, until it is sent back to its plane.

## Abilities

A sylf moves swift as the wind and is hard to strike with any ordinary weapon, being little more than rushing air. It can knock a creature off its feet with a sudden gust, overbearing the victim by main force.
