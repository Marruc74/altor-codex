# Elemental Creatures

The elemental creatures are the native beasts of the elemental planes: the fire horse of the burning world, the frost wolf of the ice, the water wave, the earth beetle and their stranger kin. Unlike the elemental spirits, they are born to their planes and have wits and wills of their own; unlike the spirits, too, they lack any special elemental power, being simply creatures shaped by the plane that bred them.

## Beasts of the Planes

Having no bodily vigour of the mortal sort, an elemental creature cannot be touched by poison, sleep, fear or disease, and when it is slain in our world it does not die but slips back to its plane. Some can be tamed or ridden by a mage who knows the right wards, for the fire horse's coat is living flame and the frost wolf's a killing cold. They turn up in our world in the wake of a summoning or an elemental lord, or now and then stray across on their own.
