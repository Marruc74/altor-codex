# Cyclone

![Cyclone](/compendium/Creatures/Elementals/cyclone.jpg "Cyclone")

The elemental lords are very powerful elemental beings that cannot be summoned by an ordinary magician; they come and go between the elemental planes and the human world as they please, each bent on spreading its own element through every world. The cyclone is the elemental lord of air, and almost always appears alone, attended by a number of lesser air-elementals and elemental creatures.

## Appearance

A cyclone can take the form either of a gigantic, funnel-shaped whirlwind or of an enormous human whose body is made of cloud, its outlines very diffuse, its eyes a pair of pale-blue, clear-shining points. It seems to wear neither armour nor clothing, and never uses weapons.

## Behaviour

Elemental lords are not by nature aggressive or any real threat to other intelligent creatures, but their enormous powers and slight interest in other living things make it easy to be caught in their wake. On the rare occasions they leave their domains they always have a set purpose, often mere reconnaissance, and they may also be set to guard something such as a portal or a dangerous object. The endless struggle between the elements shows itself only seldom, and then as great natural catastrophes.

## Abilities

Like all elemental lords, a cyclone wields great magic, is utterly immune to its own element of air, cannot be harmed by ordinary weapons, which it instead dulls, and can summon or send away air-elementals and elemental creatures at will. It can control every movement of the air within a wide radius, raising winds strong enough to bowl creatures over, which makes ranged weapons all but useless against it, and it can hurl a concentrated bolt of wind that wounds a single victim and flings him backward. It can destroy fire-elementals, fire horses and magmani by overpowering their will.
