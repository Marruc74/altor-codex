# Light Bird

![Light Bird](/compendium/Creatures/Elementals/light-bird.jpg "Light Bird")

Light birds dwell on the elemental plane of light. Like all elemental creatures they have an intelligence of their own but lack the elementals' special powers, and being without bodily vigour they cannot be harmed by poison, sleep, fear or disease; when slain in our world they return to their plane.

## Appearance

The light bird appears as an enormous, eagle-like bird that blazes with strong white light, normally lighting up a wide area around it.

## Behaviour

A predator of the air, the light bird strikes with beak and claw and, above all, with its blinding radiance. Like all elemental creatures it has a will of its own.

## Abilities

All who look upon a light bird must steel their minds or be struck blind for a short while; it can loose this blinding glare only now and then, a handful of times a day. It cannot be affected by spells of fire or lightning, and like all elemental creatures it is immune to poison, sleep, fear and disease, returning to the plane of light when its body is destroyed.
