# Luminal

Luminals are the elementals of light, incarnations of that element summoned from its plane into our world by elemental magic and bound to the will of the magician who calls them. Like all elementals they have no wit of their own, are immune to poison, and can be harmed by magic as normal.

## Appearance

A luminal appears as a globe of clear, shining light.

## Behaviour

Like all elementals, the luminal has no will of its own and acts only at the command of the magician who summoned it, until sent back to its plane.

## Abilities

A luminal cannot be harmed by material weapons and is immune to all fire. It blinds every creature that looks upon it, the more surely those who see in the dark, leaving them for a while as though in pitch blackness.
