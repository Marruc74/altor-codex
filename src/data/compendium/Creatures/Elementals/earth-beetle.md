# Earth Beetle

![Earth Beetle](/compendium/Creatures/Elementals/earth-beetle.jpg "Earth Beetle")

Earth beetles dwell on the elemental plane of earth. Like all elemental creatures they have an intelligence of their own but lack the elementals' special powers, and being without bodily vigour they cannot be harmed by poison, sleep, fear or disease; when slain in our world they return to their plane.

## Appearance

The earth beetle looks like a gigantic beetle.

## Behaviour

A predator that strikes with bite and claw, it can also hide itself almost perfectly in mud, earth, sand or gravel, and tracks its prey with patience. Like all elemental creatures it has a will of its own.

## Abilities

The earth beetle can spit a stream of compressed clods of earth at a foe a short way off, a handful of times a day. Fire and fire-spells do it only half harm, and like all elemental creatures it is immune to poison, sleep, fear and disease, returning to the plane of earth when its body is destroyed.
