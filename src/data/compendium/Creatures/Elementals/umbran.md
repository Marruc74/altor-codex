# Umbran

The umbran is the elemental of darkness, called up from the dark elemental plane and easily mistaken for a dark wraith.

## Appearance

A vaguely man-shaped figure formed of compact, lightless darkness.

## Abilities

It cannot be harmed by material weapons but takes double damage from fire. It can raise a globe of darkness in which no light carries and even dark-sighted creatures are blind, and by enfolding a victim it strikes them with terror.
