# Sea King

![Sea King](/compendium/Creatures/Elementals/sea-king.jpg "Sea King")

A sea-king is the elemental lord of water, one of the very powerful elemental beings that come and go between the planes and the human world as they please, bent on spreading water through every world. Like all its kind it appears almost always alone, attended by lesser water-elementals and elemental creatures.

## Appearance

A sea-king looks either like an enormous human clad in seaweed, with bluish skin, or like a five-metre wave with two arm-like limbs reaching from its sides. In its human form it fights with enormous tridents.

## Behaviour

Like all elemental lords it is not by nature aggressive, but its enormous powers and indifference to other living things make it dangerous; it may be set to guard something or sent on reconnaissance, and the clash of the elements shows itself in catastrophes such as floods and giant waves.

## Abilities

Like all elemental lords, a sea-king wields great magic, is immune to its own element of water, cannot be harmed by ordinary weapons, which it dulls, and can summon or dismiss water-elementals and elemental creatures. It can control every movement of water within a wide radius, raising fierce currents, and from any body of water can direct a concentrated jet that wounds and bowls over its target; in its wave-form it can wash away earth-elementals, earth beetles and matter queens by overpowering their will.
