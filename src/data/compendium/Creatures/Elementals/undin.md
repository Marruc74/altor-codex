# Undin


![Undin](/compendium/Creatures/Elementals/undin.jpg "Undin")
Elementals are incarnations of the elements, summoned from the elemental planes into our world by a magician's elemental magic. They have no will or wit of their own and must be directed by the full attention of the one who called them; they are immune to poison but can be harmed by magic as normal. The undin is the elemental of water.

## Appearance

An undin appears as a shifting form of clear water, holding its shape only so long as the magic that called it endures.

## Behaviour

Like all elementals, the undin has no will of its own and acts only at the command of the magician who summoned it, until it is sent back to its plane.

## Abilities

An undin throws itself over its prey in a drowning surge, and a creature caught in it may swallow water and be left choking and helpless. Natural fire cannot harm it, and ordinary weapons pass through it with little effect, though magical fire will boil it away.
