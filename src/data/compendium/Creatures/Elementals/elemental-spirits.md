# Elemental Spirits

The elemental spirits are the elements themselves given shape: the salamander of fire, the undin of water, the sylf of air, the gnom of earth, and their kin of light, darkness, heat and cold. A magician draws them out of the elemental planes with elemental magic, and while the spell holds each takes on a body of pure element.

## Summoned, Not Born

An elemental spirit has no will or wit of its own. It does nothing but what the magician who called it wills, and only so long as he bends his whole attention to it; let his focus slip and the spirit unravels back to its plane. They feel no poison and no fear, but magic wounds them as it would anything, and when the binding ends there is simply nothing left behind. For all that, a well-steered elemental is a fearsome thing, a living piece of the element loosed in whatever shape its master chooses.
