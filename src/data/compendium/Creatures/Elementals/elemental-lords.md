# Elemental Lords

The elemental lords are the mightiest of all elemental beings, one to each element: the Sea King of water, Magmani of fire, the Ice Lord, the Cyclone of the winds, and the rest. Unlike the mindless spirits, a lord needs no summoning and no master. It crosses between the planes and the human world as it pleases, and it comes with one purpose above all, to spread its own element through every world it touches, until fire or flood or sand has swallowed everything.

## Alone and Attended

A lord almost always appears alone, yet never truly unaccompanied, for lesser elementals and elemental creatures gather to it wherever it goes. They are proud, willful and all but impossible to command, and where one settles the land itself starts to change to suit it: seas rise, deserts spread, the air turns to unending storm. To drive one off is hard, and to destroy it harder, for like all its kind a lord can be ended for good only on its own plane.
