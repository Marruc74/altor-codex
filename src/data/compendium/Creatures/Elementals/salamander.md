# Salamander


![Salamander](/compendium/Creatures/Elementals/salamander.jpg "Salamander")
Elementals are incarnations of the elements, summoned from the elemental planes into our world by a magician's elemental magic. They have no will or wit of their own and must be directed by the full attention of the one who called them; they are immune to poison but can be harmed by magic as normal. The salamander is the elemental of fire.

## Appearance

A salamander appears as a vaguely man-shaped figure of living, leaping flame.

## Behaviour

Like all elementals, the salamander has no will of its own and acts only at the command of the magician who summoned it, until it is sent back to its plane.

## Abilities

A salamander burns whatever it touches and sets alight anything that will catch, so that to grapple with one is to be scorched, and it takes no hurt at all from fire or heat. Water and cold, on the other hand, bite at it cruelly.
