# Gnom


![Gnom](/compendium/Creatures/Elementals/gnom.jpg "Gnom")
Elementals are incarnations of the elements, summoned from the elemental planes into our world by a magician's elemental magic. They have no will or wit of their own and must be directed by the full attention of the one who called them; they are immune to poison but can be harmed by magic as normal. The gnom is the elemental of earth, and should not be confused with the gnomes of forest and field, who are an elf-folk entirely.

## Appearance

A gnom appears as a heavy, man-shaped figure of packed earth and stone.

## Behaviour

Like all elementals, the gnom has no will of its own and acts only at the command of the magician who summoned it, until it is sent back to its plane.

## Abilities

A gnom is slow but immensely strong, striking with the weight of stone, and fire scarcely troubles it. It moves through soft ground as easily as a swimmer through water, and is at home in the deep places of the earth.
