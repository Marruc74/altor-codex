# Carry-Beetle

The carry-beetle is a great, docile beetle bred underground as a beast of burden, used by the deep-folk as both a pack animal and a slow riding-mount. Generations of breeding have left it placid and biddable, content to haul or carry wherever it is led, and stables in the larger underground halls keep them for hire much as a surface town keeps horses.

For all its size the carry-beetle is no fighter. It has the hard shell and patient strength of its kind and little else, and depends on its handlers for protection. In the caverns of places like Nordsal a traveller can rent one to bear goods through the long lightless roads below.
