# Rat Swarm

![Rat Swarm](/compendium/Creatures/Animals/rat-swarm.jpg "Rat Swarm")

A rat swarm is a seething mass of rats from sewer, cellar or ruin, harmless one by one but overwhelming in their numbers. They fall upon a victim together, biting from every side, and are worse against any who has fallen; fire and torches will frighten them off for a time.
