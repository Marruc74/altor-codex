# Hippopotamus

![Hippopotamus](/compendium/Creatures/Animals/hippopotamus.jpg "Hippopotamus")

The hippopotamus is a huge, barrel-bodied river beast that spends its days half-submerged and its nights grazing the banks. For all that it eats only grass, it is among the most dangerous of animals, fiercely territorial in the water and able to bite a small boat, or a man, clean in two.
