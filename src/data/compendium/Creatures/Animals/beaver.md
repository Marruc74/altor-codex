# Beaver

![Beaver](/compendium/Creatures/Animals/beaver.jpg "Beaver")

The beaver is a broad-tailed water-rodent of forest rivers and streams, a tireless builder that fells trees with its teeth and dams whole watercourses to raise the ponds it lives on. Its work reshapes the land around it, flooding meadows and woods, and its rich fur is much sought after by trappers.
