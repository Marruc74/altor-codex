# Night-Cat

Night-cats are coal-black hunting cats of dark and petrified forests, named for a coat that all but vanishes in shadow and for the yellow eyes that give them away, pupil-less and faintly glowing. They hunt by night among the dead trees, and a traveller often hears nothing of them until the eyes are already close.

Their claws are long and razor-sharp, and they hunt in the patient, stalking way of all cats, picking off the slow and the strayed. The dark coat and the silent tread make them hard to mark until they strike.
