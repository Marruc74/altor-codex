# Sand-Rassler

Sand-rasslers are great flesh-eating centipedes of the open plains, long segmented hunters that hunt by scent and fall on anything that stirs the sand near them. They lie still beneath the surface until the ground moves, then come up fast, and a band crossing their country learns to step lightly and watch the dust.

What they lack in cunning they make up in numbers and persistence, following a scent until they reach it. They make no distinction between prey and traveller; whatever disturbs the sand is food.
