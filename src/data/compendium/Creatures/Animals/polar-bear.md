# Polar Bear

![Polar Bear](/compendium/Creatures/Animals/polar-bear.jpg "Polar Bear")

The polar bear is a great white bear of the frozen north, larger and stronger than the black bears of the southern forests, at home on the ice and in the cold seas. It is a powerful swimmer, as much at ease in the water as on land.

## Behaviour

A solitary hunter, the polar bear roams the ice after seals and fish and whatever else the cold lands afford, and is dangerous when surprised or cornered.

## Abilities

For all its bulk it moves fast over snow and ice, strikes with raking claws and a crushing bite, and can seize a foe in a bone-breaking hug. Its thick white pelt both wards off the cold and hides it against the snow.
