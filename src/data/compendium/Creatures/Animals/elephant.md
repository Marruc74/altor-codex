# Elephant

The elephant is the greatest of the land beasts, a towering grey grazer with a long grasping trunk and two curved tusks, found wild on the warm plains and kept tamed as a beast of burden and of war. It is peaceable unless provoked, but a maddened or frightened bull can seize a man in its trunk, hurl him down and trample him. Its great cold-land kin, the shaggy mammoth and the mastodon, are reckoned its cousins.
