# Yeti

The yeti, or snowman, is a great ape-man of the high, snowbound mountain forests, where it lives quietly on plants and small game. It keeps to family groups of two grown beasts and their young, denning most often in a cave.

## Appearance

A yeti is very large and heavily built, covered head to foot in thick grey-white fur that hides it almost perfectly against the snow.

## Behaviour

The yeti is a deeply shy creature that shuns all thinking folk and would far rather slip away unseen than be found, though it fights to the death for its young. It has some wit of its own and a simple, primitive speech.

## Abilities

Few things hide as well as a yeti, and in snow it is next to invisible. It is a strong climber, sure-footed on the worst slopes, and has a keen nose for danger.
