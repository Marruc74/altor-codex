# Lava Lizard

![Lava Lizard](/compendium/Adventures/TheFinalBattle/Yndar/Lava-Lizard 1.jpg "Lava Lizard")
![Lava Lizard](/compendium/Adventures/TheFinalBattle/Yndar/Lava-Lizard 2.jpg "Lava Lizard")
Lava lizards are heat-proof red predators that live where nothing else can, basking and hunting along molten ravines and the edges of lava flows. Their scaled hide shrugs off the heat that would kill another animal, and they take their prey from among the creatures that stray too close to the burning ground.

Quick and bold on their own scorched territory, they are rarely a threat away from it, and few hunters care to follow one back into the heat to find out.
