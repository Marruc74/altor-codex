# Bison

![Bison](/compendium/Creatures/Animals/bison.jpg "Bison")

The bison is a great shaggy wild ox of the grasslands, roaming the plains in vast herds many hundreds strong. Peaceable grazers for the most part, a stampeding herd is a deadly thing, and a cornered bull will gore and trample anything in its path. They cannot be tamed.
