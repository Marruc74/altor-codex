# Fur Lizard

The fur lizard is a warm-blooded flying lizard of the wilds, set apart from its cold-blooded kin by a covering of soft hide and a sharper wit. It is a wary wild thing, hunting on the wing and denning in high crags, and its skin fetches a fair price from furriers who prize the unusual pelt.

Because a fur lizard will scavenge as readily as it hunts, a half-eaten carcass left by one is now and then mistaken for the work of something far worse, and a nervous district can talk itself into a monster where there is only a lizard finishing someone else's kill.
