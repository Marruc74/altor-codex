# Wood Elves

The forest elves, the skogsalver, are the most numerous of all elvenkind, and yet their hand lies lightest upon the chronicles of the world. Theirs is a contemplative and passive way of life, turned inward toward the quiet of the woods rather than outward toward conquest or craft, and so they never shaped the course of history as humans and dwarves did. Their heart and their greatest home is the living forest of Landori, where the elven spirit endures undisturbed by the restless ages of other peoples.

## Overview

- **People:** the forest elves (skogsalver)
- **Numbers:** the most numerous of the elves
- **Character:** contemplative and passive
- **Mark on history:** slight, far less than humans or dwarves
- **Great centre:** the living forest of Landori, between the Nida Mountains and Berendien

## The Most Numerous Elves

Of all the kindreds of elves, the forest elves are the most numerous. They are spread widely through the woodlands of the world, and in sheer number they far outstrip their grey-elven and other cousins. Yet numbers alone have never made them a power in the way of humankind, for what the skogsalver possess in multitude they decline to spend on ambition.

## A Contemplative People

The culture of the forest elves is contemplative and passive. They live turned toward reflection, toward the slow seasons of the forest and the long thoughts of a long-lived people, and they feel little of the human hunger to build, to rule and to remake the world. This temper has kept them apart from the great struggles of history, content to let the ages pass through their woods like weather.

## A Light Hand on History

Because of this gentle and inward nature, the forest elves never shaped history as much as humans and dwarves did. While other peoples founded empires, forged metals and waged the wars that the chronicles remember, the skogsalver kept to their forests and left the loud business of the world to others. Their influence is real but quiet, felt more in what they have preserved than in what they have changed.

## The Forest of Landori

The great centre of the forest elves is Landori, a living forest that lies between the Nida Mountains and the land of Berendien. Here the skogsalver gather in their greatest strength, and here their way of life flourishes most fully. Landori is no mere woodland but the very heart of forest-elven culture, a place where the contemplative spirit of the people and the deep life of the trees are bound together as one.
