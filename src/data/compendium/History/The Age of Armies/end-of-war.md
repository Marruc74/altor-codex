# The Close of the Age of Armies

The Age of Armies was a long season of struggle around the Copper Sea, when realm contended against realm and no single power held the whole. It ended not with a final, world-shaking battle but with a settling of the scales, as one realm rose above the rest. With the ascent of Jorpagna to pre-eminence, the time of endless contest gave way to the order of the Imperial Age that followed.

## Overview

- **Era:** The Age of Armies
- **Date:** 1080 - 950 fO
- **Region:** The lands around the Copper Sea
- **Character:** An age of power struggles among rival realms
- **Outcome:** The rise of Jorpagna to pre-eminence; the opening of the Imperial Age

## An Age of Power Struggles

From 1080 to 950 fO the lands around the Copper Sea were locked in contest. Realms rose and fell, alliances formed and broke, and the balance of power shifted from one year to the next. It was an age defined by struggle, when the question of who would rule the region remained open and bitterly fought.

## The Rise of Jorpagna

The age drew to its close as Jorpagna rose to pre-eminence above its rivals. Where many had contended, one at last prevailed, and the long uncertainty began to resolve into a settled order. Jorpagna's ascendancy did not merely end a war; it brought the whole age of contest toward its conclusion.

## The Threshold of the Imperial Age

With Jorpagna pre-eminent, the way lay open to the Imperial Age that came after. The endless struggles of the Age of Armies gave way to the rule of a dominant power, and the lands around the Copper Sea passed from an age of many contenders into an age of empire.
