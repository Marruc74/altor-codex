# The Sack of Sombatze

The Sack of Sombatze was one of the great calamities of the Age of Armies in the south. When the armies of Efaro fell upon the city of Sombatze and laid it waste, they did more than destroy a single city - they tore the heart out of northern Soluna and cast the whole region into a chaos that would not lift for generations.

## Overview

- **Era:** The Age of Armies
- **Region:** Northern Soluna
- **Aggressor:** Efaro
- **Target:** The city of Sombatze
- **Outcome:** Sombatze destroyed; northern Soluna in chaos until around 1000 fO

## The Fall of the City

Troops from Efaro marched against Sombatze and sacked it utterly. The wealth and order that the city had represented were swept away in the violence, and a place that had stood as a fixture of the north was reduced to ruin. The blow was felt far beyond Sombatze's own walls.

## The Chaos in the North

With Sombatze broken, northern Soluna lost the anchor that had held it steady. In the absence of that stability the region descended into disorder, and the turmoil endured until around 1000 fO. For a long age the north was a land without certainty, its people left to weather the long aftermath of a single city's destruction.
