# The Krun War

The Krun War was the great reckoning between Krun and Hynsolge, the northern folk whom Krun had once raised up as friends and clients. The barbarians who had been granted the land of Hynsolge turned upon their old patron, and on the field of Grivela they broke Krun's power for good. From the blood of that victory grew a new capital and a new order, one that would hold sway over Ereb for centuries to come.

## Overview

- **Era:** The Age of Armies
- **Date:** Decided in 1065 fO
- **Belligerents:** Hynsolge against its former patron Krun
- **Decisive battle:** The battle of Jorpagna, fought at Grivela
- **Outcome:** Decisive Hynsolger victory; founding of the capital Jorpagna

## A Patron Betrayed

Hynsolge owed its very land to Krun, which had granted it to the northern barbarians in the days of its golden age. Yet patronage breeds resentment as readily as gratitude, and in time the Hynsolgers rose against the realm that had made them. The war that followed pitted client against master, and it would not end until one of them was broken.

## The Battle of Jorpagna

The matter was settled in 1065 fO at Grivela, where the armies met in the battle of Jorpagna. The Hynsolgers won the day decisively, shattering Krun's hold over the northern lands. It was a victory so complete that it did not merely end a war - it ended an age, toppling the old power that had dominated the region.

## The Rise of Jorpagna

Upon the very ground where they had triumphed, the Hynsolgers raised their capital and named it Jorpagna, after the battle that had won them their place in the world. From this seat the realm would grow into the master of the lands around the Copper Sea, and Jorpagna's dominance over Ereb would endure until the 500s fO.
