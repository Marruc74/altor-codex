# Slimpaku

The Slimpaku Expedition was meant to be a punishment. It became a catastrophe. Sent to chastise the land of Slimpaku, the expedition instead loosed upon the world the dreadful creatures called slempukar - and those creatures went on to lay waste to Kamsun and Yndar both. What was launched as an act of imperial vengeance ended as one of the sharpest wounds of the age of ruin.

## Overview

- **Event:** The Slimpaku Expedition
- **Nature:** A punitive expedition sent against Slimpaku
- **What it unleashed:** The slempukar
- **Lands ravaged:** Kamsun and Yndar

## The Expedition

The expedition set out as a punitive venture, a force dispatched to Slimpaku to deliver chastisement. Such expeditions were a familiar instrument of the powers of the age - a show of strength meant to bring a defiant land to heel. This one, however, did not go as its makers intended.

## The Slempukar Unleashed

Whatever the expedition stirred up at Slimpaku, it loosed the creatures known as the slempukar. Once set free these creatures could not be contained, and they turned upon the settled lands with terrible effect. They razed Kamsun, and they razed Yndar - two realms laid waste by a horror that an act of imperial punishment had let slip into the world. The Slimpaku Expedition is thus remembered as a grim warning: that the cure sent against one trouble may breed a far greater one, and that what is unleashed in anger does not always answer to the hand that loosed it.
