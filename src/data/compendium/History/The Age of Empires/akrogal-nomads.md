# Akrogal Nomads

In the last century of the Imperial Age, a new terror came riding out of the wastes of Akrogal. The nomads of that far land had achieved what few peoples ever had: the mastery of the hippogriff as a mount of war. Borne aloft on beating wings, their raiders fell upon settled lands with a speed and reach that no wall or border garrison could answer, harrying both mighty Jorpagna and the island realm of Yndar.

## Overview

- **People:** The nomads of Akrogal
- **Era:** Roughly 750 to 600 fO
- **Homeland:** Akrogal
- **Mounts:** Hippogriffs, ridden in war and raid
- **Targets of their raids:** The Empire of Jorpagna and Yndar

## The Riders of Akrogal

The Akrogalian nomads were a wandering people, and like many wanderers they made their strength from mobility. What set them apart was the creature they had learned to ride. Around 750 fO they mastered the hippogriff - a beast of wing and talon - and turned it to the purposes of raid and war. To break a wild hippogriff to the bridle is no small feat, and the nomads who could do it commanded a weapon that crawled across no road and forded no river, but came down out of the open sky.

## The Raids

For roughly a century and a half, from about 750 to 600 fO, the Akrogalian riders were a scourge upon their neighbours. They struck at the Empire of Jorpagna, descending upon its provinces from above, and they harried distant Yndar across the water. Mounted raiders who could appear without warning and withdraw beyond pursuit were a torment that even the disciplined legions of Jorpagna struggled to counter. The nomads took what they could carry and were gone before any host could be mustered against them, leaving burned fields and emptied granaries behind.
