# Krau ki

Of all the strange fates that befell the doomed island of Yndar, none is stranger than the vanishing of Krau-Ki. The city did not burn, nor was it cast down stone by stone. It simply ceased to be where it had always stood - lifted up entire by the gods and set down again upon the far-off island of Marjura, leaving only emptiness behind.

## Overview

- **Place:** The city of Krau-Ki
- **Land of origin:** The island of Yndar
- **Fate:** Vanished entirely from its place
- **Agency:** Carried off by the gods
- **Destination:** The distant island of Marjura

## The Vanishing

Krau-Ki was a city of Yndar, and like the rest of that island it was caught up in the long age of ruin. But where other places fell to plague, war or failing magic, Krau-Ki met a fate without parallel: it vanished altogether. One moment the city stood in its place; the next it was gone, with nothing left to mark where it had been.

## Borne to Marjura

The city was not destroyed but moved. By the will of the gods, Krau-Ki was lifted whole from Yndar and carried across the sea to the distant island of Marjura, there to stand upon strange soil far from the land that bore it. Why the gods chose to spare and remove this one city, when so much else was left to ruin, is a question without a clear answer. That they did so is remembered as one of the great wonders - and mysteries - of the fall of Yndar.
