# Dryad

Tree-spirits, the wood-nymphs of forest glades. One tree in the glade is the nymph's own, and as it fares, so fares she: cut or burn it and she dies; let it grow old and wither and she turns grey and senile.

## Appearance

A beautiful young woman with long, light, curling hair and blue eyes, who can melt into her own tree whenever she wishes.

## Behaviour

Neither good nor evil by nature, her temper ranging from playful to gentle to cruel from one dryad to the next. She dislikes other creatures trespassing on her glade.

## Abilities

Within her glade she rules absolutely, bending branches, blooming flowers at a whim and snaring trespassers' legs with root and brush. She can be harmed only in physical form and destroyed only by destroying her tree.
