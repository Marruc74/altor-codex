# Najad

Spring-nymphs who make their home in a spring, waterfall, woodland pool or rushing stream, and who can dissolve into the water itself while immersed.

## Appearance

A very beautiful, pale young woman with long black hair and green eyes.

## Behaviour

Perhaps the most malicious of all nymphs, luring men with charm and light flirtation out into deadly currents, helpless to resist.

## Abilities

She commands the currents, waves and flow of her water and the fifty metres around it, and can be slain only by destroying her home, such as by diverting her stream.
