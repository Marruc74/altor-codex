# Faerie

Tiny, mischievous sprites of bright, sunlit woods, living on berries and nuts and almost never without their little magic bows.

## Appearance

Like minute elves with sheer, transparent insect wings, pointed ears, almond eyes and an upturned nose, rarely wearing more than a scrap of cloth.

## Behaviour

An exuberant lot who love to play harmless tricks on any human or beast that wanders into their domain; should anyone come to harm they are stricken with remorse and set it right. Now and then one or two attach themselves to a person as loyal, maddening companions.

## Abilities

They turn invisible at will and read minds, and their arrows can bring deep sleep or wipe away the last hour's memory.
