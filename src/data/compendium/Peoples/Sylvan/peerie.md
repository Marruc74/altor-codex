# Peerie

Tiny fae cobblers, spending summer in bright, flowering meadows and the dark of winter in underground workshops given over wholly to shoemaking. Next to cobbling, their favourite pastime is tormenting humans with pranks until they are nearly driven mad.

## Appearance

About half a metre tall, with pointed ears and nose, rosy cheeks and a merry grin; small and nimble, and very hard to spot.

## Behaviour

They deeply distrust anyone who is not fae and delight in plaguing them, stealing belongings, scattering itching-powder in their camps or spooking their mounts. They hoard great treasures in secret, guard them fiercely, and love wine to a fault.

## Abilities

Most are skilled illusionists, and their true gift is the making of magic footwear, from seven-league boots to boots that bring sleep or change one's shape.
