# Oread

The nymphs of high places: mountains, highlands, caves, ravines and valleys.

## Appearance

A woman with brown or blond hair and brown or blue eyes.

## Behaviour

Gentler and more peaceable than most nymphs, the meadow-and-flower kind especially, and sometimes seen in groups gathering dew across the meadows at dawn or dancing in the morning mists.

## Abilities

Like all nymphs, she can be harmed only in physical form and destroyed only by ruining the home that is her true being.
