# Fire Pixie

A strange little fae folk of the deep forests, often living alongside dryads, najads or wood elves who keep them fed.

## Appearance

A tiny human with pointed ears, drifting inside a small, clear, softly glowing ball of flame whose heat and brightness it controls completely; the fire never goes out while the pixie lives.

## Behaviour

There is nothing they love better than playing harmless practical jokes on strangers in the woods, and if a victim ever comes to real harm they will fetch help from the wood elves.

## Abilities

Immune to all fire and heat and impossible to blind with bright light, but struck twice as hard by water and cold. Their flame works much like a fire spell, which they can stoke hotter at the cost of their own strength.
