# Ice-Gnome

A gnome of the far north, on the tundra and the bare fells where the burdus hold sway, who lives a wandering life in bands under a chieftain, most often the eldest whatever their sex, each band holding to its own stretch of country. They dwell in skin tents and travel by sled drawn by the tinnuit, a tame lemming-like beast that they also ride. At the heart of all they do is kinnuicht, their duty to the band, to the ancestors and to the land in their keeping, taught from the cradle, and nothing in the world can make an ice-gnome break it.
