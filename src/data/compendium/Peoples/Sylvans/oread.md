# Oread


![Oread](/compendium/Peoples/Sylvans/oread.png "Oread")
Oreads are nymphs that live in mountain country, highlands, caves, ravines and valleys. There are two kinds, the limonaids, who are meadow- and flower-nymphs, and the aulonaids, who are valley-nymphs. Like all nymphs an oread is bound to her home, a crag or a cave, and unkillable except by its destruction.

## Appearance

In physical form the oread is a human with brown or blond hair and brown or blue eyes.

## Behaviour

The oreads, and the meadow-nymphs especially, are generally far more peaceable and gentle than other nymphs, and they may even be seen in groups, crossing the flower-meadows at dawn to gather dew, or dancing in the morning mists.

## Abilities

Oreads have no special powers beyond those common to all nymphs: they can heal with a touch and turn a charm on men, can shift at will between physical and spirit form, invisible in the latter, and can be hurt only in physical form.
