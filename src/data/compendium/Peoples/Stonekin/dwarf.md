# Dwarf

The most civilised of the stone-folk, perhaps of all humanoids. Their underground cities and mining towns are far better ordered than most human realms, and they are harder-working, more disciplined and more loyal than men. Dwarves the world over feel a deep kinship, and a lone or outcast dwarf is almost certainly a miserable one. Restless without work, they devote themselves to mining and craft, above all to smithing.

## Appearance

Short and sturdy, around a metre and a quarter tall, with brown or red hair and a beard worn as a badge of status. The common image is a warrior in finely smithed ring-mail with a two-handed axe or war-hammer.

## Behaviour

They live by five ideals: solidarity, principle, industry, endurance and an unshakable calm. Proud and stubborn, shrewd in a bargain and a little greedy, a dwarf never forgets a wrong or a kindness.

## Abilities

They see in the dark as far as a man would by torchlight, even with no light at all.
