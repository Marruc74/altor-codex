# Stone Biter

The stone-folk that best live up to the name: they live in stone, on stone, and are all but made of stone. They dwell only underground as miners, light work for them since they can dig through solid bedrock with bare hands. Stranger still, they eat stone, preferring ore-free minerals, so their mines and their larders often lie miles apart, joined by perfectly cut tunnels. What they do with the metal and gems they unearth no one knows, for they never sell them.

## Appearance

About two and a half metres tall with an enormous torso and almost no neck, so the head sits straight on the shoulders. Their hide is like a suit of thumb-sized slate plates, black but glittering when the light falls right; they wear no armour and rarely any weapon, preferring bare fists.

## Behaviour

Reclusive even by stone-folk standards, they admit no outsiders to their barred, trap-laden cities, and leave only to seek new homes, travelling in small patrols that avoid all contact.

## Abilities

They see perfectly in the dark but only blurrily in sunlight, and hear poorly.
