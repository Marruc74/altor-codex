# Giant

Giants live in remote forests and mountains where other folk leave them in peace, leading simple lives as woodcutters, farmers and herders. They make crude tools themselves and get the rest from the far more skilled gargants. Like many peoples they have spread across the world and adapted to its climates; far in the north live the shaggy, cold-proof ice giants.

## Appearance

Near six metres tall and otherwise much like men, growing stooped with great age. They dress mostly in hides, rarely wear armour, and fight with uprooted tree-trunks as clubs.

## Behaviour

Mostly peaceful and friendly to those who treat them well, though their goodwill turns easily to hatred when smaller folk send adventurers to kill them. The few seen in human lands are usually ones driven from their villages for being too quarrelsome, which is why so many wrongly think the whole race surly and cruel.
