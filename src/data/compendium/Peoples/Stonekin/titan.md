# Titan

A very rare and rather close kin of the giants, set apart by the fact that every one of them is versed in magic. They live alone in great mist-shrouded marble palaces and shun all company but other titans, and far outlive other stone-folk, reaching up to a thousand years.

## Appearance

The largest of all humanoids, up to eight metres tall, always clean-shaven of head and chin and dressed in light, airy robes, donning ornate bronze and linen armour with a long sword and round shield for war.

## Behaviour

Solitary egoists, caring nothing for short-lived humans, the dull-witted other stone-folk or the slight elf-folk, they spend their lives scheming against rival titans to crush or seduce them. Cunning and deeply vain, they share a kind of love-hatred with dragons and may toy with a visitor before killing him.

## Abilities

Each titan wields one or more schools of magic, most often mentalism, to formidable degree.
