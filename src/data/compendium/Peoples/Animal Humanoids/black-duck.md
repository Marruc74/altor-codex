# Black Duck


![Black Duck](/compendium/Peoples/Animal Humanoids/black-duck.png "Black Duck")
The duck-folk come in three kinds: the dreaded and commonest black ducks, who are seafaring pirates; the far more peaceable white ducks, almost all traders; and the rarer, more warlike brown ducks, who often grow up to be mercenaries. They usually live among humans in human cities, though there are pure duck-towns and duck-quarters as well.

## Appearance

Picture a very large duck with arms instead of wings; the plumage is black, white or brown by race. They dress in the local fashion and can use any arms, but favour leather armour and ranged weapons.

## Behaviour

Much like humans in temperament, if famously hot-tempered. The black ducks are violent but reliably honourable, and never the gleeful, bloodthirsty butchers that some dark folk can be.
