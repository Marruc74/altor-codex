# Kojin

The kojin are a sea-dwelling folk of Jih-pun, half shark and half human, who keep whole societies beneath the waves much like those of the islanders above. They breathe through gills and can last only an hour or so on land before they must return to the water.

## Way of Life

In the water they favour the yari, the long spear, which serves them well in the close press of an undersea fight. Their own iron rusts away no matter how they oil it, and so they raid the fishing villages of the coast for the worked metal they cannot keep for themselves, and it is for iron above all that a kojin war-band comes ashore.

## Origin

Legend holds the kojin to be the restless spirits of the drowned, given shape and a place of their own under the sea. Whatever the truth of it, they are a people and not mere beasts, with their own ranks, crafts and quarrels, and a coastal village that loses its iron year after year learns to fear the still water offshore.
