# Wolfman


![Wolfman](/compendium/Peoples/Animal Humanoids/wolfman.jpg "Wolfman")
Wolf-men are often mistaken for werewolves, whom they resemble, but they share nothing in temperament and so carry an undeserved bad name. They live in small, close-knit hunting tribes, and those who go among humans often serve as hunters or scouts in armies.

## Appearance

Powerfully built humans with a wolf's head and grey fur all over, dressed in soft hide clothing and at most leather armour. They can use any weapon.

## Behaviour

Deeply group-minded, working superbly together under a respected leader, so they rarely leave their home clans, and then usually in company. Tolerant and sparing of words, they make little fuss and turn wanderers off their territory gently but firmly.

## Abilities

They take double damage from all fire.
