Ratmen are a rat-headed folk of the dark and crowded places, the sewers and cellars and ruined undercrofts where they breed and scheme out of the sight of taller peoples. They live by scavenging and stealth, quick, cunning and quarrelsome, and gather in great numbers under chieftains of their own.

## The Rat-folk

For all that they are despised as vermin, the ratmen keep their own histories and their own pride, and remember leaders who rose to bind the scattered nests into something greater, among them the names Nibbnubb and Ghassis. Where many of them gather, the rat-folk can grow from a nuisance into a real danger to the streets above.
