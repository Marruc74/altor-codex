# Reptileman


![Reptileman](/compendium/Peoples/Animal Humanoids/reptileman.png "Reptileman")
The undisputed lords of the marshes, the reptile-men live as hunters and fishers in small villages of reed huts, and put fear into intruders with their needle-sharp tridents.

## Appearance

Man-like, with a crocodile's head, heavy clawed hands and a powerful tail, webbed between fingers and toes. They wear little more than a loincloth, seldom any armour beyond a helm and arm-guards, and favour harpoons and tridents.

## Behaviour

Fairly primitive and warlike, above all near their own villages. Out-and-out predators, they kill weaker foes to eat them rather than for sport.

## Abilities

They are wholly immune to natural fire.
