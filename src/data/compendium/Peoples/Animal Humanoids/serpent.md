# Serpent

Serpents love warm, damp places like the underworld and the jungles, distant kin of the reptile-men and living much as they do. Their tribes of twenty to forty hunt almost any creature, even reptile-men when they can, and their craft never rises above stone-age clubs.

## Appearance

A serpent's body with a man-like upper half, a lizard's head and two clawed arms, a long forked tongue and yellow or red eyes with slit pupils, a spiny ridge down the back and small, glittering dark-green scales over a hairless body. They wear nothing and fight with stone weapons or arms taken from the fallen, moving like snakes but helped along by their arms.

## Behaviour

Predators who eat most of what they catch. The only other intelligent creatures they can speak with are the reptile-men, and they are neither friendly nor hospitable.

## Abilities

Great venom-fangs whose bite can drop a victim into instant unconsciousness for hours, immunity to natural fire, and the trick of coiling around a foe to crush him helpless.
