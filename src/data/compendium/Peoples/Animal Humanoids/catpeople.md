# Catpeople


![Catpeople](/compendium/Peoples/Animal Humanoids/catpeople.jpg "Catpeople")
Cat-people are creatures of the wilds, living alone or in small families of half a dozen as skilled and deadly hunters.

## Appearance

Fairly short, about a metre and a half, with warm fur in many shades, most often grey with brown stripes, a cat's head and tail on an otherwise human body. They usually go unclothed, rarely wear armour, and fight with their speed, their claws and sometimes a bow.

## Behaviour

Individualists by nature, so good at hiding and moving in silence that contact happens only on their terms. They think themselves a little above creatures like humans who must huddle in groups to survive, and are curious, playful hunters who love a challenge but never take needless risks.

## Abilities

Retractable claws, excellent night vision outdoors, and an agility that makes them superb climbers.
