# Karkion


![Karkion](/compendium/Peoples/Animal Humanoids/karkion.png "Karkion")
The karkions are not of this world; they fled here when their own was destroyed thousands of years ago, and so have no true home, living as wanderers and hermits here and there. They are very few and keep to the margins, and so have left little mark on humanity.

## Appearance

Like a human with a panther's head and enormous bat-wings spanning four metres, a little over a metre tall, with a golden lion-like pelt over all but the black, hairless wings. They wear only a loincloth and favour light arms they can carry in flight.

## Behaviour

Highly intelligent and gifted in magic, they are thought mysterious and dangerous, yet are in truth gentle and tolerant. Knowing they do not belong to this world, they give and expect respect, and being unable to carry many books aloft they commit all they learn to memory; few creatures hold as much wisdom, for they live very long.

## Abilities

They are of both sexes at once and need no mate to bear young, but a single tear in either wing robs them of flight entirely.
