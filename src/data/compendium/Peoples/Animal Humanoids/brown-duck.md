The duck-folk come in three kinds: the dreaded black ducks, seafaring pirates and the commonest of all; the far more peaceable white ducks, almost all traders; and the rarer, more warlike brown ducks. The brown ducks are the least common of the three and have a more martial bent, often growing up to be mercenaries or warriors. They usually live among humans in human cities, though there are pure duck-towns and duck-quarters as well.

## Appearance

Picture a very large duck with arms instead of wings; the brown duck's plumage is brown, with variations within the race. They dress in the local fashion and can use any arms, but favour leather armour and ranged weapons, and being warlike are handy with a melee weapon besides.

## Behaviour

Much like humans in temperament, if famously hot-tempered. The brown ducks are the most warlike of the duck-folk, and many take up the mercenary's life.
