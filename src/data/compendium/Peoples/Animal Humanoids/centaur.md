Centaurs are chiefly a forest people, living as hunters, craftsmen, traders, warriors and farmers, and preferring bright, open woods and fields where they can move freely; they fare badly in mountains and caves where their hooves slip.

## Appearance

A horse's body with a human upper half rising where the neck would be, in every colour of hair and eye, the men usually bearded. For war they wear fine armour and carry longbows or a broad-bladed spear and a tall, narrow shield.

## Behaviour

A merry, peaceable folk who can turn fierce if threatened or pushed too far. They keep easy company with elf-folk and neither seek out nor shun humans, loving music, dance, sport and games.

## Abilities

Knowledge-hungry and long-lived at around two hundred years, they adore riddles and claim, not always wrongly, to be the wisest creatures alive. By their build they can neither ride nor climb, but are unmatched jumpers.
