Shark-men are aquatic humanoids of the coasts, seldom seen for what they are, since in the water they pass easily for the sharks they resemble. They hire out as assassins, and a body torn apart in a harbour by night is as often their work as any natural beast's.

## Behaviour

A shark-man can take a shape that lets it lurk among the boats and the bathers without drawing a second glance, striking from the water where its prey is slow and clumsy. They are bold only at advantage, and cowardly once the fight turns: hurt one and it flees rather than die for its fee. In Moril Dilaz a trio of them once worked for the conspirators who meant to drown the city, springing their ambush on the water and leaving mangled corpses in the canals that the city blamed on ordinary sharks.
