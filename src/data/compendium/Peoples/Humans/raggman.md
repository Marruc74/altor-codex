# Raggman

Raggmen live in small, heavily armed tribes in great remote forests, hunters and gatherers who seldom leave their clearly marked territory. They have a deep bond with nature and its creatures, and wild animals never attack them.

## Appearance

At a glance easily mistaken for wolf-men: very powerfully built and covered all over in grey or red-brown hair, but with wholly ordinary human heads. They prefer to fight at range, though they always carry their trade tools, a hand-axe and a silver sickle.

## Behaviour

Quite aggressive when their tribe is threatened, but win a raggman's friendship and you find them loyal and selfless almost to a fault. They dislike elf-folk especially and feud endlessly with wolf-man tribes. Sparing of words and trusting deeds over talk, they find it hard to mix with ordinary humans.
