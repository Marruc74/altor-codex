# Pamperna

The Pamperna, the horse-folk of Zorakin, are a small and unusual people, barely a thousand of them, who keep the most sought-after horses anywhere on the Copper Sea. They live apart from the kingdom around them and ask nothing of it but to be left alone, and the crown guards them closely, because the animals they raise are worth a small fortune.

## The Horses

The Pamperna's whole wealth is their herd of about three hundred half-wild horses, so prized that a single good one can fetch upward of twenty thousand silver. They sell only two or three in a year, and never a wilful one; only a horse they judge truly obedient is ever let go. The rest run half-wild under their loose keeping, and they are the foundation of a name that reaches across the whole region.

## Way of Life

The Pamperna live in small villages of perhaps ten houses, each ringed with stables, and they keep a plain, frugal life. They eat only fish and grain, follow no religion any outsider has ever found, and speak a guttural tongue of their own, unlike the speech of the lands around them. Reserved and inward-looking, they have little to do with the kingdom beyond their fields.

## The Royal Grant

The Pamperna hold their land by an old gift. Around 50 fO **King Valien** granted it to them outright, asking only a single horse each year in return, and set royal troops to guard them. The bargain has held ever since: the horse-folk pay their yearly horse, and the crown shields them and their priceless herd from anyone who would seize them.
