The magir are the remnant of an ancient people who lived in the world long before humans spread across it, kin to neither elf, dark folk nor stone-folk. They linger now as hermits in caves and hills, their few surviving communities strictly divided into four classes: warriors, priests, mages and farmers. Once a fiercely expansive people, they are still known for producing superb warriors and mages.

## Appearance

Short and slightly plump, with long pointed ears, faintly yellow skin, brown hair and very large, red-shimmering eyes, their heads rather large for their bodies and their fingers and toes long and slender.

## Behaviour

Good-natured by nature and, like the elf-folk, long accustomed to living in harmony with nature. Honest and honourable themselves, they despise the opposite, and are known to remember both kindnesses and wrongs.

## Abilities

They speak mind to mind among themselves and live six to eight hundred years.
