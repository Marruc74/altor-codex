# Half-orc

Half-orcs seem a grim blend of human and orc, but are in practice humans with orcish traits, far more human than orc; unlike any of the dark folk, they can even learn magic. They are born of unions between humans and orcs, usually violent ones, and so are rare, though they tend to appear several at a time.

## Appearance

Much like ordinary humans but for three orcish marks: thick black hair all over the body and dark skin, yellowed whites of the eyes, and an unusually powerful build.

## Behaviour

Little separates half-orcs from humans by nature, but their upbringing often leaves them embittered, desperate, hardened loners, hateful toward humans and all their works. At best several half-orcs find one another and band together, and such bands grow as close as siblings.

## Abilities

They see in the dark like dwarves.
