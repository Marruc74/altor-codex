# Sharg

The sharg are a nomadic people of the great open plains of northern Ereb. A hardy race, they bear low temperatures without feeling the cold, though they still wrap themselves in furs and leather.

## Appearance

Shargs have rough-hewn faces and heavy, rounded noses. The men grow little hair on the head, mostly at the back, but a thick beard that climbs almost to the eyes; the women have only a thin covering of hair on the scalp.

## Behaviour

Shargs live by hunting, fishing and herding, ranging the plains and frozen wastes in their bands. They are excellent warriors, and the tribes fall on one another without mercy over grazing-borders and the rights to water. In the field they fight with spear and composite bow, and those who ride carry the lance; their armour is mostly studded leather behind a medium shield.

## Abilities

A sharg has very keen eyesight and a measure of night-sight, though nothing to match an elf or a dwarf. They cannot work metal themselves, and so trade for their blades and arrowheads with settled folk; the poorer warriors must make do with tips of knapped stone.
