# Half-elf

Half-elves are a rare crossing of elf and human, uncommon mostly for cultural reasons, since any elf race can bear children with humans. Whoever the elven parent, the child takes far more after the human side.

## Appearance

They pass for ordinary humans, if a little below average height and less heavily built, with blue, grey or green eyes and fair, white, brown or black hair. They inherit neither the pointed ears nor the cat-slit pupils of elves, and the men may even grow beards.

## Behaviour

Lacking the elves' endless years, they feel none of that easy indifference to worldly things; in truth there is little to tell a half-elf from a human.

## Abilities

Sight and hearing about twice as keen as a human's, half the rate of ageing and twice the lifespan, and a strong resistance to disease, but none of the other elven gifts.
