# Dyler

The Dyler are an ancient barbarian folk of the mountains, the aboriginal people of the land that became Magilre. Once they rolled across the whole peninsula; now they are penned back in the heights, nursing a grudge a thousand years old.

## Appearance

Dyler are short, robust and very strong, built for hard country and hard living. The blood carries: even those only part Dyler, a quarter or so, wear the people's stamp plainly in the face.

## Behaviour

They are masters of woodcraft and wilderness skill, but weak in book-learning and lore, and famously volatile of mood, swinging without warning from rage to tears to wild mirth. In war they are fierce. Long ago they were driven back into the Klomeller Mountains, and their old coastal city of Jicon was taken by the incoming Jorers and rebuilt as the canal-city of Moril Dilaz. More than a thousand years on, the Dyler have never forgiven the theft of their city, and the memory of it still moves them to plots and to violence.
