# Lindskiarn

The lindskiarn are a people of unknown origin, thought by most who have studied them to be magical in some way. Aggressive and skilled warriors, they are feared wherever they appear, and rulers who find them in their lands often launch outright crusades to drive them out, so for all their gifts they lead a hard existence. Where they have managed to build settlements, they prove superb artists and craftsmen.

## Appearance

Built perfectly for war: large, well over a head taller than a man, their smooth yellow-white skin tougher than oxhide and wholly hairless. Their feet and lower legs recall an elephant's, giving them a low, steady stance, and a second thumb drawn down from the little finger makes their grip sure. At nearly two hundred kilos they are a match for almost any humanoid, fighting with bronze two-handed swords or captured polearms.

## Behaviour

They worship battle as a god and are intensely aggressive; one may assume a lindskiarn will attack anything it sees, whatever the odds, which is perhaps its only weakness.

## Abilities

They take no harm from ordinary fire, cold, acid, poison or lightning, and only half from other spells, and they speak mind to mind among themselves. Iron, however, harms them badly, so they handle captured iron weapons only with thick gloves.
