A peaceful folk who live in dwellings dug into hills and cliffs, getting their living by farming, craft and trade. Some halfling cultures keep wholly apart from the Big Folk, while others live right among humans in their towns.

## Appearance

About a metre tall and rather plump from their love of good food and drink, usually with curly brown hair on the head and on their large feet.

## Behaviour

They love good company, tales by the fire, plentiful food and drink and a quiet life free of surprises, yet when pushed into hard straits they prove surprisingly tough and brave.

## Abilities

They live eighty to a hundred and ten years, and can sense whether another means them well or ill.
