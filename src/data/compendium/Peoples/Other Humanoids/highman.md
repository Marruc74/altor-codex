# Highman

The origin of the highmen is still debated, though they themselves claim descent from a people raised up in the elder days by the presence of a god they call Tarban. Over the ages their small numbers have dwindled further through mingling with ordinary humans, so they are now very rare.

## Appearance

Larger and stronger than common men, often with great black beards, dark eyes and dark hair. Fond of highlands and cold climates, they favour thick wool and adorn their clothes with gold and silver worked into stylised beasts and gods rather than with jewels.

## Behaviour

Many think themselves a cut above ordinary folk and can be arrogant and hard. Knowing their people are dying out, they are often gripped young by a dread of death that turns them either into reckless, near-suicidal adventurers or into reclusive hermits.

## Abilities

They live a hundred to a hundred and twenty years.
