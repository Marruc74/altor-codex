# Silver Elf

The winged silver elves live a nomadic life on the wing as hunters and gatherers, roaming in bands of thirty to fifty and living on fruit, berries and birds caught in great nets. As they are not strong flyers over long distances, they raise herds of pegasi to ride.

## Appearance

About a metre and a half tall, with pale skin, silver-blond hair, light blue eyes and great feathered wings spanning some five metres, each feather pattern as unique as a fingerprint.

## Behaviour

Hardy and self-reliant, they can seem almost recklessly sure of themselves and tend to be people of few words. Their one great weakness is a near-panic dread of enclosed spaces, for underground they cannot trust their wings to carry them clear of danger.

## Abilities

They can freeze a passing cloud solid as wood for a week, and fly for about twenty minutes at a stretch before they must rest.
