# Cave Elf

Unlike most elves, who love the open light of the forest, the cave elves have made their home underground, enlarging natural cavern systems into cities. Like most subterranean folk they live mainly on cultivated mushrooms and a little mining, but the metal they dig they turn into art rather than tools, and their smithwork rivals dwarven craft for sheer beauty, if not for sturdiness.

## Appearance

Very pale skin, large ears, brown hair and brown eyes. They wear light clothing reinforced with leather and don supple, self-made metal armour only when forced to fight, rarely bothering with ranged weapons underground.

## Behaviour

A wary people who admit outsiders only rarely, for dark folk, dwarves and others are forever seeking new homes underground, forcing the cave elves into a fairly warlike existence.

## Abilities

Living mostly in darkness, they have learned to see by sound the way bats do, moving freely in pitch black and even sensing invisible creatures.
