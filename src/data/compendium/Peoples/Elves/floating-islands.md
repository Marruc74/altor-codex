# Floating Islands

![Floating Island](/compendium/Peoples/Elves/floating-islands.jpg "Floating Island")
![Floating Island](/compendium/Peoples/Elves/floating-islands-2.jpg "Floating Island")
![Floating Island](/compendium/Peoples/Elves/floating-islands-3.jpg "Floating Island")

The grey elves have no fixed homeland. They live on floating islands called mistaristani, grown from sea-lindens whose roots have knit together into a single living raft, and they drift wherever the ocean currents carry them. Between the islands run the goldiner, the swift ships the grey elves are known for.

## On the island

A lookout sits in the tallest sea-linden, watching the horizon for ships and weather. Below it lie the harbours and the shipyards where the goldiner are built and mended, and a freshwater lake the island carries along with it. The dwellings and storerooms gather around a smaller inner island, and on that inner island stands the castle.

## The holy place and the wind-weavers

Each island keeps a holy place, and there the wind-weavers work. They bend the wind and the weather to the island's need, calling up a breeze to fill the sails or turning a storm aside before it strikes. As much as the currents, it is their craft that decides where an island goes, and how fast.
