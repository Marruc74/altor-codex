Sea elves are an elf-kin that, like their cousins the grey elves, live in the sea, but who, like the shark-men, can breathe both air and water. They make their homes in caves five to ten metres down and very rarely leave the sea, and then only to trade. They live on fish, shellfish, seals, dolphins and algae, hunting with tridents and harpoons.

## Appearance

In looks sea elves have very little in common with ordinary elves. They have very small ears, no body-hair, webbing between their very long fingers and toes, and a long neck with gills. On land they try to hide their true nature with long hooded cloaks, high collars and soft skin gloves.

## Behaviour

Sea elves avoid contact with the folk above the water as far as they can, and limit their dealings with the outside world to the grey elves. Their bitterest enemies are the shark-men.

## Abilities

Sea elves can breathe underwater and dive to great depths without trouble, but all their prowess is much diminished on land. Like all elves they are immune to disease and do not visibly age.
