# Sea Elf

The sea elves live in the ocean itself and, like the shark-men, can breathe both air and water. They keep to grottoes five to ten metres down, leaving the sea only rarely and only to trade, living on fish, shellfish, seals, dolphins and algae which they take with tridents and harpoons.

## Appearance

Little like other elves: tiny ears, hairless bodies, webbed fingers and toes, and a long, gilled neck, all hidden ashore beneath hooded cloaks, high collars and soft gloves.

## Behaviour

They shun surface folk, dealing almost only with the grey elves, and count the shark-men their bitterest enemies.

## Abilities

They breathe underwater and can dive to a hundred metres without difficulty, though their skills serve them poorly on dry land.
