# Wood Elf

Wood elves build permanent homes in the crowns of great trees, their mages coaxing the trees themselves into the shapes they need so that little other material is required; the silver-linden serves best of all. They live a secluded life as gatherers and hunters, and their cities are almost impossible to find unless you know exactly where to look.

## Appearance

Blond hair with a faint green tinge, and natural greens and browns for clothing that make them very hard to spot. They care little for jewellery, favouring simple, functional dress, and almost always carry a bow and a short sword.

## Behaviour

Intensely shy of strangers, their best defence is not to be seen, so they hide rather than fight. A threatened wood elf flees away from his home so as not to lead pursuers to it, and lost wanderers are nudged back to the path by signs in nature without the elves ever showing themselves. Dark folk within their territory are killed swiftly from ambush.

## Abilities

In forest they are harder still to find, their already superb concealment growing better among the trees.
