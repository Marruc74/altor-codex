# Wood Elf

Wood elves build permanent homes in the crowns of great trees. Their magicians have the power to make a tree grow into the shape they wish, so that little other building material is needed, the silver-linden being far the best wood for the purpose. Wood elves live a very secluded life as gatherers and hunters, and their dress and woodcraft let them avoid all contact with other humanoids if they so wish. Their towns are very hard to find unless one knows exactly where they are.

## Appearance

A wood elf has blond hair with a faint green tinge. They wear nature-coloured clothes of green and brown, which makes them very hard to spot, and care little for jewellery or fine ornament on everyday dress, preferring a simple, functional outfit of breeches, a short jacket and perhaps a hooded cloak. They almost always carry a bow and a short sword.

## Behaviour

Very shy of strangers. A wood elf's best protection is to go unseen, so it hides sooner than fights. Discovered and feeling threatened, it always flees away from its home, so that pursuers are not led there; lost wanderers in the forest are set right by signs in nature, without the wood elf ever showing itself, while dark folk within its bounds are killed as swiftly as possible by ambush with the bow. A wood elf's nightmare is to be drawn into close combat, and should that happen it trusts to its great skill at moving swiftly and unseen through the forest where it was born and has likely lived for hundreds or thousands of years, knowing its woods as well as the back of its own hand.

## Abilities

Wood elves are far harder to spot when they are in forest. Like all elves they have keen sight and hearing, see well by night, are immune to disease and do not visibly age.
