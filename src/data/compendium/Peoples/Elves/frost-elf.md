# Frost Elf

A very rare people of the arctic north, descended from wood elves driven long ago from the northern forests. They live in small nomadic bands by herding reindeer, hunting and fishing, and have adapted remarkably well to the brutal cold.

## Appearance

White hair and green eyes, dressed almost entirely in seal, reindeer and bear hide. They hunt with longbows and carry spears and short swords, wearing at most light leather, since metal armour is madness in such cold.

## Behaviour

A peaceful folk who would rather flee than fight. Being so isolated, they treasure visitors and make warm, attentive listeners.

## Abilities

They endure temperatures down to fifty below without trouble, but wilt in the heat, growing slower and clumsier the warmer it becomes.
