# Blood Elf

A rare and dreaded elven people of the deep forests of central Ereb, such as the woods of Nargur, the blood elves live entirely in the crowns of giant oaks. Legend says their god cast them out in the elder days for terrible crimes against their own kin, leaving them more tainted by the world's evil than other elves; they breed so slowly that young ones are almost never seen. They petrify their oaks with a magical brew, carve stairs and rooms into the trunks, and move between the branches by rope and vine faster than squirrels.

## Appearance

Sallow, almost yellow skin, wholly red eyes, black pointed teeth and claw-like nails, with long black hair worn in a braid. They keep the pointed ears and cat-slit eyes of the wood elves they resemble, and move even more silently.

## Behaviour

The very opposite of gentle elf-folk: bloodthirsty hunters who live almost wholly on raw meat and sometimes kill for sport, said to run down deer with their bare hands. Strangers are rarely welcome, and they loathe other elves above all, a hatred fully returned.

## Abilities

A small number can speak mind to mind and read the thoughts of others.
