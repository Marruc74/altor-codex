# Water Elf

Water elves are a rare elf-folk wholly given over to life beneath the waves, dwelling in undersea cities among the selkies, the sea-fae, the mermaids and the dolphins. Unlike their cousins the sea elves, who breathe both air and water, the water elf can breathe only water, and drowns in the open air as surely as a landsman drowns in the deep.

## Appearance

A water elf has shimmering greenish skin, hair of green and blue, webbed hands and feet, and eyes of green or blue, and over each eye a thin clear membrane that shields it against such things as a cloud of gas in the water. Nearly all of them are green-eyed; the rare blue-eyed few trace their line to the hero Angridye Onbane, who carried human blood and slew two sea-dragons, and a blue-eyed visitor is met more warmly for it.

## Life in the Deep

They are trained one and all as hunters and warriors, and fight with the trident, the dagger and the short spear; of the magical arts they learn only the animist's and the mentalist's. Their bitterest foes are the shark-men, who hunt them through the deep with trained white sharks, and the long enmity between the two peoples colours much of life in the undersea cities.
