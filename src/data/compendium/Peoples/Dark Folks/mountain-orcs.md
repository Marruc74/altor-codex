The mountain orcs, the girnackh, are the orcs of the high peaks. They are heavier and stronger of body than the common kraukh-orc, hardened by life in the cold and the heights.

## Behaviour

Mountain orcs share the high country with the dwarves, and the two are old and bitter enemies. Much of a mountain-orc tribe's life turns on that feud: raiding dwarf-holds, ambushing them in the passes, and breaking into their halls. The clan called the Dwarf-stranglers is drawn from this race.

## Abilities

They are powerful climbers, at home on cliffs and broken ground where heavier foes founder, and they carry heavy armoured shields against the dwarves' axes. Like all orcs they see well in the dark and can never learn magic.
