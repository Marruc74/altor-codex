# Olog-hai

The olog-hai are a troll-kind unlike all the others, for they can bear the light of day. Where common trolls turn to stone in the sun and must sleep the daylight hours away, the olog-hai walk abroad by day untroubled, though they like it little and keep to shade where they can. They do not much resemble orcs, yet some hold they were bred from orc stock among other things.

## Appearance

An olog-hai is a great, brutish creature with a faintly greenish hide, very hard over most of the body save the face and the palms of the hands.

## Nature

They are coarse and savage, given to eating their food while it still lives, and bear heat and cold alike without complaint. For all the toughness of their bodies they have one telling weakness: they are exceedingly easy to sway with magic, far more so than other trolls, so that a spell which would scarcely touch a mountain-troll may turn an olog-hai with ease. In a fight they favour the greatsword.
