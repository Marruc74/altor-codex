The shikome are the mountain-orcs of Jih-pun, a folk of the high stone who hate all human-kind, for it was the islanders who long ago drove them up out of the good country and into the peaks. They live in cave-complexes deep in the Fujimuri range, and they eat the very rock.

## Raiders

The shikome come down to carry off women and children, holding them for ransom paid in gemstones, and they are eaters of human flesh, counting fingers and toes the choicest part. They favour the katana and the yari, and fight much as the orcs of Ereb do. The fire of a tatsu cannot harm them, which makes them bolder than most in the dragon-haunted heights.

## In the Mountains

Theirs is a hard and hidden life among the crags and the hewn-out galleries, and they share the high places with worse things than themselves, for the great mountain-spiders, the kumo, hunt the shikome as readily as they hunt lost travellers and goats.
