Something between a great orc and a giant, even dimmer than orcs but sharing their values. They live alone or in small bands, often among orc clans or svartalf groups where they serve as prized warriors but seldom as leaders, and very rarely join with trolls.

## Appearance

About three metres tall and as heavily built as orcs and trolls, with coal-black skin, long stringy black hair, red eyes, a broad nose and great tusks. They dress like orcs and swing two-handed clubs, or wield two-handed weapons in one hand.

## Behaviour

The dullest ogres throw in with other dark folk, where they are childish and poor at taking initiative, while the cleverer ones live alone as hunters that attack most things they can eat.

## Abilities

Limited night vision and, unlike orcs, no daylight weakness, yet still they shun bright sun. Ogres can never learn magic.
