The steppe orcs, the brackhs, are the orcs of the open plains, nomads who took to a wandering life without fixed homes some six thousand years before Odo and never gave it up. They move with the seasons and live out of their camps.

## Behaviour

Rather than caves or captured holds, the steppe orcs live under cloth: dome-like tents they call kisch, pitched in the open with the chieftain's tent at the centre. They keep wolves in open pens beside the camp, and favour red war-paint, the colour they give to madness.

## Abilities

Always on the move, they fight light, carrying small round shields that leave them free to ride and run. Like all orcs they see well in the dark and can never learn magic.
