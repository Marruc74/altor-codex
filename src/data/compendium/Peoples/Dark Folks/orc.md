# Orc

The commonest of the dark folk and the best at building working societies, though only under a leader big, strong and clever enough to hold them, for orcs respect nothing but raw strength. Such leaders are rare, but necromancers, high elves, half-orcs and other black-hearted spellcasters sometimes seize an orc tribe and bend it to their own ends.

## Appearance

Shorter than men but far burlier, with coal-black skin, twisted yellow tusks, evilly red eyes and blunt curved claws on hands and feet. They wear whatever sacking, looted coats and scavenged armour they can find.

## Behaviour

Selfish and short-sighted, sneering bullies to the weak and bootlicking flatterers to the strong; beat their leader and the rest may well throw down their weapons and follow you. On home ground, though, they fight to the last.

## Abilities

Perfect night vision and a hatred of daylight, in which they see only blurrily. Orcs can never learn magic.
