# Orc

The commonest of the dark folk and the best at building working societies, though only under a leader big, strong and clever enough to hold them, for orcs respect nothing but raw strength. Such leaders are rare, but necromancers, high elves, half-orcs and other black-hearted spellcasters sometimes seize an orc tribe and bend it to their own ends.

## Appearance

Shorter than men but far burlier, with coal-black skin, twisted yellow tusks, evilly red eyes and blunt curved claws on hands and feet. They wear whatever sacking, looted coats and scavenged armour they can find.

## Behaviour

Selfish and short-sighted, sneering bullies to the weak and bootlicking flatterers to the strong; beat their leader and the rest may well throw down their weapons and follow you. On home ground, though, they fight to the last.

## Society

Orcs build the most organised societies of any dark folk, but always around strength alone. A warrior's rank is won by killing: by how many foes he has put down and how dangerous they were, a dwarf worth more than a man and a knight more than a common soldier, and it is worn openly in trophies and scars, the more and uglier the better. The strong rule, and the old or crippled who can no longer fight lose their place. Orcs make almost nothing themselves, taking their food, tools and weapons by theft and war, so they are forever raiding and forever dependent on the peoples they prey on. They band together loosely, gather into tribes under their own banners, and at their greatest into clans many thousands strong. A warrior who works deeds of real savagery may be raised to a ravager, the closest thing orcs have to a hero.

## Religion

The orcs' priests are the bone-men, called Urganob, who dress in scraps of bone and paint themselves white, the colour of death. Every orc fears them, for they are believed to hold power over the gods, and they alone keep and pass on what little the dark folk believe.

## Abilities

Perfect night vision and a hatred of daylight, in which they see only blurrily. Orcs can never learn magic.
