# Cave Troll


![Cave Troll](/compendium/Peoples/Dark Folks/cave-troll.png "Cave Troll")
The commonest kind of troll and probably the closest to the original stock, sheltering underground or in caves by day from the sun's petrifying rays and venturing out by night to find food.

## Appearance

About two and a half metres tall and very heavily built, with dark, almost black, thick warty hide, long filthy stringy hair, claw-like hands, heavy tusks and yellow eyes that glow in the dark.

## Behaviour

Forever hungry and greedy, they attack almost anything that moves unless it is plainly too dangerous, though they will often try to steal a party's horses first.

## Abilities

Like all trolls they reek horribly, radiate fear, regenerate their wounds and dread fire. Direct sunlight turns them instantly to stone, so they shun the open in any weather, and by day all their skills are halved. Trolls can never learn magic.
