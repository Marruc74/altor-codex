The raukk are what remains of the first race, the stock from which all the dark folk are descended. Proud, wise and solitary, they wander the world alone, forever searching for their god. They breed very rarely, so they have always been few, and grow fewer with the ages.

## Origin

The dark folk tell that the god Raukkchim, lord of weapon-craft, felt slighted by the great gods and made a creation of his own in secret: the raukk, beautiful to look on but full of strife and a corrosive hate. The raukk sowed war among the peoples of the world, and the great gods commanded Raukkchim to destroy what he had made. He refused, and walked away into eternity as the Eternal Wanderer. Seven lesser gods had hidden many of the raukk away, and they reshaped them into their own images, and so made the orcs, the svartalfs, the trolls, the resar and all the rest. The dark folk dread Raukkchim's return, for they are only the warped offcuts of his work, and his coming would mean their end.

## Abilities

The raukk can never learn magic, but they have near-perfect sight in the dark and keenly sharp senses. Like all the dark folk they are at home in the night.
