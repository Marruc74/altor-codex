The ylk-orcs are the orcs made by the black god Ylkaur, and they are the largest, most muscular and most aggressive of all the orc races. Where the common kraukh-orc is merely brutal, the ylk-orc is cruel by nature, murderous and full of hate.

## Behaviour

The ylk-orcs are bound up with the worship of Ylkaur more tightly than any other orcs. Among them the bone-men form a warlike priesthood, and their war-banners are the most grotesque of any dark folk, daubed with blood and dung and hung with the heads of the slain.

## The Cult of Ylkaur

Ylkaur is held to be the one and only god, whose will is law and whose priests speak for him among the living; all who do not worship him must die, and to die with his name between the teeth is the highest honour, the road to his realm. The cult is led by bone-men, and above them stand the great bone-men, the most powerful of all. No single great bone-man has ever managed to unite every ylk-orc under one banner, and to do so remains the cult's great ambition.

## Abilities

Ylk-orcs are stronger and more enduring than other orcs. Like all orcs they see well in the dark and can never learn magic.
