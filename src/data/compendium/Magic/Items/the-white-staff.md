# The White Staff

The White Staff, called the White Tamer, is the chief treasure of Landori's Demon-Slayer Legion, the band out of Ereb that hunts down everything demonic or in league with demons and kills it without mercy. Some two meters long and slightly warped, it is cut from demon-bone but painted to look like a plain whitewashed walking staff - though any mage feels the power in it at once.

## Powers

The staff has a will of its own, a strength of mind it spends on its workings and on overmastering the minds of demons, and it recovers slowly on its own. At a touch it can imprison a demon outright, and while the demon is caught within, the bearer may call on its own powers. It warns when a demon enters Altor and points the way, and in the hand of one who hunts them it makes the bearer surer in commanding a demon and in sending one away. As a weapon it strikes demons doubly hard, and no demon's invulnerability turns it aside.

## Origin Unknown

Colonel Penn, who leads the Legion's demonologers, found the staff in the ruins of the Devil's Palace on the mountain called the Shadow-Watcher, and it galls him that he cannot learn where it was made. He has been known to send adventurers back to that palace to find out - and what waits for them there is no small thing.
