# Notable Magic Items

Not every enchantment is bent to war or ruin. The mages of Ereb have, over the ages, bound their art into a great many useful things (carpets that fly, bottles that never run dry, boots that walk on water), and the best of them carry the names of the masters who first made them. What follows is a traveller's sampling of the wares a wealthy buyer might find, at ruinous price, in the workshops and back-rooms of the academies.

## Travel and Flight

- **Ibn-Azud's fabulous carpet** - a flying carpet that bears its rider through the air at the speed of a galloping wind; the cheaper sort drains the will of whoever rides it.
- **Athariel's airborne litter** - a flying sedan-chair, swift over open country and steady at a marching pace.
- **The magic carriage** - a fine coach that draws itself along without horses.
- **The magic ship** - a vessel, from a small boat to a full cog, that sails crewless before an unseen wind.
- **Simon's water-boots** - boots that let a man walk upon water, though not in a hard wind.
- **Tenser's underwater helm** - a helm by which the wearer breathes and swims beneath the water as easily as on land.
- **Athariel's falling-belt** - a belt that spares its wearer all harm from a fall, however great.

## Comfort and Plenty

- **Wulf's ever-full flask** - a flask that fills itself anew with water, or, more dearly bought, with ale or wine.
- **Seron's copper kettle** - a kettle forever full of warm, nourishing broth.
- **Seron's tempting breakfast-basket** - a basket that always holds a ready meal, the costliest sort changing its fare each time.
- **Wulf's savoury pipe** - a pipe whose tobacco never burns out and is always warm.
- **Tenser's tidy broom** - a broom that sweeps a room clean of its own accord.
- **The lantern of eternal light** - a lamp that throws a strong beam a hundred paces into the dark.
- **Morgaine's magic quill** - a pen that sets down whatever is spoken aloud, its ink never failing.

## Protection from the Elements

- **Gyax's ring** - a ring that wards its wearer against cold down to fifty below, though not against magical frost.
- **Fu Chu's cooling cloak** - a cloak that holds off the heat and keeps its wearer at a pleasant warmth.
- **Gabriel's chain** - a chain that turns aside rain, cold and wind alike.
- **Gomez's protecting clasp** - a clasp that eases sickness, and in its finest form cures it outright.

## Stealth, Senses and Speech

- **Ochara's ring of invisibility** - a ring that makes its wearer unseen, so that all eyes pass over him.
- **Elf-boots** and the **elf-cloak** - boots that leave no trace in the wild, and a cloak that melts into the green so the wearer goes unseen as he moves.
- **Gyax's surpassing translator** - by its virtue the bearer understands and speaks every tongue.
- **Ricardo's gloves** - gloves that grip any surface, so the wearer climbs sheer walls like a spider.

## Vermin and Pests

- **N'komo's magnificent fly-trap** - a charm that kills every insect within a hundred paces.
- **The rat-catcher's flute** - a flute that draws any chosen vermin to it from far off.

> Many of these come in two makings: a true enchantment that works of itself, and a cheaper, half-bound copy that spends a little of the bearer's own spirit each time it is used. Only the wealthiest carry the former; the rest make do, and tire.
