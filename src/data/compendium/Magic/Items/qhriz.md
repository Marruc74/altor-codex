# Qhriz

Qhriz is a flying broom, two metres of ironwood heart whose bristles look like ordinary straw but are in truth the feathers of a predator-crane. It was made by the witch Tara, and "Qhriz" is the name of the spirit she bound inside it; it is that spirit, and not any sealing magic, that lends the broom its power.

## Flight

The broom carries a mighty flying enchantment, woken when the rider spits in his hands and speaks the words *"Away and fly"* in the old tongue. It can fly at better than a hundred kilometres an hour and is steered by thought alone, but it is a treacherous mount: it holds the flight itself, so a rider who slips off, which is easily done, simply falls, and every sharp turn threatens to throw him. Tara found the binding for it in Heshík'har's book, an ancient work of the spirit-binder Heshík'har from the time of the second conflux; the book was lost in the very ritual that made the broom.

## Tara's Hold

The ritual drew enormous power out of Tara herself, and some of it was stored in the broom, so that when she rides it she can draw on that store rather than her own strength, the power returning to the broom with each rising of the moon. She need not even hold the broom to command it, and anyone who climbs aboard must overmaster her will to keep control of it at all.
