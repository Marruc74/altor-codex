# The Queen of the Sea

The Queen of the Sea is the swift-sailing ship of Tamros Stormsailer, the most successful pirate in all the Western Sea. It was a gift from three magicians of Mereld, Ambri, Uluvar and Teogar, whom Tamros had saved from a colony of raugoner; their own ship went down in the rescue, and they repaid the debt with a vessel the like of which few sailors ever see.

## The Ship

She is built of a light wood reinforced with pine and oak, forty metres from stem to stern yet only seven across the beam, a narrow, knife-keen hull that would be perilous in any ordinary craft but rides the sea beautifully through the magic worked into her. She carries two masts and a fine figurehead in the shape of an elf in armour, and for teeth she mounts a pair of ballistae with shot enough for thirty rounds.

## The Magic

The enchantments laid on the Queen of the Sea give her balance and command of the wind, bound fast and lasting. They are worked from a bronze lever in the captain's cabin, by which the helmsman calls up the wind and sets its strength and quarter as he pleases, so that the ship can outrun almost anything afloat.
