# The Staff of Mokylider

The Staff of Mokylider is the great relic of the forest-god's faith on the Montures, a rod cut from the stone-hard knotgran wood and topped with a carved stag's head set with eyes of silver. It was made long ago by Sogam Hammarskaft, one of the first priests of Mokylider and a mighty animist, and it has passed down the dwindling line of his successors ever since.

## Powers

In the hand of its bearer the staff masters the wild. It leaves him trackless in the woods, so that no trail betrays his passing, and grants him the power to call the beasts to him, to speak with them, and to bend them to his will. Its magic is woven to draw on no strength of its own, so the staff serves freely and without cost, a fitting tool for a priest sworn never to leave the forest. It will serve as a cudgel at need, but its worth is in the forest, not the fight.
