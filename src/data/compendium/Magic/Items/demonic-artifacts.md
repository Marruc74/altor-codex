# Demonic Artifacts

Where demons are bound and bargained with, demonic things find their way into the world: weapons forged in the smithies of Demonicum, gifts pressed upon mortal servants by the Guardians, and the cold instruments the demon-hunters turn against them all. Each is a thing of power and of peril, and none is held without a price.

## Gifts of Nehcrom

The Horned One rewards his servants with the tools of war.

- **Själabane** - "Soulbane," the demon-sword granted to a favoured servant of Nehcrom.
- **Trum** - a weapon of lightning.
- **Deloch** - Nehcrom's war-steed, a mechanical horse driven by demonettes sealed inside its shell; armoured enough to fight on even with its head struck off.

## Gifts of Caliban

The lord of madness gives stranger things.

- **Gholgath** - the staff of Caliban.
- **The cup and the horn of madness**, and **Caliban's mantle** - pressed upon those who please him, and upon those he means to ruin.

## The Masks of Bemoth

The Prince of Beasts grants three masks, each giving lordship over a kind of living thing.

- **Grymkäft's mask** - by it the wearer speaks with and leads all flying and hovering beasts.
- **The Moon's mask**.
- **Snarl's face** - mightiest of the three, by which the wearer commands all loathsome, spineless things, the insects and the worms.

## Cursed Weapons

- **Belgor's dagger** - a blade graven with a plague-rune upon a haft of human bone. A wound from it can rob a victim of half his very spirit and loose a swift, spreading plague; the stricken swells and dies in moments.
- **Blodtörst** - "Blood-thirst," a dwarf-axe whose edge was hardened in demon blood and graven with war-runes and a sign of lightning. It drives its wielder to berserk fury, and can loose a stroke of lightning with a blow.

## Instruments of the Hunters

Against all these stand the tools of the demon-hunters, who hunt their quarry across the worlds.

- **Isghul's astral map** - one of five charts of the Grey Halls, useless in the waking world, but a sure guide through the grey country between the worlds.
- **The lintropic pendel** and the **lintropic disc** - a hunter's divining tools: the one to locate a hidden being across miles, the other to see astral bodies, fading traces, and unseen portals.
