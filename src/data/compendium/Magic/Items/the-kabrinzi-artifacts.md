# The Kabrinzi Artifacts

The true power of the old Kabrinzi order lay not in its preaching but in three relics, holy things that could sway a whole crowd with a single spoken word. They were kept under heavy guard in a vaulted cellar and used only with great caution, for fear of what else they might do.

## The Three Relics

The relics are three: an amulet, worn at the neck; a short staff of silver; and a glass flask in which a vapour swirls and never settles. Together they gave the church the power to turn a gathering of strangers to its will at a word, and that power made them worth killing for.

## Scattered

When the rulers of Magilre came to favour the Shining Way and bold nobles broke into the vault, they stole all three relics and murdered the order's leaders. The relics were then scattered and hidden away, for none who held them knew how they were meant to be used. Two have since been recovered by the Seekers who keep the faith alive; the third, the amulet called the Holy God-Wine, was long worn by a statue in a crypt deep in the swamp of Efronzink, where an insectoid queen took it for a jewel of her own.
