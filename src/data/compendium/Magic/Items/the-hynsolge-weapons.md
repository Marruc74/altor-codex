# The Hynsolge Weapons

Three blades of great power are bound up with the history of Hynsolge: one carried by its king, one by a creature of demonic blood, and one lost from a hero's tomb.

## Chilphéric

Chilphéric is the broadsword borne always by King Mefimor, so old that the family's earliest records of it burned in an archive fire. It is of Krunish make, taken as spoils when the Hynsolgers beat Krun at Grivela better than a thousand years before the present age; hidden away during the Akrogalian nomad raids, it was then forgotten for a millennium, until a Hamellian fisherman turned it up in the reign of King Tanbul and his son handed it over as a tax payment, so passing it into the royal treasury. The court magicians who have studied it can say only that it lends its bearer powers akin to certain mind-magics, with a trace of the elemental about it, and that the king wields it more surely than any common broadsword. Its full strength is still hidden.

## Kred-Bar-Onaabys

Kred-Bar-Onaabys is as hideous as its master, Parikila Omurtag, who is of demonic descent; the demon Onaabys forged the blade himself in the south of the salamanders. Its edge burns a dazzling pale green and flashes in anger when it is swung. The sword drinks the souls of those it kills and turns them into the energy that Parikila and Onaabys both need to remain in this world, and so Parikila must slay an intelligent being every week with it or fade from Altor altogether. In a fight its light flares so fiercely that all caught in it are unmanned, and against the unholy it bites harder still; it can loose lightning, darkness and other workings from the stolen power it hoards.

## Merkumiror

Merkumiror was carried by Fingal Orc-hewer at the last battle of Korumil, where the orcs that fell to its edge were more than any man could count. The legends tell nothing of its powers save that it makes its bearer all but unbeatable in war. Where it lies now is a mystery: some fifty years ago it was stolen from Fingal's crypt beneath the temple in Orkovia, and it has not been seen since.
