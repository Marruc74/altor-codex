# Harmonism

Harmonism is the magic of music, worked not with words and gestures but with song and the playing of instruments, calling on what its mages name the harmonies of the spheres. Its colour is silver, and a harmonist is reckoned as much a performer as a sorcerer, for without a true musician's skill the art will not answer at all.

## Overview

- **Worked through:** song or instrument, never spoken spells
- **Draws upon:** the harmonies of the spheres
- **Colour:** silver
- **Practised by:** humans, elves, and metamorphs such as the swan-maidens
- **Limits:** the listener must hear it, and the harmonist is never moved by his own music

## The Music of the Spheres

A harmonist holds only one harmony at a time, and it lasts for just as long as he keeps singing or playing. His targets must be able to hear him, so that the deaf and the undead cannot be touched at all, while creatures of keen hearing are swayed the more easily. With a loud enough performance he can lay his charm on a whole crowd at once, a mass-peace to still a brawl or a mass-dance to set a hall reeling, and need only be heard to do it.
