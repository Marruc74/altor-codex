# Voice Magic

Voice magic is the loud art of the dwarves, who achieve by sheer cried sound what other mages do with whispered spells. Some name it disharmonism, the rough mirror of the harmonist's sweet song, for where one soothes with melody the other compels with din.

## Overview

- **Worked through:** great shouts and cries of power
- **Aspect:** order
- **Practised by:** the dwarves above all
- **Also called:** disharmonism, the harsh counter to harmonism

## The Cried Word

The voice-mage's power rides upon noise: a scream that strikes like a blade, a wall of sound that throws men down, a shout that commands obedience, a wave of force flung from the throat. In the deep halls of the dwarves such bellowing carries far through the stone, and a master of the art can make himself heard, and felt, where no quiet spell would reach.
