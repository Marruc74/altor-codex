# Spiritism

Spiritism is the art of the spirit world, of the souls of the living and the dead and of the formless spirits that were never alive at all. It belongs to the aspect of chaos, kin to demonology, and is regarded as an unsettling craft, for it reaches toward what lies beyond the ordered world.

## Overview

- **Domain:** the world of spirits, living, dead and never-living
- **Aspect:** chaos, kin to demonology
- **Practised by:** humans, dwarves, onaquians, vättar and others
- **Standing:** an uneasy art, close to the chaos beyond the world

## Dealings with the Unseen

The spiritist treats with what has no flesh, calling spirits up, binding them, and turning them against the living. His workings strike at the soul rather than the body, withering the spirit or assailing the mind, and the spirits he commands can carry his will where no living messenger could go. It is a power little trusted, for those who deal too long with the chaos of the spirit world are seldom unchanged by it.
