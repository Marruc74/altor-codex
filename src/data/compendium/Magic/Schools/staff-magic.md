# Staff Magic

Staff magic is the art of binding one's power into a staff, an art mighty but perilous, for the mage's strength comes to rest in a thing of wood that can be broken. Those who take it up are set apart even among sorcerers, for they wield more than most and risk more than any.

## Overview

- **Power bound in:** a wooden staff, between two-thirds and the full height of its mage
- **Entry:** beyond the usual academy test, a further trial of the psyche
- **Strength:** a staff that grows ever mightier with its bearer
- **Weakness:** break the staff and you cripple the mage

## The Bound Staff

A staff-mage receives his staff from his teacher and pours half his own psyche into it, and from then on it is the staff that grows in power rather than the man. He needs no waving of arms to work his magic, only a spoken word, often a single one. But the gift is also the danger: split or destroyed, the staff deals its bearer a grievous shock, and he wins back his lost strength only slowly, a little with each passing year. A foe who takes the staff has taken the magic, and for this reason the staff-mage guards his above his own life.
