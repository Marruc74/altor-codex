## The Marks That Bite

Siola is a quiet wool-town with the empire's arsenal at its back, until the killings start - and the dead are found by the work of beasts in the heart of the harbour quarter. The trail leads to a ship newly come to anchor and to the man who plies his needle aboard it. Xelimogga is no common inker but a moon-priest of the Ptaklun circle, who hold the werewolf a holy thing, and the ink he drives under the skin is mixed with the saliva of a werewolf chained in the hold. Every mark he gives is a seed of the curse, and he carries it from port to port. His paymaster cares nothing for the faith: the silver-merchant Kawsa da Krafans owns the ship and would be glad to see wolves walk the towns, for a frightened realm buys silver against them, and the silver is his to sell. The heroes may come at the thread from any end - a careless friend newly tattooed, a priest visited by a troubled "wolf," or a fresh-marked corpse - and the deeper they follow it, the closer the chained beast in the hold.
