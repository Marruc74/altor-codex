## The Light Against the Dark

A month after a crown was won at a terrible price, the heroes are summoned to the elf-king's living castle, where Ereb's heroes have gathered around a single hope: a way to destroy the archmage at last. The dark Punisher loosed at the Hell Fort now fights in his service, and only its bright twin can answer it. But the gathering is betrayed - a black raptor strikes down the prince who brought the call, and when the great teleportation is worked, it is sabotaged, and only the heroes reach the road ahead.

## The Twin Staff

That road runs first through Yndar, a dead land of blood-red skies and necromancers, to a mountain that is a petrified god, in whose stone hand the Twin Staff lies sealed. Won past its serpent-guardian, the staff drags the heroes toward the buried city of mages - by way of a desert and a hermit who is not what he seems - for only at Densel's heart, beneath four magical labyrinths, is there power enough to summon the Good Punisher. There the two titans meet, and the bright one casts down the dark.

## The Reckoning

Sent home with the staff turned to a shining sword, the heroes learn the last evil to undo: the cursed hand by which the archmage rules the very queen they crowned. The hand struck away and the queen freed, they ride the pegasi over the Great Wall to the archmage's tower, where he shows his true and ancient face. With the moon darkened and his magic quenched, the reckoning is theirs to win - and as the Nidlandish armies break and the enslaved elves go free, the war ends. Whether evil is truly gone, or only waiting to wear a new face, the tale does not say.
