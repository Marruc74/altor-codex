## A Face for Hire

In Tricilve the Fate Lottery is no game but a yearly terror: the Pillar-Father of Kastyke reaches into the urn, draws a noble's name, and that man is made an outlaw, to teach the city that the gods favour whom they please. Sansigald Silkenhand, a cloth-merchant grown fat on the trade, is certain his name will come up this year, for an old enemy has lately become the emperor's darling and the draw is not always as blind as it seems. So he hires a double - a hero with the right build and nerve - to wear his clothes and his face, and to slip the city should the worst be called. The silver is good and paid in advance. But a stand-in who is recognised will meet the mob meant for his master, and if the name truly falls on Sansigald, the merchant will already have taken ship, his promises and his second payment sailing with him.
