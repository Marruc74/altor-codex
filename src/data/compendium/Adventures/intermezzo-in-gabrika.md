## The Sick Baron

The barony of Gabrika is a poor and out-of-the-way corner of Magilre, and its baron, Castorin, is sick, or believes he is, and keeps to his bed while the realm's coin drains away into the hands of healers. The ablest of them, Sartibug the Body-Twister, has used his place at the bedside to take the running of the barony for himself, shouldering aside the baron's old adviser Algotis Ferizan and breeding a quiet discontent that has since hardened into a resistance.

## Two Commissions

The party comes into it from more than one side. The sidelined Algotis seeks them out; and at the inn called Alzia and the Ziplodit, in Estra, they are taken down to the cellar headquarters of the resistance, where its leader, Asterion the Swan, sets them to murder Baron Castorin and offers them maps of the keep and all his people's help, with a cell waiting for anyone who hesitates too long.

Soon the party holds two opposing commissions, each patron wanting the other dead. They may take a side, play both against each other, or trick the lot and walk away rich; the threads run every way, even to the party killing all comers and ending up rulers of Gabrika themselves, which is as fine a seed for a civil war as any.
