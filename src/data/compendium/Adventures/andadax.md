Andadax is a cooperative forest-village of some sixty timber cabins, set east of the Forskarl Falls and north of Lake Sango. Its people live by hunting and woodcraft, hold hard work and fellowship as the highest of virtues, and share what they gather through a common purse, keeping a free guest-cabin for travellers and a cooperative trading-post.

## A Quiet Defiance

For all its peaceableness, Andadax stands against the rulers of Montures. The village quietly shelters the rebels of the Vergili Forest and sends food to their camps, and it keeps the worship of Mokylider, the true forest-god, rather than the state Church of Sbintor. Each year it holds the Hunter's Games, a contest of running, climbing, swimming, riding, archery and the cast of axe and bola, crowned by a trial of the hunt.

## Those Who Dwell There

The village is led in spirit by its elder, Eskel Bearclaw, an old man of great learning and gentleness. Its pride is the hunter Demio Furottis, master of the Games, who is never without his hound and his falcon. And among its gatherers is young Hanna Furottis, who found a book of magic and taught herself the animist's art in secret, a gift that would send her to the stake if the church ever learned of it, and that might one day carry her into the forest to join the rebels.
