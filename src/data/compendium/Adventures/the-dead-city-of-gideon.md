Midway down the Gideon Chasm, some two hundred paces above the sea, hang the ruins of the underground city the imariots raised in the age of Queen Karaleia. It fell into the depths when the island broke in the Third Conflux, and it has been a place of the dead ever since.

## The Vigil

The ruin is not empty. A remnant of imariot undead still keeps it, maintaining its dark watch and seizing living captives, whom they carry down into the city to lay before their goddess. Beneath the city lies a half-circle lagoon, five hundred paces across and thick with corpses and the wreckage of lost ships, and there the giant squid makes its lair.

## The Prison

In the southern reaches of the city stands Imaria's temple and prison, a hall of black marble shrouded in a cloud of darkness, where five chains of red-glimmering light hold the demigoddess fast within a pentagram. Shadow-beasts and the walking dead guard her, and those who descend this far come either as her captives or as fools, for there is no known power that can destroy what waits at the bottom of the chasm.
