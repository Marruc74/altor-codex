Zygosund's Bridge is a dwarf-built bridge and border fortress east of Zorakin, on the road that runs between Fristaden and the dwarven realm in the eastern foothills of the Aidne mountains. It carries the road across a small river that flows down toward the Dakkilo Bay.

## The Bridge

Built of stone by dwarven hands, it stands on three arches some three metres above the water, an inscription in one of the vaults dating the first bridge to the year 79. At either end rises a ten-metre stone tower with its own garrison, and the crossing can be sealed at both ends with gates and dropping iron portcullises, while grilles can be lowered to block the river itself. A garrison of some fifty dwarven warriors holds it.

## History

When Fristaden grew into a trade centre between the dwarves and the Caddo in 511, the dwarves set about securing the road; the bridge was rebuilt between 520 and 522, manned half by dwarves and half by Fristaden, an arrangement Zorakin watched sourly. In the late summer of 540 a dark-folk army laid siege to it for a month, only to be swept away when relief came up from Fristaden, and there have been no great assaults since. After Fristaden fell to Duke Wilfred of Salmora in 591, trade dwindled, and the bridge is now held by dwarves alone.
