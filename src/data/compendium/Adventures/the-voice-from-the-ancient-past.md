## Plot

Northwest of the town of Faltrax, in the contested borderlands between orcs and elves, stands the castle of El Nhassiv. A century ago it belonged to the Zeen family until war broke out and the orcs stormed it, and several of the Zeen were taken and died in its cells. Today the ruin shelters a robber band of orcs and dark elves under the orc Orta, and the party's road runs through the surrounding wilderness as the old story resurfaces.
