## The Cliff That Drinks Magic

Off the coast of Saphyna stands a vast pillar of crystal the folk call Shamash's Finger, and the monk Kalyphard has come to learn its secret. His master, Cambalum the Wise, walked into the warped country of Krindenland and never came out, but his papers survive, and in them a perilous idea: that the magic spilled from every spell cast anywhere in the world is gathered, a little at a time, into the crystal cliff - drawn across the Saphyna strait through Krindenland, whose strangeness is the price of that draining. Cambalum did not merely vanish; he was murdered, by a high priest of Shamash who would see the cliff broken. To shatter it might calm the seething of Krindenland - or it might loose, all at once, the hoarded magic of an age, and unmake far more than the priest has reckoned. The heroes are caught between a faith that wants the truth buried and a sea-cliff whose breaking no one truly understands.
