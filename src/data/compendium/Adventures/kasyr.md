Kasyr is an island city-state to the south-east of Targero, an independent and wealthy place known above all for the slave market that runs there without pause. The island itself is barren and farms poorly, so its people live chiefly on fish, but its harbours are rich on a far uglier trade.

## The Slave Market

To Kasyr come pirates, warlords and slavers of every stripe, and the buyers settle their business in an easy, luxurious calm. The city takes a twentieth of every sale as a turnover tax, and that levy is its single greatest income, paying among other things for the mercenaries who hold its high walls. Slaving is officially forbidden on Caddo and rare on Erebos, which is exactly why it has flourished here.

## Rule

The city is governed by rich merchants who made their fortunes on smuggling and on servicing the trade, and they are wholly without scruple. Behind their walls and their hired swords, they keep the market open and the gold flowing.

## History

Kasyr was founded in the early 600s before our era by the emperor Ergyr of Jorpagna, as a way-station and watering-port for his ships. In 276 the city's elders resolved to make more of it as a port of transshipment; the scheme worked for only one ware, slaves, and from that the great market grew into what it is now.
